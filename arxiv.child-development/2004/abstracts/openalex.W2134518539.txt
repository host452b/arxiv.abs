Title: Grandparent Identity, Intergenerational Family Identity, and Well-Being
Authors: Donald C. Reitzes, Elizabeth J. Mutran
ID: openalex.W2134518539
URL: https://doi.org/10.1093/geronb/59.4.s213

The findings confirm the expectation that grandparent identity meanings may
encourage well-being. Further, the intergenerational identity reflects the
overlapping meanings and experiences of being a parent and a grandparent.
