Title: Young muslim men in Scotland: inclusions and exclusions
Authors: Peter Hopkins
ID: openalex.W2074977304
URL: https://doi.org/10.1080/14733280410001720548

Young Muslim men, by virtue of their age, religion and complex masculinities,
are marginalised in human geography.This article builds on research with young
Muslim men aged 16-25 who live in Scotland to show how signifiers of Muslim
identity have gained prominence following the events of September 11th 2001.The
discussion considers what this means for the multiple ways that young men
negotiate their national identities as Scottish Muslims.
