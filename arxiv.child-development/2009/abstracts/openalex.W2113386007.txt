Title: Indices of Multiple Deprivation predict breastfeeding duration in England and Wales
Authors: Amy Brown, Peter Raynor, David Benton, M. D. Lee
ID: openalex.W2113386007
URL: https://doi.org/10.1093/eurpub/ckp114

Deprivation, as assessed by the IMD is predictive of breastfeeding duration.
Postcode and thus deprivation level can be used as a non-intrusive way to
identify women most at risk of low breastfeeding rates. Service provision can be
targeted directly at women in areas recognized at being high in deprivation.
