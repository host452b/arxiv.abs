Title: An Ethnographic Investigation of the Influence of the Home Environment on the Developing Reading Skills of First Grade Children
Authors: Bertha W. Kingore
ID: openalex.W2273628304
URL: https://digital.library.unt.edu/ark:/67531/metadc332065/

This study described (a) the factors and activities in the home environment
which appear to affect children's reading competence and affective dimensions of
reading, (b) the parents' language style when interacting with their children,
(c) the rate of reading acquisition for children from differing home
environments, and (d) observable behaviors as children learn to read.
