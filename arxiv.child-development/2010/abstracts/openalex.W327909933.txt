Title: Everything I Learned About Teaching in the Contact Zone I Learned From Charles Xavier and the Uncanny X-Men
Authors: James Bucky Carter
ID: openalex.W327909933
URL: https://digitalcommons.unl.edu/sane/vol1/iss1/3

The article asserts that reading and studying comics via the lens of the contact
zone help illuminate comics' literary and literacy value and helps illuminate
notions of comics art and notions of schooling.
