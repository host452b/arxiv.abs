Title: Is this the past? The place of role-play exercises in undergraduate history teaching
Authors: Cedric Beidatsch, Susan Broomhall
ID: openalex.W2993862475
URL: https://doi.org/10.53761/1.7.1.6

Increasingly, academic teachers are exploring the learning opportunities offered
by student-centred participatory classroom and web-supported exercises. Role-
play and gaming activities have been highlighted as fora which provide
development of a range of personal and social skill sets in students, as well as
understanding of subject content. This paper reports on research exploring how
such exercises in an undergraduate history unit can be used to deliver core
historical content and generic skills, and to develop students’ knowledge of the
professional historian’s craft and historiographical practice.
