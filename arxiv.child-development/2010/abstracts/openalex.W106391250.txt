Title: Sociodemographic and Health Correlates of Sleep Quality and Duration among Very Old Chinese
Authors: Danan Gu, Jessica M. Sautter, Robin Pipkin, Yi Zeng
ID: openalex.W106391250
URL: https://doi.org/10.1093/sleep/33.5.601

Age and health conditions are the two most important factors associated with
self-reported sleep quality and duration. Good quality of sleep among long-lived
old adults may have some implications for achieving healthy longevity.
