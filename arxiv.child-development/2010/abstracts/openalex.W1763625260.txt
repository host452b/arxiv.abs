Title: Feminist Organizations and Intersectionality: Contesting Hegemonic Feminism
Authors: Benita Bunjun
ID: openalex.W1763625260
URL: https://doi.org/10.7202/1120167ar

This paper is a theoretical examination of the foundational ideologies of
hegemonic feminism which continue to be contested by feminists engaging with
intersectional frameworks and analyses both within the women's movement(s) and
feminist organizations in North America. The paper additionally discusses the
structures and processes of feminist collectives while drawing on the author's
current research on Vancouver Status of Women.
