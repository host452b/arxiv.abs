Title: Factors Associated With Duration of Breastfeeding in Ireland
Authors: Roslyn C. Tarrant, Katherine M. Younger, Margaret Sheridan‐Pereira, John Kearney
ID: openalex.W2111747349
URL: https://doi.org/10.1177/0890334411413097

There is a need to comprehensively examine why mothers in Ireland discontinue
breastfeeding early and to explore the factors influencing duration of
breastfeeding during the first 6 months postpartum. Findings from this study
provide valuable direction for future strategies and interventions aimed at
increasing breastfeeding duration rates in Ireland.
