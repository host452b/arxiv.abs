Title: The climate of inclusive classrooms: the pupil perspective
Authors: Susan Tetler, Kirsten Baltzer
ID: openalex.W2027717368
URL: https://doi.org/10.1080/14748460.2011.616326

This paper offers insights into learning experiences in inclusive classrooms,
gained by giving voices to pupils about their perceptions of themselves and
their opinions on classroom climate. A positive response pattern is identified
concerning academic and social dimensions of schools, while the overall picture
concerning the dimension of participation is more blurred. The paper argues for
further explorations of the concept of educational participation in inclusive
settings, not only with a focus upon the psychological classroom climate
aspects, but also upon the pedagogical and didactic processes that constitute
the learning conditions for pupils with disabilities included in mainstream
classrooms.
