Title: Gender Inequitable Masculinity and Sexual Entitlement in Rape Perpetration South Africa: Findings of a Cross-Sectional Study
Authors: Rachel Jewkes, Yandisa Sikweyiya, Robert Morrell, Kristin Dunkle
ID: openalex.W2016478660
URL: https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0029590

Perpetration of rape is so prevalent that population-based measures of
prevention are essential to complement criminal justice system responses. Our
findings show the importance of measures to build gender equity and change
dominant ideas of masculinity and gender relations as part of rape prevention.
Reducing men's exposure to trauma in childhood is also critically important.
