Title: The Association between Short Sleep Duration and Weight Gain Is Dependent on Disinhibited Eating Behavior in Adults
Authors: Jean‐Philippe Chaput, Jean‐Pierre Després, Claude Bouchard, Angelo Tremblay
ID: openalex.W2072033593
URL: https://doi.org/10.5665/sleep.1264

We observed that having a high disinhibition eating behavior trait significantly
increased the risk of overeating and gaining weight in adults characterized by
short sleep duration. This observation is novel and might explain the
interindividual differences in weight gain associated with short sleep duration.
