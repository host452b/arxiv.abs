Title: Drama as a pedagogical tool for practicing death notification-experiences from Swedish medical students
Authors: Anna Nordström, Anncristine Fjellman‐Wiklund, Tomas Grysell
ID: openalex.W2166212227
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/1472-6920-11-74

The exercise, Marathon Death, based on forum play with video playback is a
useful pedagogical tool that enables students to practice delivering death
notification. The ability to practice under realistic conditions contributes to
reinforce students in preparation for their future professional role.
