Title: Measuring problem gambling in adolescent populations: Phase one - report
Authors: Jamie Wiebe, Harold Wynne, Randy Stinchfield, Joël Tremblay
ID: openalex.W2279810333
URL: http://hdl.handle.net/1880/48156

The main objectives of the present research are to reconceptualize the
adolescent problem gambling construct, establish an operational definition that
will guide development of a new survey instrument for measuring the construct in
adolescent populations, and develop an initial version of the new measurement
instrument.
