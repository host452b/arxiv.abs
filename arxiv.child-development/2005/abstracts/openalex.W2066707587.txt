Title: The Adolescent Lesbian Identity Formation Model: Implications for Counseling
Authors: Suzanne Degges‐White, Jane E. Myers
ID: openalex.W2066707587
URL: https://doi.org/10.1002/j.2164-490x.2005.tb00030.x

An interview protocol based on V. Cass's (1979) sexual identity formation work
was used with 13 self-identified lesbians. Data analysis yielded a sexual
identity formation model similar to J. E. Marcia's (1966, 1987) identity
development model. Potential emotional and physical health risks for each phase
of the model are discussed, and interventions are suggested.
