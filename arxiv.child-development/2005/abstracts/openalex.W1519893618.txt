Title: The Myths We Live By
Authors: Mary Midgley
ID: openalex.W1519893618
URL: https://doi.org/10.4324/9780203480922

Mary Midgley argues in her powerful new book that far from being the opposite of
science, myth is a central part of it. In brilliant prose, she claims that myths
are neither lies nor mere stories but a network of powerful symbols that suggest
particular ways of interpreting the world.
