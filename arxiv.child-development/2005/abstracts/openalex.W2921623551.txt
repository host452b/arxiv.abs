Title: Creative, imaginative English-as-a-foreign-language using storytelling and drama
Authors: Man-Lin Rau
ID: openalex.W2921623551
URL: https://scholarworks.lib.csusb.edu/etd-project/2693

With a view to improve English teaching, this project provides creative teaching
methods for English teachers of elementary schools in Taiwan. Storytelling,
creative writing, and creative drama are interesting and lively activities that
are used to motivate students to learn English.
