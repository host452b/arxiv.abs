Title: The Autobiography as Language Reflection
Authors: Kathy Everts Danielson
ID: openalex.W204135497
URL: https://scholarworks.wmich.edu/reading_horizons/vol29/iss4/5

Often, preservice teachers need to be reminded of the many influences on
children's language. By reflecting on their own past language experiences,
perhaps prospective teachers can better examine the important elements for
language development.
