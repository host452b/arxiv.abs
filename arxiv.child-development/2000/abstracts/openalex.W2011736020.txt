Title: Breastfeeding training for health professionals and resultant changes in breastfeeding duration
Authors: José Augusto de Aguiar Carrazedo Taddei, Márcia Faria Westphal, Sônia Y. Venâncio, Cláudia Maria Bógus, Sônia Buongermino de Souza
ID: openalex.W2011736020
URL: https://doi.org/10.1590/s1516-31802000000600007

This randomized trial supports a growing body of evidence that training hospital
health professionals in breastfeeding promotion and protection results in an
increase in breastfeeding duration.
