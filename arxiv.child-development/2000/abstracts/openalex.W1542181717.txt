Title: Retribution and Redemption in the Operation of Executive Clemency
Authors: Elizabeth Rapaport
ID: openalex.W1542181717
URL: https://digitalrepository.unm.edu/law_facultyscholarship/46

In this Article, my goal is to raise doubts about the adequacy of the neo-
retributive theory of clemency and stimulate reappraisal and development of what
I will call the "redemptive" perspective. To this end I will present an
exposition and critique of neo-retributive theory of clemency.
