Title: Demented patients and the quandaries of identity: setting the problem, advancing a proposal
Authors: Giovanni Boniolo
ID: openalex.W3131934835
URL: https://doi.org/10.1007/s40656-021-00365-y

In the paper, after clarifying terms such as 'identity', 'self' and
'personhood', I propose an empirical account of identity based on the notion of
"whole phenotype". This move allows one to claim the persistence of the
individuals before and after their being affected by dementia. Furthermore, I
show how this account permits us to address significant questions related to
demented individuals' loss of the capacity of moral decisions.
