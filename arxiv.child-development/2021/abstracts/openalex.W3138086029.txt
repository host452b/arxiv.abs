Title: Exploring competencies of military nurses in general hospitals in China: a qualitative content analysis
Authors: Huijuan Ma, Lin Li, Suofei Zhang, Lei Lei, Jinyu Huang
ID: openalex.W3138086029
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/s12912-021-00673-5

Existing knowledge of competencies of military nurses in general hospitals is
limited. A detailed exploration of this topic can provide guidance for
recruitment, competency assessment, and competency building.
