Title: Trends in electronic cigarette use and conventional smoking: quantifying a possible ‘diversion’ effect among US adolescents
Authors: Arielle Selya, Floe Foxon
ID: openalex.W3119796698
URL: https://doi.org/10.1111/add.15385

A simulation model shows that a substantial diversion effect is needed to
explain observed nicotine use trends among US adolescents, and it must be larger
than any possible opposing catalyst effect, if present.
