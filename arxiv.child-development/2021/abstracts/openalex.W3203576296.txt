Title: MARIA MONTESSORI: AN INTELLECTUAL BIOGRAPHY
Authors: Anasuya Adhikari, Birbal Saha
ID: openalex.W3203576296
URL: https://doi.org/10.36713/epra8535

Maria Tecla Artemisia Montessori (August 31, 1870 – May 6, 1952), best known for
her contribution to education and for the Philosophy of education that bears her
name, was also a well-acclaimed science nurturer, psychiatrist, feminist and
played an indispensable role in politics too. Montessori’s career has been a mix
and match of different role where she engaged herself besides imparting
education. The efficacy of this paper would be to peek into Maria Montessori’s
career and inspect her role as a feminist, scientist, psychiatrist and study her
social contributions. This paper is also an attempt and endeavour to seek into
the relevance of Montessori today. KEYWORDS: Montessori as A Scientist,
Montessori as A Psychiatrist, Montessori as A Feminist, Montessori’s Relevance
Today
