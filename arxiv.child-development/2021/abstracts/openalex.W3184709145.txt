Title: The Gender-Sensitive Social Risk Factors for Internet Addiction in College Undergraduate Students
Authors: Lin Xia, Jing-yan Gu, Wanjun Guo, Yajing Meng, Huiyao Wang
ID: openalex.W3184709145
URL: https://doi.org/10.30773/pi.2020.0277

IA was a common phenomenon in college students with shared and unique
precipitating and social risk factors in males and females. The gender-sensitive
risk factors for IA warranted earlier and individualized intervention and
prevention strategies for IA in this population.
