Title: Are We Thinking Too Straight? Why We Need More LGBTQ+ Studies in India: A Perspective
Authors: Anjali Mediboina
ID: openalex.W3196960357
URL: https://doi.org/10.52629/jamsa.v9i1.234

Despite India’s antiquity recognizing all types of queer sexualities, there is
still much stigma and taboo that surrounds the Indian LGBTQ+ community. This
stigmatization can be reflected in the scarcity of literature that focuses on
the community. This article discusses the lack of LGBTQ+ studies in India, the
consequences, and how encouraging more studies for this community can help
eradicate prejudice and discrimination.
