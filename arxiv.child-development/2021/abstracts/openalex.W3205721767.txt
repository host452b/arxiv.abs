Title: “What I thought was so important isn’t really that important”: international perspectives on making meaning during the first wave of the COVID-19 pandemic
Authors: Irina Todorova, Liesemarie Albers, Nicole Aronson, Adriana Băban, Yael Benyamini
ID: openalex.W3205721767
URL: https://doi.org/10.1080/21642850.2021.1981909

The paper provides a detailed overview of the methodology, the main themes
identified inductively in the stories and differences according to select
demographic variables. We identify several major ways of making meaning of the
pandemic. The pandemic has impacted many aspects of people's lives which give it
meaning, no matter where they live.
