Title: Pengaruh Program Seniman Masuk Sekolah terhadap Motivasi Siswa
Authors: Hary Murcahyanto, Muhammad Fahrurrozi, Mohzana Mohzana
ID: openalex.W4312757803
URL: https://doi.org/10.31539/joeai.v4i1.2148

ABSTRACT&#x0D; This study aims to analyze student motivation in music lessons.
Using a multi-case study, ten state senior high schools in East Lombok Regency
were selected as the research sample. This research is a qualitative study using
an in-depth interview approach in which data collection, observation and
analysis of related documents is applied. Data presented in a narrative. The
results of the study revealed that the artists in school are significantly
functional and play a role in increasing and encouraging the level of student
motivation in music lessons. This research concludes that students' motivation
in music is strongly influenced by the presence of practitioners or artists who
are in direct contact with students, especially in the learning process at
school.&#x0D; Keywords: Artists go to school; Motivation; Music Arts Education
