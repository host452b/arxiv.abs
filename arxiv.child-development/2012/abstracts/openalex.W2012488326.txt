Title: Teaching Literature In and Outside of the Classroom
Authors: Katrin Kalamees-Ruubel, Urve Läänemets
ID: openalex.W2012488326
URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.sbspro.2012.06.558

Designing literature learning when selecting the content and organizing the
learning process can be enriched in constantly changing modern learning
environments by using drama, theatre, museum visits, publication of students
original literary work, recital competitions and other elements of the “hidden
curriculum”. These in unison with the manifested National Curriculum can offer
insights into new vistas for aspiring towards educational excellence and re-
create students‟ interest and motivation for reading and comprehension of
literature as a means of personality development and identity building.
