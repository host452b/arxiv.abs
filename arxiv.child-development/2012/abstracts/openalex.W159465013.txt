Title: The Conflict of the Faculties. Perspectives on Artistic Research and Academia
Authors: Henk Borgdorff
ID: openalex.W159465013
URL: https://doi.org/10.26530/oapen_595042

Artistic research is an endeavour in which the artistic and the academic are
connected. In this emerging field of research artistic practices contribute as
research to what we know and understand, and academia opens its mind to forms of
knowledge and understanding that are entwined with artistic practices. Henk
Borgdorff also addresses how we comment on such issues, and how the things we
say cause the practices involved to manifest themselves in specific ways, while
also setting them into motion. In this sense, this work not only explores the
phenomenon of artistic research in relation to academia, but it also engages
with that relationship.
