Title: Simulating school closure policies for cost effective pandemic decision making
Authors: Özgür M. Araz, Paul Damien, A. David Paltiel, Sean Burke, Bryce van de Geijn
ID: openalex.W2125130311
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/1471-2458-12-449

These findings highlight the importance of obtaining early estimates of pandemic
severity and provide guidance to public health decision-makers for effectively
tailoring school closures strategies in response to a newly emergent influenza
pandemic.
