Title: English Is Not &lt;i&gt;All&lt;/i&gt; That Matters in the Education of Secondary Multilingual Learners and Their Teachers
Authors: Kara Mitchell
ID: openalex.W1830042230
URL: https://doi.org/10.18251/ijme.v14i1.495

Utilizing the critical race theory (CRT) construct of majoritarian stories and
the already identified story of English-is-all-that-matters in the education of
multilingual learners and their teachers, this study illustrates the influence
of this powerful narrative in classroom practice. By promoting English-only
instruction, maintaining a limited perspective of what it means to know English,
and treating multilingual learners as if they were monolingual, this study
demonstrates that multilingual learners&amp;rsquo; educational opportunities are
being limited through a persistent and unhelpful overemphasis on
English.&lt;span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Arial;"&gt;&lt;/span&gt;
