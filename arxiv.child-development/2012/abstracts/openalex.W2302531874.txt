Title: Lessons For Teachers Integrating Visual And Performing Arts Into Kindergarten English Language Arts Common Core Standards
Authors: Lauren Ecker
ID: openalex.W2302531874
URL: http://hdl.handle.net/10211.3/10211.8_257

The purpose of this project was to create kindergarten curriculum that
integrates art-based language arts instruction that is focused on the Common
Core and Visual and Performing Arts Standards. The curriculum includes twelve
15- 20-minute lessons. Each lesson addresses one of the four main areas of
visual and performing arts; music, visual art, dance, or theater. Each lesson
contains an objective, standards, assessment, instructional steps and the
teaching resources for implementation. The literature reviewed for this
curriculum project included three main areas: how visual and performing arts can
increase learning, strategies for integrating the arts, and strategies to
support teacher to use arts in curriculum and instruction. The literature was
reviewed with the intention to identify best practices for integrating arts into
kindergarten language arts curriculum. Each strategy identified was
strategically used in one or more of the lessons. And the best practice list was
used as a tool to evaluate the curriculum before completion. The curriculum
provides educators a tool to integrate the arts as well as a model of how
educators can integrate the arts for their students. It is crucial for educators
to integrate visual and performing arts for students??? academic success and
holistic development. Although this curriculum is complete, it is a supplement,
and adaption is key to meet each student???s needs.
