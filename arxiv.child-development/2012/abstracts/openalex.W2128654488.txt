Title: High initiation and long duration of breastfeeding despite absence of early skin-to-skin contact in Karen refugees on the Thai-Myanmar border: a mixed methods study
Authors: Adrienne White, Verena I. Carrara, Moo Kho Paw, Malika, ColleyPaw Dahbu
ID: openalex.W2128654488
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/1746-4358-7-19

In a population with a strong culture of breastfeeding and robust breastfeeding
practices, high rates of initiation and duration of breastfeeding were found
despite a lack of early skin-to-skin contact. Local preferences, traditions and
practices that protect, support and maintain high rates of breastfeeding should
be promoted.
