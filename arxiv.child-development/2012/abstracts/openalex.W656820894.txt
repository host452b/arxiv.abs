Title: Literacy studies:Ethnographie de l'écrit
Authors: Karin Tusting
ID: openalex.W656820894
URL: 

This chapter first outlines the Anglo-Saxon approach of the 'New Literacy
Studies', putting it in relation to the French 'Anthropology of Writing'
tradition. Research in paperwork in educational workplaces is then described to
show how a focus on literacy practices from an ethnographic perspective can give
insight into broader social processes.
