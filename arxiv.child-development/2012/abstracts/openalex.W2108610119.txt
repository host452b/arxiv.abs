Title: Focusing on first year assessment: Surface or deep approaches to learning?
Authors: Sharn Donnison, Sorrel Penn‐Edwards
ID: openalex.W2108610119
URL: https://doi.org/10.5204/intjfyhe.v3i2.127

This paper investigates the assessment and learning approaches that some first
year students employ to assist them in their transition into their first year of
study and extends our previous work on first year student engagement and timely
academic support It is situated within the First Year transition and student
engagement literature and specifically speaks to concepts of learning within
that body of literature. In this paper we argue that while students are in the
transitional period of their studies, the use of assessment as a motivator for
learning (surface approach) is valid first year pedagogy and forms an initial
learning stage in the student's progress towards being lifelong learners.
