Title: Basic education teachers’ concept of effective teaching: Inputs to teacher education curriculum in the Philippines
Authors: Edna Luz R. Abulon
ID: openalex.W1977464285
URL: https://doi.org/10.5861/ijrse.2014.723

Conceptual understanding about effective teaching was explored among basic
education teachers from public schools situated in the City of Manila. Teacher-
respondents from kindergarten, elementary and high school levels (N=355) were
asked to respond to a questionnaire with open-ended items from which qualitative
research data were gathered.
