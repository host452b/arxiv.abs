Title: Identity and entrepreneurship
Authors: Simon Down, Andreas Giazitzoglu
ID: openalex.W3122771674
URL: https://doi.org/10.4324/9780203096512-19

Interest in identity as a form of understanding individual behaviour in its
social context has been extensive in the social sciences and within business and
management, particularly organization studies (Alvesson et al. 2008), for around
30 years. In the field of entrepreneurship interest in identity is more recent
but increasingly prevalent across a range of traditions and approaches, which at
their broadest can be split into sociology and social psychology.
