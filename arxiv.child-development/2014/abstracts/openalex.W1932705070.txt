Title: Interrelationships between anthropometric variables and overweight in childhood and adolescence
Authors: Bente Brannsether, Geir Egil Eide, Mathieu Roelants, Robert Bjerknes, Pétur Benedikt Júlíusson
ID: openalex.W1932705070
URL: https://doi.org/10.1002/ajhb.22554

The interrelationships between BMI SDS and five standardized overweight related
anthropometric variables were dependent on age, being weakest in the youngest
age group. Independent of sex and age, WC SDS was in this study superior to
other anthropometric variables in contributing to variation in BMI SDS during
childhood.
