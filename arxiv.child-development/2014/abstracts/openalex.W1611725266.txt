Title: The education system in Indonesia at a time of significant changes
Authors: Tatang Suratno
ID: openalex.W1611725266
URL: https://doi.org/10.4000/ries.3814

Indonesia’s national education system developed from the struggle for
independence and the renaissance of the nation. During its development, many
influences, local or global, contributed to the changing nature of the
curriculum, pedagogy, assessment, and teacher professionalism. The author argues
that education reform should entail a dialog involving the government,
educators, and society to attain the goal of constructing an independent society
of democracy and social justice.
