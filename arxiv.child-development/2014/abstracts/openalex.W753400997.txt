Title: Autoethnography as a Transformative Research Method
Authors: Dwayne Custer
ID: openalex.W753400997
URL: https://doi.org/10.46743/2160-3715/2014.1011

Autoethnography is a qualitative, transformative research method because it
changes time, requires vulnerability, fosters empathy, embodies creativity and
innovation, eliminates boundaries, honors subjectivity, and provides therapeutic
benefits. This article discusses these seven lenses using select passages from
“Jesus Christ and Reese's Peanut Butter Cups: A Narrative on Homosexual
Identity, Spirituality, and Human Development” (an unpublished manuscript)
written in 2013.
