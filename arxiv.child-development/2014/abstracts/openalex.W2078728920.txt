Title: Exclusive breastfeeding: measurement and indicators
Authors: Ted Greiner
ID: openalex.W2078728920
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/1746-4358-9-18

Exactly which indicator is best to use depends on the purpose for which the data
are being used. However, for surveys, the best approach, rarely used, would be
to report indicators based on both point-in-time and life-long data.
