Title: To see or not to see: a qualitative interview study of patients’ views on their own diagnostic images
Authors: Leslie Carlin, Helen Smith, Flis Henwood
ID: openalex.W2105591716
URL: https://doi.org/10.1136/bmjopen-2014-004999

Viewing images of one's interior, invisible body is powerful and resonant in a
number of ways. The experience of not seeing, whether through the patient's or
the physician's choice, is also fraught with meaning.
