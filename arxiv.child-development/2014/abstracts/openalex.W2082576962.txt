Title: Ambiente letrado y estrategias didácticas en la educación preescolar chilena
Authors: Pelusa Orellana, Carolina Melo
ID: openalex.W2082576962
URL: https://doi.org/10.11144/javeriana.m6-13.aled

This study analyzes the quality of literate environment and didactic strategies
used by preschool teachers in 147 establishments of different socioeconomic
environments in Chile. For data collection, the ELLCO (Early Language and
Literacy Ob- servation Tool) was used, which includes self-reports, checklists,
surveys and rating scales. The results show that the quality of literate
environment is low and that there are significant differences between
establishments of different socioeconomic level.
