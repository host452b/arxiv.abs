Title: Mundos virtuales 3D para la comunicación e interacción en el momento educativo online
Authors: Miguel Baños González, Teresa C. Rodríguez García, Mario Rajas Fernández
ID: openalex.W2066254921
URL: https://doi.org/10.5209/rev_hics.2014.v19.44967

3D Virtual Worlds offer the possibility of a redesign of communication
situations of traditional online education, being its potentiality to restore
characteristics of classical classroom education. Here we present the design of
a teaching methodology that includes the phases of action for the development of
educational activities in 3D platforms as complementary resource for online
education. As a result, it is confirmed the positive impact of its use in
learning skills, social dimension of learning and student motivation.
