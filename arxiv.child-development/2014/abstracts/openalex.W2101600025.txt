Title: Predictive factors of delay in contact with a psychiatrist in depressive disorders: a sample of Iranian patients.
Authors: Seyedeh Sahar Hosseini, Mehri Rejali, Gholam Reza Kheirabadi, Roqayeh Aliyari
ID: openalex.W2101600025
URL: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/24829722

More delay of help seeking by children and adolescence and probability of
maladaptive coping style such as substance use and complicating of situation
need to earlier diagnosis of depression in young group and earlier treatment for
decrease DALLY for MDD, Therefore we suggest the mental health awareness
programs for adolescent in school, and more ever for the influenced roles of
parent and other family members and teachers on teen and young person's life,
the education for families and teachers on adolescent mental health problems can
be effective. First help seeking contact with non-psychiatric medical
professionals enhance the delay of contact with a psychiatrist. Appropriate
training of non-psychiatric medical professionals and developing a referral
system would lead to better provision of mental health care.
