Title: A transdisciplinary approach to the decision-making process in extreme prematurity
Authors: Marc Simard, Anne-Marie Gagné, Raymond Lambert, Yves Tremblay
ID: openalex.W2152498867
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/1756-0500-7-450

We propose, with our transdisciplinary analysis, a relevant and innovative
complement to existing guidelines regarding the decision-making process for
premature infants born at the threshold of viability, with an emphasis on the
respective responsabilities of the caregivers and the parents.
