Title: Parental perceptions of neighborhood environments, BMI, and active behaviors in girls aged 7–9 years
Authors: Aristides M. Machado‐Rodrigues, Ana Margarida Sebastião Santana, Augusta Gama, Isabel Mourão, Helena Nogueira
ID: openalex.W1657441566
URL: https://doi.org/10.1002/ajhb.22577

The study found some significant relationships between parental perceptions of
the environment and BMI and habitual PA of Portuguese girls, especially related
to security for walking and unpleasant neighborhoods, and access to
destinations.
