Title: Short Sleep Duration and Childhood Obesity: Cross-Sectional Analysis in Peru and Patterns in Four Developing Countries
Authors: Rodrigo M. Carrillo‐Larco, Antonio Bernabé‐Ortiz, J. Jaime Miranda
ID: openalex.W2071078718
URL: https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0112433

We aimed to describe the patterns of nutritional status and sleep duration in
children from Ethiopia, India, Peru and Vietnam; to assess the association
between short sleep duration and overweight and obesity, and if this was similar
among boys and girls in Peru.
