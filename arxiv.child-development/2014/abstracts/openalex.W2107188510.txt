Title: Why Synchrony Matters during Mother-Child Interactions: A Systematic Review
Authors: Chloë Leclère, Sylvie Viaux‐Savelon, Marie Avril, Catherine Achard, Mohamed Chétouani
ID: openalex.W2107188510
URL: https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0113571

Synchrony is a key feature of mother-infant interactions. Adopting an objective
approach in studying synchrony is not a simple task given available assessment
tools and due to its temporality and multimodal expression. We propose an
integrative approach combining clinical observation and engineering techniques
to improve the quality of synchrony analysis.
