Title: The Unequal Impact of Suspension on the Opportunity to Learn in CA
Authors: Daniel J. Losen, Kacy Martin
ID: openalex.W2960292698
URL: https://escholarship.org/uc/item/50b4g9h1

In 2016-17, schoolchildren in California lost an estimated 763,690 days of
instruction time, a figure based on the combined total of 381,845 in-school
suspensions (ISS) and out-of-school suspensions (OSS). This is an updated report
on CA suspension practice.
