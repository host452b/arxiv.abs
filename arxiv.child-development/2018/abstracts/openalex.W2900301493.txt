Title: Nuestras Historias- Designing a novel digital story intervention through participatory methods to improve maternal and child health in the Peruvian Amazon
Authors: Neha Limaye, Andrea C. Rivas-Nieto, César Cárcamo, Magaly M. Blas
ID: openalex.W2900301493
URL: https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0205673

The digital stories address community-specific problems through narrative
persuasion using local voices and photography. This combination had a high
acceptability among the target population and can serve as a model for
developing educational strategies in a community-tailored manner. This package
of seven videos will be further evaluated through a cluster randomized trial.
