Title: Predictors of breastfeeding duration in a predominantly Māori population in New Zealand
Authors: Kathy Manhire, Sheila Williams, David Tipene‐Leach, Sally Baddock, Sally Abel
ID: openalex.W2891763616
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/s12887-018-1274-9

Breastfeeding duration in this group of mainly Māori women was shorter than the
national average. Increasing the duration of breastfeeding by these mothers
could be further facilitated by ante and postnatal education involving their own
mothers and their partners in the support of breastfeeding and by addressing
pacifier use, smoking and alcohol use.
