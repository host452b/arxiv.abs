Title: Masking Level Difference em escolares: análises ambientais
Authors: Quemile Pribs Martins, Vivian Amaral Faccin, Mirtes Brückmann, Daniela Gil, Michele Vargas Garcia
ID: openalex.W2808770887
URL: https://doi.org/10.1590/2317-1782/20182017048

The Masking Level Difference in schoolchildren from seven to ten years old is
7.65 dB and is independent of the gender, parents' schooling and the average
monthly income of the schoolchild.
