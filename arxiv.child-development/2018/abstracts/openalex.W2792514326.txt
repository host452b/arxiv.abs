Title: Association between Breastfeeding Duration and Type of Birth Attendant
Authors: Jordyn T. Wallenborn, Saba W. Masho
ID: openalex.W2792514326
URL: https://doi.org/10.1155/2018/7198513

Findings from the current study highlight the importance of birth attendants in
breastfeeding decisions. Interventions are needed to overcome barriers
physicians encounter while providing breastfeeding support and education.
However, this study is limited by several confounding factors that have not been
controlled for as well as by the self-selection of the population.
