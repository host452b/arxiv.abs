Title: Estimating a Dutch Value Set for the Pediatric Preference-Based CHU9D Using a Discrete Choice Experiment with Duration
Authors: Donna Rowen, Brendan Mulhern, Katherine Stevens, J.H. Vermaire
ID: openalex.W2795864103
URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jval.2018.03.016

The valuation of the Child Health Utility 9D using online discrete choice
experiment with duration with adult members of the Dutch general population was
feasible and produced a valid model for use in cost utility analysis. Normative
questions are raised around the valuation of pediatric preference-based
measures, including the appropriate perspective for imagining hypothetical
pediatric health states.
