Title: Enfoques de aprendizaje y enfoques de enseñanza en la educación superior chilena: un análisis comparativo
Authors: Alejandro Villalobos Clavería
ID: openalex.W2783125989
URL: https://doi.org/10.15366/tp2018.31.007

This article describes and analyzes the results of a research carried out in
2015 and 2016 in a sample of students and professors from three regional
universities on their approaches to teaching and learning..The results obtained
show a correlation between the teaching and learning approach in the sample of
teachers and students consulted. Gender differences in the type of motivation
are recognized, where women have a deeper motivation for their learning. There
are also differences in the students according to the type of secondary
education, where graduates of high school technical professionals achieve higher
levels of motivation and strategies used.
