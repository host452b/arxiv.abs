Title: Oral Tradition and Welsh Literature: A Description and Survey
Authors: Brynley F. Roberts
ID: openalex.W2098459391
URL: https://hdl.handle.net/10355/64096

Brynley F. Roberts (National Library of Wales) has contributed numerous articles
and monographs to the study of Welsh literature, including a recent essay in The
Craft of Fiction (1984) on the transition from traditional tale to literary
story.
