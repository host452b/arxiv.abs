Title: Trends in alcohol use among young people according to the pattern of consumption on starting university: A 9-year follow-up study
Authors: Lucía Moure-Rodríguez, Carina Carbia, Eduardo López‐Caneda, Montserrat Corral, Fernando Cadaveira
ID: openalex.W2797126601
URL: https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0193741

The prevalence rates of both RC and BD at age 27 years were much higher among
university students who already followed these patterns of consumption at age 18
years, with the differences being proportionally higher among women. Focusing on
the age of onset of alcohol consumption and hindering access to alcohol by
minors should be priority objectives aimed at preventing students from engaging
in these patterns of alcohol consumption at university.
