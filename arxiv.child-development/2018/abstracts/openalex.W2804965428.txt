Title: Decolonizing Education Through Outdoor Learning: The Learning Story of an Indigenous Kindergarten Teacher
Authors: Alexandria Middlemiss

This study examined the decolonizing pedagogy and practices of a First
Nations&#13;\nkindergarten teacher. Indigenous communities across Canada and the

both&#13;\nan Indigenous and non-Indigenous lens. The author and the teacher
