Title: Arts Education Research through the Lens of the Every Student Succeeds Act
Authors: Yinmei Wan, Meredith Ludwig, Andrea Boyle
ID: openalex.W4401906238
URL: http://dx.doi.org/10.59656/a-ya1991.001

This report explores research on approaches to arts education and identifies
those that qualify for federal education funding under the Every Student
Succeeds Act of 2015.
