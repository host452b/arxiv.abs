Title: The role of intention and self-efficacy on the association between breastfeeding of first and second child, a Danish cohort study
Authors: Hanne Kronborg, Else Foverskov, Michael Væth, Rikke Damkjær Maimburg
ID: openalex.W2901843768
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/s12884-018-2086-5

Due to a reinforcing effect of intention and self-efficacy, breastfeeding
support should focus on helping the first time mothers to succeed as well as to
identify the second time mother with low self-efficacy and additional need for
support.
