Title: The Use of Metaphors With LEGO® SERIOUS PLAY ® For Harmony and Innovation
Authors: Sean McCusker, Joanne Clifford Swan
ID: openalex.W2905999581
URL: https://doi.org/10.18646/2056.54.18-013

This paper presents LEGO SERIOUS PLAY as a method of enabling members of staff
with diverse expertise, experience and expectations to come together to work
towards a shared strategic plan in the light of the changing HE culture. The
method is applied to a teacher education department within a post-92 university,
where despite differing views of the challenges facing the department, consensus
is achieved through the building of shared metaphors and joint narratives.
