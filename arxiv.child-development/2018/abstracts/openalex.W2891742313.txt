Title: Opioid and Drug Prevalence in Top 40's Music: A 30 Year Review
Authors: Curtis Hanba, Daniel Hanba
ID: openalex.W2891742313
URL: https://doi.org/10.3122/jabfm.2018.05.180001

There is increasing prevalence in the lyrical mention of opioids, among other
drugs, in Top 40's music. Nearly 50% of 2016 Top 40's songs reference drugs or
alcohol. Further inquiry may be warranted to evaluate the societal impact and
persuasive abilities of popular culture, including Top 40's music, on American
drug and alcohol use.
