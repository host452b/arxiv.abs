Title: Current Perspectives on Research Ethics in Qualitative Research
Authors: Wolff‐Michael Roth, Hella von Unger
ID: openalex.W2893959541
URL: https://www.ssoar.info/ssoar/handle/document/59972

In this article, we provide a brief introduction to the special issue on
research ethics in qualitative research. We describe the general context within
which our idea emerged to organize a special issue and present its design and,
for purposes of transparency, some particulars with respect to the selection and
review process. We sketch some of the common themes that are shared across parts
of the paper set, including critical analysis of ethics codes and ethics
reviews, the intricacies of informed consent, confidentiality and anonymity in
qualitative research and questions of vulnerability.
