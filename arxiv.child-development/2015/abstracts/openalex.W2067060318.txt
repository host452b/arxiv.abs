Title: Effect of distribution of educational material to mothers on duration and severity of diarrhoea and pneumonia, Midlands Province, Zimbabwe: a cluster randomized controlled trial
Authors: Meggie Gabida, Milton Chemhuru, Mufuta Tshimanga, Notion Tafara Gombe, Lucia Takundwa
ID: openalex.W2067060318
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/s13006-015-0037-6

A combined community and distribution of a newsletter to mothers on promotion of
exclusive breastfeeding reduces duration of diarrhoea at 14 weeks. At 20 weeks,
the newsletter worked better for both duration of diarrhoea and pneumonia
compared to cIYCF training alone.
