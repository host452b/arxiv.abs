Title: Challenges for Work-Based Learning in Vocational Education and Training in the Nordic Countries
Authors: Christian Helms Jørgensen
ID: openalex.W2418045973
URL: https://doi.org/10.1007/978-94-6300-244-8_12

Systems of vocational education and training (VET) that include periods of
workbased learning have many attractive qualities, seen from a political
perspective. VET systems that build on the tradition of apprenticeship with
alternating training provide a valuable alternative for young people who chose
not to pursue an academic career.
