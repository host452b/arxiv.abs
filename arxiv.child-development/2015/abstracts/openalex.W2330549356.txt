Title: Dental Ritual Mutilations and Forensic Odontologist Practice: a Review of the Literature
Authors: Vilma Pinchi, Patrizia Barbieri, Francesco Pradella, Martina Focardi, Viola Bartolini
ID: openalex.W2330549356
URL: https://doi.org/10.15644/asc49/1/1

The knowledge of dental alteration due to oral mutilations can be a powerful
tool for the identification procedures of living or dead persons or even in
human remains especially providing relevant information about the ethnic origins
and the cultural background of a subject. Some medical legal issues for the
odontologist about dental mutilation are also addressed in the paper.
