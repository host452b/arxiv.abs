Title: Exploring Metrics to Express Energy Expenditure of Physical Activity in Youth
Authors: Robert G. McMurray, Nancy F. Butte, Scott E. Crouter, Stewart G. Trost, Karin A. Pfeiffer
ID: openalex.W2215126364
URL: https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0130869

No energy expenditure metric completely eliminated the influence of age,
physical characteristics, and sex. The Adult MET consistently overestimated EE.
YOUTH-MET was better for expressing energy expenditure for sedentary and light
activities, whereas allometric scaling was better for moderate and vigorous
intensity activities. From a practical perspective, The YOUTH-MET may be the
more feasible metric for improving of the Compendium of Physical Activities for
Youth.
