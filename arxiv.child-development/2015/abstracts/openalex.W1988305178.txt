Title: WHO European Childhood Obesity Surveillance Initiative: associations between sleep duration, screen time and food consumption frequencies
Authors: Claudia Börnhorst, Trudy Wijnhoven, Marie Kunešová, Agneta Yngve, Ana Isabel Rito
ID: openalex.W1988305178
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/s12889-015-1793-3

The results suggest a potential relation between high screen time exposure and
increased consumption frequencies of foods high in fat, free sugar or salt
whereas long sleep duration may favourably be related to children's food
choices. Both screen time and sleep duration are modifiable behaviours that may
be tackled in childhood obesity prevention efforts.
