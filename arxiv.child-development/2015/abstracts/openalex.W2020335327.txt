Title: Prospective associations between sedentary time, sleep duration and adiposity in adolescents
Authors: Paul J. Collings, Katrien Wijndaele, Kirsten Corder, Kate Westgate, Charlotte L Ridgway
ID: openalex.W2020335327
URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.sleep.2015.02.532

Sedentary time may not determine changes in adiposity from mid- to late
adolescence, nor may sleep duration in girls. However, sleep length may be
inversely associated with adiposity gain in boys, depending on whether the
relationship is confounded or mediated by MVPA and depression.
