Title: Is Drama the New Bullying? Differentiating Bullying and Drama Among College Students
Authors: Katherine Magner
ID: openalex.W3083230045
URL: https://doi.org/10.22215/etd/2019-13446

Compared to research on bullying in schools and in the workplace, little is
known about the prevalence and typology of bullying among college age-students.
Recent research suggests that young adults may be less in favour of the term
"bullying", but more open to the notion of "drama". The concept of drama has
existed for some time, and is common vernacular in both media and entertainment,
