Title: The Effects of Brain-Based Teaching With I-Think Maps and Brain Gym Approach towards Physics Understanding
Authors: Salmiza Saleh, Ahmad Suffian Ahmad Mazlan

The purpose of this study was to assess the effects of Brain-Based Teaching with
i-Think Maps and the Brain Gym Approach (BBT-iTBA) compared to the conventional
teaching approach (CTA) towards Physicsâ€™ conceptual understanding amongst male
