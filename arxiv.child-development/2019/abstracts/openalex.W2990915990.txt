Title: Association between sleep duration and hypertension of migrant workers in China: a national cross-sectional surveillance study
Authors: Yunqi Guan, Mei Zhang, Xiao Zhang, Zhenping Zhao, Zhengjing Huang
ID: openalex.W2990915990
URL: https://doi.org/10.1136/bmjopen-2019-031126

The association between sleep duration and hypertension varies by age. Short
sleep duration (<6 hours) is associated with an increased prevalence of
hypertension in both genders.
