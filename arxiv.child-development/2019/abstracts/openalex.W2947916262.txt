Title: Rethinking engagement: Exploring women’s technology use during the perinatal period through a Kaupapa Māori consistent approach
Authors: Norina Gasteiger, Anneka Anderson, Karen Day
ID: openalex.W2947916262
URL: http://doi.org/10.12784/nzcomjnl55.2019.3.20-26

Background: Barriers to accessing perinatal health services in New Zealand
remain a significant problem, especially for Mori and rural populations.
Information technologies and telehealth can facilitate the distribution of
health-related information and promote provider engagement during the perinatal
period.
