Title: Relationship between sleep duration and hypertension in northeast China: a cross-sectional study
Authors: Meng Li, Shoumeng Yan, Shan Jiang, Xiaoyu Ma, Tianyu Gao
ID: openalex.W2914050748
URL: https://doi.org/10.1136/bmjopen-2018-023916

Our study demonstrates that short sleep duration was significantly associated
with hypertension among people aged 18-44 years in northeast China.
