Title: The experience of parents of children with rare diseases when communicating with healthcare professionals: towards an integrative theory of trust
Authors: Beni Gómez‐Zúñiga, Rafael Pulido Moyano, Modesta Pousada, Alicia García Oliva, Manuel Armayones
ID: openalex.W2953665651
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/s13023-019-1134-1

Trust is the cornerstone of parent-doctor communication in the field of children
with rare diseases. If the sources of trust are present, they create a degree of
trust that bolsters both parties in the search for a common goal: providing the
child with the best possible care.
