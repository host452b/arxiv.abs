Title: Developments in Information Technology and the Sexual Depression of Japanese Youth since 2000
Authors: Maki Hirayama
ID: openalex.W2913738576
URL: https://doi.org/10.1007/s41978-019-00034-2

Abstract In Japan more young people became sexually inactive in 2000s,
especially since around 2005.On the other hand, Internet and digital technology
were spread in the same period. In this paper, five phases of Internet and
digital technology are investigated to realize what happened to the sexuality of
Japanese youth associated with the technology: e-mail and SNS, online
pornography, fantasy world of Otaku leisure, dating sites and applications,
sexual service industry. Online pornography of extreme contents and strong
stimuli with completely male-centered vision overflew in the 2000s. With the
influence, both men and women have got difficulties in having real sex.
Animations and games to satisfy the romantic needs and libidos of the youth
gained popularity in 2000s,to overwhelm real romance and sex. In the last part,
the need of cross-cultural comparative studies on technology and sexuality is
insisted.
