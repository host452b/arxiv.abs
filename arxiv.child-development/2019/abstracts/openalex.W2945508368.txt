Title: Placing the Public in Public Health in Post-War Britain, 1948–2012
Authors: Alex Mold, Peder Clark, Gareth Millward, Daisy Payling
ID: openalex.W2945508368
URL: https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-030-18685-2

This open access book explores the question of who or what ‘the public’ is
within ‘public health’ in post-war Britain. Drawing on historical research, the
book presents a new perspective on the relationship between state and citizen
from 1948 onwards.
