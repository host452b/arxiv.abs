Title: Conceptual framework for living with and beyond cancer: A systematic review and narrative synthesis
Authors: Clair Le Boutillier, Stephanie Archer, Claire Barry, Alex King, Louise Mansfield
ID: openalex.W2920071870
URL: https://doi.org/10.1002/pon.5046

Three themes describe the experience of living with and beyond cancer:
adversity, restoration, and compatibility. The ARC framework provides an
empirically informed grounding for future research and practice in supportive
cancer care for this population.
