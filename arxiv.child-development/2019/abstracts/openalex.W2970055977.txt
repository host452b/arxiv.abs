Title: Education for Democratic Intercultural Citizenship
Authors: Wiel Veugelers
ID: openalex.W2970055977
URL: https://doi.org/10.1163/9789004411944

Europe and its participating countries need a strong democracy, an inclusive
society, and active citizens. Intercultural and democratic competences are
crucial for the future of Europe, its countries and its citizens. Education can
play a central role in developing the necessary knowledge, skills and attitudes
among young people.
