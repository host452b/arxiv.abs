Title: Narratives of Place, Culture and Identity : Second-Generation Greek-Americans Return 'Home'
Authors: Anastasia Christou
ID: openalex.W1900598862
URL: https://doi.org/10.5117/9789053568781

IMISCOE is a European Commission-funded Network of Excellence of more than 350
scientists from various research institutes that specialise in migration and
integration issues in Europe. These researchers, who come from all branches of
the economic and social sciences, the humanities and law, implement an
integrated, multidisciplinary and internationally comparative research program
that focuses on Europe's migration and integration challenges.
