Title: Teenage sexual attitudes, norms, desires and intentions : the impact of preferred musical genres
Authors: Séna Agbo-Quaye
ID: openalex.W90218283
URL: http://bura.brunel.ac.uk/handle/2438/6300

This thesis focuses on teenage sexual attitudes and norms as moderated by their
preferred music genre. The research questions addressed here are: What are the
genre differences in lyrical representations of relationships and male and
female characteristics? What are young peoples' perceptions of the impact of
these genre differences on their lives? How does genre preference influence
teenage safer sex attitudes, desires, norms, control, group identity and
intentions?
