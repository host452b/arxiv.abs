Title: Breastfeeding duration in mothers who express breast milk: a cohort study
Authors: Nwet Nwet Win, Colin Binns, Yun Zhao, Jane Scott, Wendy H. Oddy
ID: openalex.W1803764633
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/1746-4358-1-28

This study found that mothers who express breast milk are more likely to
breastfeed to six months (any breastfeeding). While further research is required
in different cultures to confirm these results, the appropriate use of expressed
breast milk may be a means to help mothers to achieve six months of full
breastfeeding while giving more lifestyle options.
