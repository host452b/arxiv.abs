Title: Cyber-Dilemmas: Gendered Hierarchies, New Technologies and Cyber-Safety in Schools
Authors: Shaheen Shariff, Rachel Gouin
ID: openalex.W1520842503
URL: https://doi.org/10.7202/1121119ar

This paper addresses gender issues relating to cyber-bullying among adolescents
which is reflective of adult Internet use. It highlights legal considerations to
address the policy vacuum on this emerging form of gendered violence.
