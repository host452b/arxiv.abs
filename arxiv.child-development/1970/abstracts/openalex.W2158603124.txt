Title: Determinants of post-partum amenorrhea among Nepalese rural mothers: A multivariate analysis
Authors: TR Aryal
ID: openalex.W2158603124
URL: https://doi.org/10.3126/kumj.v8i1.3214

The variables like birth interval, parity, breastfeeding, education, socio-
economic status and child survival are found the main determinants of PPA
duration among Nepalese mothers.
