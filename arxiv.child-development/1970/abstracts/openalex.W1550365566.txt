Title: Culture shock: negotiating feelings in the field
Authors: Rachel Irwin
ID: openalex.W1550365566
URL: https://doi.org/10.22582/am.v9i1.64

Culture shock is the depression and anxiety experienced by many people when they
travel or move to a new social and cultural setting. Although many
anthropologist experience culture shock whilst in the field, this is a subject
that is rarely discussed in the academic setting. This article explores the
issue of culture shock and offers some thoughts on how to relieve it, drawing on
the author's own experiences.
