Title: Going Nowhere? The Politics of Remembering (and Forgetting) Molly Ringwald
Authors: Christina Lee
ID: openalex.W2051483857
URL: https://doi.org/10.5130/csr.v13i1.2156

This article focuses on the actor Molly Ringwald, forever famous for her roles
in a series of 1980s teen films directed by John Hughes, to explore notions of
generational remembering and forgetting.
