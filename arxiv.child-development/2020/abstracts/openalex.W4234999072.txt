Title: Psychosocial Experiences of African Migrants in Six European Countries
Authors: Erhabor Sunday Idemudia, Klaus Boehnke
ID: openalex.W4234999072
URL: https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-030-48347-0

This open access book is an empirical narrative of the psychological and social
experiences of 3500 African migrants who migrated to 6 European countries:
Germany, Spain, Italy, The Netherlands, France, and the UK. It is the only book
that reports a cross-cultural study of African migrants to Europe
