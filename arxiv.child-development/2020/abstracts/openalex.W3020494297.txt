Title: Barriers to Cancer Screening Uptake in Women: A Qualitative Study from Tamil Nadu, India
Authors: S Mahalakshmi, Suresh Sundaram
ID: openalex.W3020494297
URL: https://doi.org/10.31557/apjcp.2020.21.4.1081

Psychosocial factors, the major barriers for screening uptake in women have
remained unchanged over the years. Increasing awareness campaigns, usage of
decision-making aids and changes in government policies are crucial for
improving the rate of uptake and successful implementation of national screening
programs.
