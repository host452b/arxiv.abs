Title: Peso ao nascer e asma em adultos jovens de uma coorte de nascimentos
Authors: Fernanda Pino Vitti
ID: openalex.W3023499363
URL: https://doi.org/10.11606/t.17.2020.tde-13022020-102326

Background: According to the hypothesis of the Developmental Origin of Health
and Disease, scientific evidence suggest that adverse intrauterine conditions
impair fetal growth and may have long-term consequences facilitating development
of chronic noncommunicable diseases in adulthood. These adverse conditions, such
as low birth weight (LBW) have been identify as risk factors for asthma. Aims:
