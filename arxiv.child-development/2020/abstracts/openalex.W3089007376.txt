Title: &lt;p&gt;The Perceived Health Needs of Primiparous Mothers Referring to Primary Health Care Centers: A Qualitative Study&lt;/p&gt;
Authors: Shahin Salarvand, Masoumeh-Sadat Mousavi, Darya Esmaeilbeigy, Farahnaz Changaee, Mohammad Almasian
ID: openalex.W3089007376
URL: https://doi.org/10.2147/ijwh.s258446

The present study showed that primiparous women need to receive more social and
psychological support from family members and healthcare workers and that it is
essential to improve the available infrastructures and services in healthcare
centers and to provide the needed counseling to pregnant mothers to enable them
to go through the pregnancy period smoothly.
