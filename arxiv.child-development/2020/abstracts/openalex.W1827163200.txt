Title: Are rural American Indian adolescents becoming a race of angels?
Authors: John Cournane
ID: openalex.W1827163200
URL: https://doi.org/10.7202/1069531ar

This article discusses the importance of identity formation and the development
in young Aboriginal persons as important in the early years of education.
Education is an important anchor that would help ensure a reduction in
adolescent suicides and improve ego development.
