Title: Longer shared parental leave is associated with longer duration of breastfeeding: a cross-sectional study among Swedish mothers and their partners
Authors: Maria Grandahl, Jenny Stern, Eva‐Lotta Funkquist
ID: openalex.W3015423465
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/s12887-020-02065-1

The duration of partial breastfeeding was associated with higher parental
educational level, higher age, non-instrumental vaginal birth and longer
parental leave.
