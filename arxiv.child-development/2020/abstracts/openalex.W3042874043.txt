Title: Changes in Body Weight, Physical Activity, and Lifestyle During the Semi-lockdown Period After the Outbreak of COVID-19 in China: An Online Survey
Authors: Ming He, Yin Xian, Xiaodong Lv, Jinsong He, Yixing Ren
ID: openalex.W3042874043
URL: https://doi.org/10.1017/dmp.2020.237

Normal weight individuals, who are not normally troubled by overweight or
obesity, had less awareness of weight gain than people with a BMI ≥ 24. Under
the conditions of the semi-lockdown, they tended to gain weight.
