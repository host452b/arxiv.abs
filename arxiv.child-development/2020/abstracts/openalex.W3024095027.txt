Title: Doctors’ identity transitions: Choosing to occupy a state of ‘betwixt and between’
Authors: Lisi Gordon, Charlotte E. Rees, Divya Jindal‐Snape
ID: openalex.W3024095027
URL: https://doi.org/10.1111/medu.14219

This paper is the first to explore longitudinally doctors' liminal experiences
through trainee-to-trained transitions. Our findings also make conceptual
contributions to the health care literature, as well as the wider
interdisciplinary liminality literature, by adding further layers to
conceptualisations and introducing the notion of occupying liminality.
