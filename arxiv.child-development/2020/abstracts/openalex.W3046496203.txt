Title: Nostalgia and Hope: Intersections between Politics of Culture, Welfare, and Migration in Europe
Authors: Ov Cristian Norocel, Anders Hellström, Martin Bak Jørgensen
ID: openalex.W3046496203
URL: https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-030-41694-2

This open access book shows how the politics of migration affects community-
building in the 21st century, thereby focusing on the discursive
conceptualization of migration in contemporary European politics and on welfare
and culture both within party politics and beyond.
