Title: Inclusion, Education and Translanguaging
Authors: Julie A. Panagiotopoulou, Lisa Rosén, Jenna Strzykala
ID: openalex.W3088008863
URL: https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-658-28128-1

This open access book is designed as an international anthology on the broader
subject of inclusion, education and translanguaging. Prefaced by Ofelia García,
the volume unites conceptional and empirical contributions focusing on various
givers and receivers within educational institutions.
