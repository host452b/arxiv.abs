Title: Assessing factors influencing adolescents’ dietary behaviours in urban Ethiopia using participatory photography
Authors: Ursula Trübswasser, Kaleab Baye, Michelle Holdsworth, Megan Loeffen, Edith J. M. Feskens
ID: openalex.W3049225939
URL: https://doi.org/10.1017/s1368980020002487

Concerns for food safety, hygiene and affordability are the dominating factors
for adolescents' food choices. These concerns, together with limited nutrition
knowledge and preference for packaged foods, could make cheap, ultra-processed
packaged foods more desirable.
