Title: Young men’s experiences of living with existential concerns: “living close to a bottomless darkness”
Authors: Maria Lundvall, Ulrica Hörberg, Lina Palmér, Gunilla Carlsson, Elisabeth Lindberg
ID: openalex.W3081777510
URL: https://doi.org/10.1080/17482631.2020.1810947

We conclude that strengthening young men's health processes requires healthcare
professionals to create an atmosphere where young men feel safe talking about
existential concerns without feeling exposed and vulnerable.
