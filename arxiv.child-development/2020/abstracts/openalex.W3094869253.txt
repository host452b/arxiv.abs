Title: The professional identity of Lithuanian midwifery students: An exploratory study
Authors: Alix Bukkfalvi-Cadotte
ID: openalex.W3094869253
URL: https://doi.org/10.18332/ejm/127515

These findings suggest that the participant student-midwives view midwifery as a
medical profession, rooted in the biomedical field, but also characterized by a
unique care approach. This exploratory study thus contributes to a better
understanding of midwifery in Lithuania.
