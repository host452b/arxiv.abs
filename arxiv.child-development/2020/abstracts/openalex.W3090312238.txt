Title: Vitimização por bullying e fatores associados em estudantes brasileiros com idade de 13 a 17 anos: estudo populacional
Authors: Vandoval Rodrigues Veloso, Felipe Barbosa de Sousa Costa, Cristiane Cronemberger de Arruda Marques, Jesusmar Ximenes Andrade, Cássio Eduardo Soares Miranda
ID: openalex.W3090312238
URL: https://doi.org/10.1590/1980-549720200097

Preventive efforts that include programs to encourage greater social support
among students, as well as the implementation of practices that encourage
respect for students' differences and singularities can contribute to reducing
bullying practices.
