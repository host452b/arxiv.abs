Title: Vulnerability in Scandinavian Art and Culture
Authors: Adriana Margareta Dancus, Mats Hyvönen, Maria Karlsson
ID: openalex.W3104680105
URL: https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-030-37382-5

In this open access book, scholars discuss how contemporary Scandinavian art and
media—fiction and documentary film, radio, social media, literature, drawings,
art installations—stage various forms of vulnerability in the welfare state. It
asks, how do privilege and vulnerability interact?
