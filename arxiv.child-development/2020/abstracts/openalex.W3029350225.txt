Title: Perception and practices of menstruation restrictions among urban adolescent girls and women in Nepal: a cross-sectional survey
Authors: Amrita Mukherjee, Mingma Lama, Uddhav Khakurel, Alok Nath Jha, Fatima Ajose
ID: openalex.W3029350225
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/s12978-020-00935-6

This study throws light on existing social discriminations, deep-rooted cultural
and religious superstitions among women, and gender inequalities in the urban
areas of Kathmandu valley in Nepal. Targeted education and awareness are needed
to make changes and balance between cultural and social practices during
menstruation.
