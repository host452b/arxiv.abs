Title: Value and the Humanities
Authors: Zoe Hope Bulaitis
ID: openalex.W4231237050
URL: https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-030-37892-9

This open access book provides a rich and previously underdeveloped narrative of
value in higher education in England, drawing upon historical, financial, and
critical debates to trace the shift from liberal to neoliberal education from
the nineteenth century to the present day.
