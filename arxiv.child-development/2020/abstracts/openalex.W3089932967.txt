Title: Prevalence and sociodemographic factors associated with meeting the 24-hour movement guidelines in a sample of Brazilian adolescents

Adherence to the sleep duration recommendation was higher than to the screen-
time and MVPA recommendations and few in this sample of Brazilian adolescents
