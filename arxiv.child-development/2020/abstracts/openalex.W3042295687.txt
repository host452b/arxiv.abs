Title: Gender-Specific Longitudinal Association of Sleep Duration with Blood Pressure among Children: Evidence from CHNS 2004–2015
Authors: Lili Huang, Jiajun Lyu, Zichong Long, Yuanqing Xia, Yiting Chen
ID: openalex.W3042295687
URL: https://doi.org/10.1155/2020/5475297

There was a time trend of decreasing sleep duration alongside increasing BP
among children from 2004 to 2015. Short sleep duration was independently
associated with increased DBP; however, only girls were susceptible to the
association.
