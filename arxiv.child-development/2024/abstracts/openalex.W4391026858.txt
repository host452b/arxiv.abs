Title: Combination of sugar-sweetened beverage consumption, screen-based sedentary time, and sleep duration and their association with South Korean adolescents' mental health
Authors: Jin Suk
ID: openalex.W4391026858
URL: https://doi.org/10.3389/fpubh.2023.1293505

The findings demonstrate that healthcare providers should develop and conduct
family-and school-based programs to restrict SSB consumption, screen-based
sedentary behaviors, and sleep duration to improve adolescents' mental health.
