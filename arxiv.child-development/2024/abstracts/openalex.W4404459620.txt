Title: Exploring the Views of Spanish Pre-Service Teachers on Phonics From a Quantitative Research Perspective
Authors: Javier Fernández-Molina
ID: openalex.W4404459620
URL: http://dx.doi.org/10.15823/p.2024.155.10

English is especially demanding for Spanish speakers due to the greater number
of sounds, the complex letter-sound relationship, and prosodic features. English
phonics programmes may offer clearer, more comprehensive instruction. 84 pre-
service teachers completed a questionnaire on the use of phonics in Spanish EFL
preschool and primary classrooms. Results show that specific training is needed,
not all participants favour this method, and a more eclectic approach to early
literacy is advocated.
