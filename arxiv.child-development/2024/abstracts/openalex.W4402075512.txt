Title: The effects of duration of any breastfeeding on body mass index in Australian children: Exploration of health, economic and equity impacts
Authors: Joseph Carrello, Vicki Brown, Anagha Killedar, Alison Hayes
ID: openalex.W4402075512
URL: https://doi.org/10.1111/ijpo.13167

Efforts to support increased breastfeeding duration could result in reduced
prevalence of overweight and obesity and save healthcare costs, however,
additional action would be required to improve equity.
