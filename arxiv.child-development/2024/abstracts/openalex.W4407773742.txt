Title: Innovative Learning Media Exhibition: Cultivating Motivation among Islamic Education Students to Create Engaging Learning Media
Authors: Umi Hijriyah, Baharudin Baharudin, Ida Fiteriani, Fitriati Fitriati, Nurul Azizah
ID: openalex.W4407773742
URL: https://doi.org/10.21043/edukasia.v19i2.28599

&lt;p class="07KatakunciKeywords"&gt;&lt;span lang="EN-GB"&gt;This research
aimed to determine the relationship between media exhibitions and students'
motivation. It was based on the fact that PAI students got lack motivation to
create innovative learning media. This lack of motivation was caused by a
limited understanding of the importance of learning media, insufficient skills
and creativity, and inadequate facilities. The research design employed in this
study was quantitative with a correlational approach. The sample consisted of 60
students from the fifth semester of the PAI program, class of 2022, at UIN Raden
Intan Lampung. The instrument used in this study was a non-test instrument in
the form of a questionnaire. The results from the T-test and correlation test
revealed a significant relationship between media exhibitions and the motivation
of students in the Islamic Religious Education program. The implication of this
research was that it could serve a reference for taking further actions to
address issues related to media exhibitions and students' motivation to develop
or create innovative learning media. This study was expected to be used by
educators as a reference for understanding the relationship between media
exhibitions and the motivation of PAI students to create engaging learning
media.&lt;/span&gt;&lt;/p&gt;
