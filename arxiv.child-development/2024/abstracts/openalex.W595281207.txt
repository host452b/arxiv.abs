Title: Beyond Fun: Serious Games and Media
Authors: Drew Davidson
ID: openalex.W595281207
URL: http://repository.cmu.edu/etcpress/3

This book focuses on strategies for applying games, simulations and interactive
experiences in learning contexts. The contributors orchestrated this collection
together, reading and writing as a whole so that concepts resonate across
articles. Throughout, the promises and problems of implementing games and media
in learning experiences are explored. The articles have been authored by Clark
Aldrich, Ian Bogost, Mia Consalvo, William Crosbie, Drew Davidson, Simon
Egenfeldt-Nielsen, Melinda Jackson, Donna Leishman, Michael Mateas, Marc
Prensky, Scott Rettberg, Kurt Squire, David Thomas, Siobhan Thomas, Jill Walker
Rettberg, and Jenny Weight.
