Title: Evolution of Sleep Duration and Screen Time Between 2018 and 2022 Among Canadian Adolescents: Evidence of Drifts Accompanying the COVID-19 Pandemic
Authors: Krystel Poirier, Lise Gauvin, Slim Haddad, Richard E. Bélanger, Scott T. Leatherdale
ID: openalex.W4391693394
URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jadohealth.2023.12.023

Adolescents now spend almost equal amounts of time sleeping and using screens, a
situation that calls for urgent public health actions. These findings highlight
the importance of tracking changes in adolescents' behaviours over time, to
design and implement interventions adapted to the changing health needs of
different groups.
