Title: Gender identity services for children and young people: navigating uncertainty through communication, collaboration and care
Authors: Hilary Cass
ID: openalex.W4402323793
URL: https://doi.org/10.1192/bjp.2024.162

This editorial describes the Cass Review findings and the extraordinary
challenge we all face in managing uncertainty amid a toxic and highly polarised
debate. Children and young people will only get the best care if patients and
professionals join forces to seek answers collaboratively and respectfully.
