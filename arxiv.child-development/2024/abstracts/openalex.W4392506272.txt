Title: Mission Hill School: Fostering Creative Storytelling Through Democratic Learning
Authors: Carolyn Shadid Lewis, Selma Lewis, Deborah Meier
ID: openalex.W4392506272
URL: https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-031-46297-9_19

Abstract Mission Hill School (MHS) was a democratically run, thematic, project-
based school in Boston. Founding member Deborah Meier provides an introduction
to a mother and daughter’s personal narrative of their time at the school.
Boston Public Schools closed MHS in 2022.
