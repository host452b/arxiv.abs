Title: “That's Online Writing, Not Boring School Writing”: Writing With Blogs and the Talkback Project
Authors: Shelbie Witte
ID: openalex.W1989088574
URL: https://doi.org/10.1598/jaal.51.2.1

The Talkback Project used blog software to involve eighth‐grade language arts
students, preservice teachers, and parents in online discussion of young adult
novels and their personal connections with them. The author shares the
challenges associated with the project—including the implications of Internet
safety and legal concerns associated with technology integration—but argues that
classroom blogging provides positive opportunities for students to Develop their
digital fluency Strengthen traditional literacy skills Apply real‐life
21st‐century skills in a safe and monitored environment
