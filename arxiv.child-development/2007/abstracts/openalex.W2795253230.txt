Title: The education of Princess Mary Tudor
Authors: Katherine K. Perkins
ID: openalex.W2795253230
URL: https://doi.org/10.31390/gradschool_theses.1090

Mary Tudor, the first officially crowned queen regnant of England, received a
humanist education. A curriculum was recommended for her in multiple writings by
Spanish humanist Juan Luis Vives. This thesis attempts to synthesize and examine
information the nature of this plan for a princess's education and the extent to
which it was implemented.
