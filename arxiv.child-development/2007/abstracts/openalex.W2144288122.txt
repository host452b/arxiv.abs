Title: Out of the smokescreen II: will an advertisement targeting the tobacco industry affect young people’s perception of smoking in movies and their intention to smoke?
Authors: Christine A. Edwards, Wendy Peia Oakes, Diane F. Bull
ID: openalex.W2144288122
URL: https://doi.org/10.1136/tc.2006.017194

This real-world study suggests that placing an antismoking advertisement before
movies containing smoking scenes can help to immunise non-smokers against the
influences of film stars' smoking. Caution must be exercised in the type of
advertisement screened as some types of advertising may reinforce smokers'
intentions to smoke.
