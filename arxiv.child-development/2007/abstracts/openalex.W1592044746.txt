Title: Arts for Academic Achievement: A Brief Review of Research on Readers' Theatre and Tableau in Literacy Instruction
Authors: Jennifer Willcutt
ID: openalex.W1592044746
URL: http://purl.umn.edu/143645

This review of the literature seeks to identify and summarize scientific
research on&#13;\nthe use and effectiveness of Readers’ Theatre and Tableau in
literacy education. These&#13;\nprograms integrate theatre activities into the
classroom and are intended to enhance literacy&#13;\nskills. This review will
also define key terms, address the use of drama techniques in the&#13;\ncontext
of current standards- and evidence-based educational practices and
policies,&#13;\nincluding Reading First, and discuss the nature of the
relationship between the use of drama&#13;\ntechniques in the classroom and
literacy achievement.
