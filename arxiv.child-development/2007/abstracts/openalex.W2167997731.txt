Title: A conceptual framework for implementation fidelity
Authors: Christopher Carroll, Malcolm Patterson, Stephen Wood, Andrew Booth, Jo Rick
ID: openalex.W2167997731
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/1748-5908-2-40

Implementation fidelity is an important source of variation affecting the
credibility and utility of research. The conceptual framework presented here
offers a means for measuring this variable and understanding its place in the
process of intervention implementation.
