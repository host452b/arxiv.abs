Title: Eyes in the back of our heads: reading futures for literacy teaching
Authors: Jo‐Anne Reid
ID: openalex.W276310785
URL: http://hdl.handle.net/2123/2338

In this presentation, I want to challenge you – on the last day of this
conference called Future Directions in Literacy – to think back over the past
few days and to review and consider what you have heard. I ask you to reflect on
the key messages you have taken from the presentations you have listened to, the
interactive sessions you have participated in, and the discussions you have had
with your friends and colleagues who have shared this experience. I want to
provoke you a little, to think, to consider what it all means, this talk about
literacy directions into the future, and the challenges that Leonie Rowan,
Barbara Comber, Peter Freebody and Jackie Marsh have thrown to you in this slot
each morning in what has indeed been an international conversation about
literacy and literacy teaching.
