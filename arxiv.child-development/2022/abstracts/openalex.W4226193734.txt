Title: Problems and Countermeasures of Preschool Education Management
Authors: Lingxuan Wang, Noichun
ID: openalex.W4226193734
URL: https://doi.org/10.18282/l-e.v10i5.2655

Nowadays, the younger generation of parents are worried about the management of
preschool education.So,the purpose &#x0D; of the article is to reveal the
problems existing in preschool education management, and put forward
corresponding solutions, to &#x0D; achieve effective improvement of educational
management,to assist the current and future management in the school
(kindergarten) &#x0D; to provide a variety of forms of management programs.The
content of this article will involve preschool education management, &#x0D;
school management, and some problems existing in management,etc.The core of the
content around the “Kindergarten Work &#x0D; Regulations”, take the actual
situation of kindergarten, four participating teachers analyzed four
representative literatures together, &#x0D; to analyze the actual problems
existing in preschool education management. In the literature review of this
paper, several articles &#x0D; on preschool education management are elaborated,
and the views and viewpoints on education management are described. This &#x0D;
not only understands the management countermeasures, but also studies the
development trend of preschool education. In this &#x0D; paper, literature
analysis method is adopted to process the collected educational management
literature, and content analysis &#x0D; method is used to express the analysis
results in the form of charts.
