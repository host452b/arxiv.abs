Title: Sleep Duration and Visceral Adipose Tissue: Linear and Nonlinear Mendelian Randomization Analyses
Authors: Yuefeng Yu, Yingchao Chen, Haojie Zhang, Sizhi Ai, Jihui Zhang
ID: openalex.W4296619086
URL: https://doi.org/10.1210/clinem/dgac551

The causal association of sleep duration with VAT was L-type. Our findings
support that short sleep duration is a risk factor for increasing VAT, thus
reinforcing the probability that increasing sleep duration may decrease VAT.
