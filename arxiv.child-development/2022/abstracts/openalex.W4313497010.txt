Title: Research on Kindergarten Curriculum Strategy with Red Cultural Resources
Authors: Long Wang, Chun Wang, Xueli Wang
ID: openalex.W4313497010
URL: http://dx.doi.org/10.23977/curtm.2022.051513

The social culture of our country is gradually diversified, and the alienation
between red education and children is intensifying. Preschool period is the key
period for the formation of children's moral quality. The integration of red
cultural resources into kindergarten curriculum is not only conducive to
enriching kindergarten curriculum types, but also conducive to cultivating
children's patriotic consciousness and enhancing children's cultural self-
confidence.
