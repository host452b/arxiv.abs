Title: Household composition after resettlement and emotional health in adolescent migrants
Authors: Manuela Orjuela‐Grimm, Maria Marti‐Castaner, Silvia Bhatt-Carreño, Maria António Castro, Alexandra Restrepo
ID: openalex.W4223995529
URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jmh.2022.100103

The needs and coping abilities of UAM migrants vary with the composition of
their immediate receiving environment, their post-resettlement household.
Understanding differences associated with these household characteristics can
guide interventions to maximize emotional health and resilience.
