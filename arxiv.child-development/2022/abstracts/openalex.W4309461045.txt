Title: Factors Associated with the Duration of Breastfeeding: The Practices of Mexican Mothers in a Megacity and in the Agricultural Town

ID: openalex.W4309461045

The agricultural population had a higher risk of the premature interruption of
EBF/TBF and the early introduction of BMSs and CF. Protective factors were
family-friendly environments and being born in a baby-friendly hospital.
