Title: Is adherence to the Mediterranean diet associated with good sleep duration in primary-school children?
Authors: Alessandra Buja, Giulia Grotto, Chiara Zampieri, Simona Fortunata Mafrici, Claudia Cozzolino
ID: openalex.W4308006847
URL: https://doi.org/10.3389/fped.2022.959643

Ensuring an adequate sleep duration may be an important strategy for enhancing
adherence to the MD. Sleep and dietary education should be included in future
health promotion programs.
