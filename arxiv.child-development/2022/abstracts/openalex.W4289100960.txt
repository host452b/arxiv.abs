Title: Quality of life in childhood advanced cancer: from conceptualization to assessment with the Advance QoL tool
Authors: Josianne Avoine-Blondin, Émilie Dumont, Marc‐Antoine Marquis, Michel Duval, Serge Sultan
ID: openalex.W4289100960
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/s12904-022-01025-z

Advance QoL is an innovative tool targeting key life domains in childhood
advanced cancer. It is focused on preserved abilities and targets of care. The
refined version is appropriate for adult respondents within families and
professionals. Future studies will develop versions for young ages to collect
the experience of patients themselves. This will open on future reliability,
validity, sensitivity, and implementation studies.
