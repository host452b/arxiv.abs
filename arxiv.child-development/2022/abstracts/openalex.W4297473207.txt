Title: “It Is a Big Spider Web of Things”: Sensory Experiences of Autistic Adults in Public Spaces
Authors: Keren MacLennan, Catherine S. Woolley, Emily andsensory, Brett Heasman, Jess Starns
ID: openalex.W4297473207
URL: https://doi.org/10.1089/aut.2022.0024

Our findings have important implications for businesses, policy, and built
environment designers to reduce the sensory impact of public places to make them
more enabling for autistic people. By making public spaces more enabling, we may
be able to improve quality of life for autistic individuals.
