Title: The individual and joint associations of depression and sleep duration with cardiometabolic diseases and mortality: A prospective cohort study
Authors: Xunjie Cheng, Feiyun Ouyang, Tianqi Ma, Lingfang He, Li Gong
ID: openalex.W4303859157
URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.atherosclerosis.2022.09.016

In this study, depression and short or long sleep duration were independently
associated with an increased risk of CMDs and mortality. We also observed
significant additive interactions between depression and short sleep duration in
relation to all-cause mortality and CHD.
