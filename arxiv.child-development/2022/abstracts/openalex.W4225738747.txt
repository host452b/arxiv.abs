Title: Participation of adolescents from the Quilombola community in the creation of an educational game about alcohol consumption
Authors: Adriana Nunes Moraes Partelli, Marta Pereira Coelho, Séfora Gasparini Santos, Isabela Lorencini Santos, Ivone Evangelista Cabral
ID: openalex.W4225738747
URL: https://doi.org/10.1590/1980-220x-reeusp-2021-0402

The active and dynamic participation of adolescents took place from conception
to evaluation of the board game, encouraging them to reflect on a community
context of cultural permissiveness of alcohol use.
