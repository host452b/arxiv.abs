Title: Predictors for the duration of breastfeeding among ethiopia women of childbearing age with babies; application of accelerate failure time and parametric shared frailty models
Authors: Getahun Mulugeta, Dagne Tesfaye, Awoke Seyoum Tegegne
ID: openalex.W4296622713
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/s40795-022-00601-z

Residence area, level of education, age of the child, smoking status of women,
and birth interval of successive birth significantly affected the duration of
breastfeeding in the current investigation. Hence, the health staff should
conduct health-related education for young women, educated women, urban women,
smoker women, and women with a shorter interval of birth to increase the women's
attitude and awareness towards the use of long-duration of breastfeeding.
