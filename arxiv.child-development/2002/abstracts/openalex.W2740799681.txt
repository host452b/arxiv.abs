Title: Learning from Others: Literacy Perspectives of Middle-School English Teachers
Authors: Cynthia A. Lassonde
ID: openalex.W2740799681
URL: https://doi.org/10.4148/2470-6353.1190

At the center of this interview-based study, middle-school English teachers talk
and write about their literacy development, their teaching philosophies, and
their curriculum, as they reflect upon their teaching. Portraits of their
perspectives of literacy emerge. Using these portraits to reflect upon teaching
practices, the author suggests we can effectively examine our own educational
philosophies, contributing to our efforts to become increasingly competent
educators.
