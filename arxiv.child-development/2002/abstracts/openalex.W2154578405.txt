Title: Hypertension and blood pressure among meat eaters, fish eaters, vegetarians and vegans in EPIC–Oxford
Authors: Paul N. Appleby, Gwyneth K. Davey, Timothy J. Key
ID: openalex.W2154578405
URL: https://doi.org/10.1079/phn2002332

Non-meat eaters, especially vegans, have a lower prevalence of hypertension and
lower systolic and diastolic blood pressures than meat eaters, largely because
of differences in body mass index.
