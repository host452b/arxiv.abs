Title: Identity matters
Authors: Sarah J. McCarthey, Elizabeth Birr Moje
ID: openalex.W4249874905
URL: https://doi.org/10.1598/rrq.37.2.6

In a conversation represented here in print, the authors ask why literacy
theorists, researchers, and teachers should care about how readers' identities
are constructed, represented, and performed during acts of reading. They share
the believe that such questions are central to literacy theory and pedagogy.
