Title: The myth of the dumb Puerto Rican : circular migration and language struggle in Puerto Rico
Authors: Erna Kerkhof
ID: openalex.W1981066292
URL: https://doi.org/10.1163/13822373-90002553

Focuses on the character with which the link between language and identity has
become invested in Puerto Rico, against the backdrop of migration and education.
Author describes the efforts on the part of some of today's politicians and
cultural elites to inculcate a 'historical myth' that revolves around the
detrimental effect that contact with the English language is assumed to have on
the mastery of Spanish, and on 'Puerto Rican identity'. She concludes with an
estimate of the general effect of the language struggle on Puerto Rican
identity.
