Title: Sex, gender identity and women’s health research and equality: An urgent need for clarity of language and accurate data collection
Authors: Karleen Gribble, Leonie Callaway, Melissa Raven, Julie Smith, Jenny Gamble
ID: openalex.W4405971528
URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.wombi.2024.101854

Researchers and clinicians globally must urgently participate in policy
discussion regarding the importance of sexed language and accurate sex data, to
protect individual and population health and data and research integrity.
