Title: The Power of Public Speaking for Social Impact
Authors: Anna Neya Kazanskaia, Anna Kazanskaia
ID: openalex.W4413241582
URL: https://doi.org/10.64357/public-speaking-social-impact-2025

This book explores the transformative role of public speaking in advancing
social impact initiatives. Dr. Anna Neya Kazanskaia demonstrates how effective
communication can inspire action, mobilize resources, and influence policy.
Through a combination of theory, practical techniques, and real-world case
studies, the book guides non-profit leaders, activists, educators, and social
entrepreneurs in mastering the art of impactful public speaking. It covers
critical elements such as message clarity, audience engagement, persuasive
delivery, and the use of storytelling to foster emotional connection. Special
attention is given to adapting communication styles for diverse cultural
contexts and platforms — from live audiences to digital channels. The book also
examines the ethical responsibilities of public speakers in the social sector,
including authenticity, transparency, and respectful dialogue. By integrating
practical exercises, checklists, and performance tips, The Power of Public
Speaking for Social Impact equips readers with the tools to become confident,
credible, and inspiring voices for change.
