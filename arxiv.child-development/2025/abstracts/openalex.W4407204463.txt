Title: Dance and stress regulation: A multidisciplinary narrative review
Authors: Sandra Klaperski, Jonathan Skinner, Jolanta Opacka‐Juffry, Kristina Pfeffer
ID: openalex.W4407204463
URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.psychsport.2025.102823

This review is the first to integrate perspectives from different disciplines on
the stress regulatory effects of dance. It shows that dance has a large
potential to aid coping and resilience at multiple levels of the human
experience. At the same time, we identified that the existing evidence is often
still limited by a narrow focus on exercise characteristics such as intensity
levels. This hinders a more holistic understanding of underlying stress
regulatory mechanisms and provides important directions for future research.
