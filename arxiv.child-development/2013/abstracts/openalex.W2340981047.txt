Title: Pedagogy as Ritual: How Teachers use Rituals to foster their Mythical values in the Early Childhood Classrooms?
Authors: Grace 劉慧中 Lau, Kwok Keung Ho
ID: openalex.W2340981047
URL: https://doi.org/10.12681/hjre.8795

A lot of literature has been written about education as the transmission of
cultural knowledge yet the way in which culture, as ritual action, constitutes
and fosters religio-cultural ideological myth remains largely unexplored in the
context of ritual pedagogies in school settings. In Hong Kong, the government
had initiated an educational reform promoting the play-based child-centred
approach to replace the traditional mode of teacher-centred approach aimed at
meeting the changing world at the start of 21st century.
