Title: Migrant background and ethnic minority status as predictors for duration of untreated psychosis
Authors: Mari Nerhus, Akiah Ottesen Berg, Marit Haram, Levi R. Kvitland, Ole A. Andreassen
ID: openalex.W1603907661
URL: https://doi.org/10.1111/eip.12106

Age at migration has a moderate, but statistically significant effect on DUP.
The findings indicate migrating after school start is associated with a longer
DUP in immigrant populations.
