Title: An exploratory study of the potential learning benefits for medical students in collaborative drawing: creativity, reflection and ‘critical looking’
Authors: Philippa Lyon, Patrick Letschka, Tom Ainsworth, Inam Haq
ID: openalex.W2171157905
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/1472-6920-13-86

This preliminary study suggests, through the themes identified, that there may
be potential learning outcomes for medical students in this model of
interdisciplinary collaborative drawing of the human body. Further research is
needed to explore their applicability and value to medical education. There is a
need to explore in more depth the beliefs, motivations and learning styles of
medical students opting for the module, the significance and weighting of
different learning and teaching elements in the module and the impact of the
learning on medical students in the immediate post-module phase.
