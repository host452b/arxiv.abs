Title: Characterizing lunch meals served and consumed by pre-school children in Head Start
Authors: Theresa A. Nicklas, Yan Liu, Janice E. Stuff, Jennifer O. Fisher, Jason A. Mendoza
ID: openalex.W2130173827
URL: https://doi.org/10.1017/s1368980013001377

Plate waste and variation in amounts served and consumed were substantial;
amounts served were associated with amounts consumed. Large portion sizes may
contribute to paediatric obesity by promoting excessive intake at meals.
Understanding factors influencing portion sizes provides insight about specific
intervention strategies that can be used in obesity prevention programmes.
