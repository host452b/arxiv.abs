Title: ‘Don’t get me wrong, it’s fun here, but …: ambivalence and paradox in a ‘fun’ work environment
Authors: Sam Warren, Stephen Fineman
ID: openalex.W2916364835
URL: https://doi.org/10.4324/9780203390191-13

A number of books written within the last few years have been telling employers
and employees to have fun at work. Whether it is throwing a ﬁ sh, squirting each
other with water guns, or throwing a pie in the CEO’s face. A problem with a
number of these ‘fun’ books is that they do not deﬁ ne what fun is before
letting the dogs loose. Rule number one is to clearly explain to the employees
what fun is and what it is not.
