Title: Creative Arts: An Essential Element in the Teacher’s Toolkit When Developing Critical Thinking in Children
Authors: C. Nilson, Catherine Fetherston, Anne McMurray, Tony Fetherston
ID: openalex.W2127959553
URL: https://doi.org/10.14221/ajte.2013v38n7.4

This paper is a position paper, which argues the position that critical thinking
is a crucial skill, which needs to be developed in the school curriculum and
that the creative arts can do this. The paper explores the states of the Arts in
the present curriculum and goes on to argue that knowing how to develop critical
thinking is an important pedagogical skill that needs to be developed in our
pre-service teachers. This position is supported through data gathered from an
innovative project that explored teachers’ and mothers’ perceptions of
children’s critical thinking.
