Title: Longer Sleep – Slimmer Kids: The ENERGY-Project
Authors: Teatske M. Altenburg, Mai J. M. Chinapaw, Elise T. W. van der Knaap, Johannes Brug, Yannis Μanios
ID: openalex.W2041042603
URL: https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0059522

Weekday sleep duration seems more strongly associated with body composition in
European school children than weekend day sleep duration. Promoting adequate
sleep duration may contribute to healthy weight in children.
