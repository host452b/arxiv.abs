Title: Relationship of wisdom and ego-identity for Korean and American adolescents
Authors: Hyeyoung Bang
ID: openalex.W2305389351
URL: http://hdl.handle.net/11244/7303

Scope and Method of Study: Wisdom is considered to be a strong predictor or
determinant of well-being (Ardelt, 2003; Bianchi, 1994; Erikson et al., 1986) as
well as the highest human achievement that makes a difference in a person's life
(Kramer, 2000; Kunzmann, 2004). Even though some scholars have shown that
adolescents may have developmental potential for wisdom (Piaget &amp; Inhelder,
1973; Sternberg, 1998), there is a need to know the direct relationship, and
cross-cultural effects.
