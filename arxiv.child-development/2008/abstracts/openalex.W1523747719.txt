Title: Infant feeding practices and beliefs among women in Podor, West Africa
Authors: Heather Lyn Schwartz
ID: openalex.W1523747719
URL: https://doi.org/10.31979/etd.b56n-xja8

This thesis determines and describes infant feeding practices and beliefs
amongst women in Podor, West Africa. Structured interviews were used to gather
data pertaining to demographics, breastfeeding, complementary feeding, and
weaning practices and beliefs. The aim of this thesis was to assemble baseline
data from which an infant feeding education intervention program could be built.
