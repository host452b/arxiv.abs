Title: Bullying perspectives among rural youth: a mixed methods approach
Authors: Judith C. Kulig, Barry Hall, Ruth Grant Kalischuk
ID: openalex.W2153932618
URL: https://doi.org/10.22605/rrh923

Rural communities will benefit as a whole if bullying, an important societal
concern, is addressed. Building on the social structure of rural communities is
important, However, listening to rural youth themselves is the key if true
change is to be implemented.
