Title: Supporting the wellbeing of caregivers of children on the autism spectrum: A qualitative report on experiences of attending group dance movement psychotherapy
Authors: Supritha Aithal, Vicky Karkou, Stergios Makris, Themis Karaminis, Joanne Powell
ID: openalex.W4385568609
URL: https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0288626

Caregivers talked about their experience of participating in the DMP groups as
positive and acknowledged the helpful and challenging aspects of taking part in
DMP intervention. They appreciated the creative and expressive nature of the
intervention to promote their emotional and social wellbeing. The challenges
identified in the study indicate that further awareness is needed within school
environments about the contribution arts therapies can make towards establishing
appropriate and sustainable interventions for caregivers.
