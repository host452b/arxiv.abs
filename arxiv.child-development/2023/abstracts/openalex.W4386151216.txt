Title: Rapid infant weight gain or point‐in‐time weight status: Which is the best predictor of later obesity and body composition?
Authors: Rachael W. Taylor, Jillian J. Haszard, Kim Meredith‐Jones, Anne‐Louise M. Heath, Barbara C. Galland
ID: openalex.W4386151216
URL: https://doi.org/10.1002/oby.23861

A simple measure of obesity status at a single time point between 6 and 24
months of age appeared a stronger risk factor for later obesity and FMI than
RIWG assessed by any indicator, over any time frame.
