Title: The Effect of Socioeconomic Factors on Maternal Perinatal Depressive Symptoms and the Contribution of Group Prenatal Support as a Preventive Measure
Authors: Κωνσταντίνα Νάτσιου, Sokratis Karaoulanis, Konstantinos Dafopoulos, Antonis Garas, Konstantinos Bonotis
ID: openalex.W4317938258
URL: https://doi.org/10.1089/whr.2022.0042

Although financial status as well as depression continued to play a role, the
deterrent contribution of psychoprophylaxis was the most important parameter in
the final maternity blues prognostic model. The results of our study show a
potential for prevention and suggest interesting hypotheses for future
interventions.
