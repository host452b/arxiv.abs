Title: Leadership in Turbulent Times
Authors: Gaëtane Jean‐Marie, T. B. Jones
ID: openalex.W2895319770
URL: https://doi.org/10.1108/9781837534944

The second of two volumes, Leadership in Turbulent Times draws upon cutting edge
theories and evidence-based strategies, integrating conceptual and empirical
work addressing higher educational leadership in these unprecedented and
turbulent times with a particular focus on cultivating diversity and inclusion.
