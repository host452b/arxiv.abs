Title: <scp>Preece–Baines</scp> 1 model validation for cross‐sectional data in male soccer players according to maturity status
Authors: Luis Alberto Flores Olivares, Isabel Fragoso
ID: openalex.W4386251272
URL: https://doi.org/10.1002/ajhb.23980

PB1 adjusted the height growth of Portuguese male soccer players from cross-
sectional data, obtaining an estimate PHV very similar to that found from
longitudinal data. A maturity time difference of ≈0.6 years was found between
the age at PHV of on-time, early, and on-time and late maturity state.
