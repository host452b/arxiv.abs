Title: “That Weird Kid Without Parents”: A Qualitative Analysis of Identity Following Bereavement due to Parental Intimate Partner Homicide in Australia
Authors: Oliver Eastwood, Rowena Conroy, Kathryn Joy, Katitza Marinkovic Chavez, John Devaney
ID: openalex.W4388230723
URL: https://doi.org/10.31234/osf.io/9ew2m

This qualitative study highlights how people bereaved due to parental intimate
partner homicide relate to themselves and the world in meaningful and trauma-
specific ways.
