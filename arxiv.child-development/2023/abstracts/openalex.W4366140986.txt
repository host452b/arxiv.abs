Title: A correlational study of breastfeeding duration among Saudi mothers: The role of self-efficacy, intention, and social support
Authors: Nourah M Al Naseeb, Hanan Badr, Salmah Alghamdi
ID: openalex.W4366140986
URL: https://doi.org/10.33546/bnj.2510

Breastfeeding intention was found to have a more significant impact on
breastfeeding duration than self-efficacy and social support. These results can
inform nurses and midwives in supporting breastfeeding mothers by providing them
with the necessary information and increasing their awareness of breastfeeding-
related factors.
