Title: Sleep duration, sleep quality, and weapon carrying in a sample of adolescents from Texas
Authors: Meghan L. Royle, Eric J. Connolly, Sara Nowakowski, Jeff R. Temple
ID: openalex.W4386170149
URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.pmedr.2023.102385

Our findings align with previous research demonstrating that sleeping 4 or less
hours increases the likelihood of adolescent weapon carrying. Restless sleep
appears to play less of a role. Future research should elucidate the
longitudinal pathways between sleep duration, sleep quality, and forms of
adolescent weapon carrying.
