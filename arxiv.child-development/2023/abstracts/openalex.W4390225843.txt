Title: ESTUDIANTE DE MEDICINA: SUS IDEAS Y ESCRITURA
Authors: José Ramón Olivo Estrada, Carmelina Montaño Torres, Bernabé Ríos Nava
ID: openalex.W4390225843
URL: http://doi.org/10.47172/sfp2020.ed.00080

The book 'Technologies Aimed at Health' edited and published by South Florida
Publishing Company brings together five chapters that are relevant to the
context of health. The e-book aims to enlighten the audience on all research
dealing with health education, pedagogy, development, and new advances in
health. It provides an insight into current trends in research in health. The
chapters are written in English and Spanish.
