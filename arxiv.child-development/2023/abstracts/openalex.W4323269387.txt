Title: Criteria for the Development of the Aesthetic Culture of a Future Teacher of Fine Arts
Authors: M. Zh. Ordasheva, V. V. Bezhina, N. A. Matveyeva
ID: openalex.W4323269387
URL: http://dx.doi.org/10.36941/jesr-2023-0035

The purpose of the article is to consider the specifics of the formation of the
professional and aesthetic readiness of future teachers of fine arts. The
empirical study involved 94 students of 1-4 years studying in the specialties
"Technology and Fine Arts" and "Fine Arts and Drawing" in universities in Russia
and Kazakhstan. Empirical research has revealed the predominance of algorithmic
and stimulating-productive levels that are not characterized by an independent
creative nature of activity, as well as the successful conscious and systematic
application of analysis and assessment in new situations.&#x0D; &#x0D; Received:
11 September 2022 / Accepted: 17 February 2022 / Published: 5 March 2023
