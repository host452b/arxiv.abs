Title: Screen use and sleep duration and quality at 15 years old: Cohort study
Authors: Priscila Echevarria, Bianca Del‐Ponte, Luciana Tovo‐Rodrigues, Alícia Matijasevich, Camila S. Halal
ID: openalex.W4377232020
URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.sleepx.2023.100073

The median time spent using screens was longer than recommended. Screen use for
≥6hs/24hs was associated with a shorter sleep duration, and ≥9hs/24hs with poor
sleep quality.
