Title: Sharing of cultural values and heritage through storytelling in the digital age
Authors: Çağın Zort, Esra Karabacak, Şevket Öznur, Gökmen Dağlı
ID: openalex.W4321453225
URL: https://doi.org/10.3389/fpsyg.2023.1104121

This research sheds a light to indicate the importance of the storytelling
approach for sharing cultural values and heritage. It is significant to address
the merits of technology in transferring cultural values and heritage. In
addition to this, this study is limited to one specific context that can be
further explored as a cross-cultural analysis.
