Title: Effects of sleep pattern, duration, and quality on premenstrual syndrome and primary dysmenorrhea in korean high school girls
Authors: Daye Jeong, H. K. Lee, Jaehee Kim
ID: openalex.W4386224320
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/s12905-023-02600-z

Our study result heightens the importance of healthy sleep hygiene, especially
sleep quality in the management of premenstrual syndrome and dysmenorrhea in
adolescent girls.
