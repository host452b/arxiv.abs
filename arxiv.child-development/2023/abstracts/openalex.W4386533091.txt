Title: Looking through the lens: a photovoice study examining access to services for newcomer children
Authors: Nahal Fakhari, Jessie‐Lee D. McIsaac, Rebecca Feicht, Sarah Reddington, Susan M. Brigham
ID: openalex.W4386533091
URL: https://doi.org/10.1080/17482631.2023.2255176

This research illustrates the lived experiences of newcomer families and
identifies opportunities to address inequities, improve early childhood
programmes, and enhance families' access to programmes and services.
