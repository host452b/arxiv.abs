Title: Screen time is negatively associated with sleep quality and duration only in insufficiently active adolescents: A Brazilian cross-sectional school-based study
Authors: Amanda B. Santos, Wagner Luiz do Prado, William R. Tebar, Jared Ingles, Gérson Ferrari
ID: openalex.W4390202032
URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.pmedr.2023.102579

High screen time was associated with poor sleep quality and insufficient sleep
duration only in insufficiently active adolescents. These results suggest that
high PA levels may contribute to improving sleep patterns in pediatric
population.
