Title: Gender Nonconforming Boys in Picture Books: Using Protagonists to Examine Social Roles and Stereotypes
Authors: David Campos
ID: openalex.W4380989456
URL: https://doi.org/10.5860/cal.21.2.14

As I was exploring the children’s literature section at my community library, I
spotted the picture book Williams’ Doll by Charlotte Zolotow (1972) standing
upright on a bookshelf. I picked it up and reminisced about reading it to my
students when I taught second grade in the early nineties. At that time, I
noticed a pattern in some of my students’ conversations that caused me concern
because of some of their comments.
