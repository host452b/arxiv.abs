Title: Building Community and Identity Online: A Content Analysis of Highly Viewed #Autism TikTok Videos
Authors: Daniel Gilmore, Deondray Radford, Meghan K. Haas, Morgan C. Shields, Lauren Bishop
ID: openalex.W4383217638
URL: https://doi.org/10.1089/aut.2023.0019

Highly viewed #autism TikTok videos primarily share autistic people's individual
experiences rather than providing general education about autism. However,
autistic Black, Indigenous, and people of color may have difficulty finding
others that represent them along both disability and racial dimensions. Future
research should investigate autistic people's motivations for using TikTok, and
how #autism content shapes social discourse about autism.
