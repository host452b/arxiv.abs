Title: Influence of socio-economic factors on emotional changes during the postnatal period
Authors: Katarzyna Wszołek, Ewa Żak, Joanna Żurawska, Jolanta Olszewska, Beata Pięta
ID: openalex.W2623874455
URL: https://doi.org/10.26444/aaem/74486

Women who demonstrate warning symptoms should be screened for postnatal
emotional changes and mood swings during their hospitalization after delivery.
EPDS seems to be a suitable tool for early detection of emotional disturbances.
