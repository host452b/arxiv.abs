Title: Tell Us About It: Children's Expressive Narratives About Head Start
Authors: Alan Hay
ID: openalex.W4236794638
URL: https://doi.org/10.22215/etd/2017-12003

The present study aims to examine children's experiences in childcare programs
that serve at-risk populations in the city of Ottawa. To achieve this goal, we
engaged children in art-based activities and conversations about their own
experiences at childcare. Twelve childcare centres agreed to participate in this
study: ten municipally funded Head Start programs and two not-for-profit
programs. This study employed artbased methods to involve children in semi-
structured discussions about childcare.
