Title: A review of transition experiences in perinatally and behaviourally acquired HIV‐1 infection; same, same but different?
Authors: Phung Khanh Lam, Sarah Fidler, Caroline Foster
ID: openalex.W2614411843
URL: https://doi.org/10.7448/ias.20.4.21506

Published transition data remain limited, and there are few studies focusing on
behaviourally infected adolescents and key population groups (e.g. adolescents
who use drugs, lesbian/gay/transgender individuals). Robust definitions of the
transition process and standardized outcome measures are required to facilitate
cross-study and geographic comparisons.
