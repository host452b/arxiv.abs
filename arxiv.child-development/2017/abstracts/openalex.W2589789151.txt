Title: Home palliative care works: but how? A meta-ethnography of the experiences of patients and family caregivers
Authors: Vera P Sarmento, Marjolein Gysels, Irene J Higginson, Bárbara Gomes
ID: openalex.W2589789151
URL: https://doi.org/10.1136/bmjspcare-2016-001141

Home palliative care teams improve patients and caregivers experience of
security when facing life-limiting illnesses at home, by providing competent
care and being present. These teams should therefore be widely available and
empowered with the resources to be present and provide competent care.
