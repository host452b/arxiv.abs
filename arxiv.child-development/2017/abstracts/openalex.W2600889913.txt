Title: The concomitants of conspiracy concerns
Authors: Daniel Freeman, Richard P. Bentall
ID: openalex.W2600889913
URL: https://doi.org/10.1007/s00127-017-1354-4

Viewing conspiracies in the world is associated with a raised risk of a wide
range of adverse circumstances. It is a type of cognitive style that requires
systematic empirical study, including monitoring of prevalence, tests of
causation, and modelling of propagation.
