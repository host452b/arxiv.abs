Title: Arts Integration Research Through the Lens of the Every Student Succeeds Act
Authors: Meredith Ludwig, Andrea Boyle
ID: openalex.W4401907713
URL: https://doi.org/10.59656/a-ya6181.001

This report scours research on activities that use arts to improve student
achievement—an approach known as arts integration—and identifies those that
qualify for federal education funding.
