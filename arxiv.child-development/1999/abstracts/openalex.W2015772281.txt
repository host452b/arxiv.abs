Title: Thoughts about rates of breastfeeding
Authors: Tahire Koctürk, Rolf Zetterström
ID: openalex.W2015772281
URL: https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1651-2227.1999.tb01119.x

Socio‐economic and other factors which influence the duration of breastfeeding
have been reviewed. We agree with the conclusion of WHO's multinational study
that breastfeeding is a biological type of behaviour with deep roots in human
societies, although there are great variations across settings.
