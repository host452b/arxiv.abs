Title: Specifying the antecedents of breast-feeding duration in Peru through a structural equation model
Authors: Rafael Pérez‐Escamilla, José A. Cobas, Héctor Balcázar, Mary Holland Benin
ID: openalex.W2116037727
URL: https://doi.org/10.1017/s1368980099000646

These results imply that an unplanned pregnancy, a delayed breast-feeding
initiation, and higher socioeconomic status are risk factors for an earlier
