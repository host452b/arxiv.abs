Title: Differences in duration of Huntington's disease based on age at onset
Authors: Tatiana M. Foroud, Jacqueline S. Gray, Julia Ivashina, P.M. Conneally
ID: openalex.W2110807036
URL: https://doi.org/10.1136/jnnp.66.1.52

Duration of disease is influenced by the age at symptom onset with juvenile and
late onset patients having the shortest duration.
