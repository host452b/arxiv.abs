Title: Spectacle in international criminal law: the fundraising image of victimhood
Authors: Christine Schwöbel‐Patel
ID: openalex.W2466247986
URL: https://doi.org/10.1093/lril/lrw014

This paper explores the relationship between spectacle and law as it unfolds in
international criminal law. The spectacularised construction of the victim of
international crime as a replica of the familiar fundraising image of victimhood
serves as a critical lens into narratives of international criminality and its
seeming antithesis of humanitarianism.
