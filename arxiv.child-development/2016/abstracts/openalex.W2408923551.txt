Title: Willingness to Communicate in English among Trainee Teachers in a Malaysian Private University
Authors: Ahmed Fahim, Mogana Dhamotharan
ID: openalex.W2408923551
URL: https://doi.org/10.3844/jssp.2016.105.112

Willingness to Communicate (WTC) in English has become a field of interest since
the construct was introduced by McCroskey and Baer in 1985. This study,
investigated 230 trainee teachers' Willingness to Communicate in English in
terms of gender, nationality, ethnic group, course and years of study at the
Faculty of Education in a Malaysian private university.
