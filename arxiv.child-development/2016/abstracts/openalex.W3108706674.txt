Title: Do Hindi Cinema &amp; Television Serials Propagate Caste Stereotypes through Surnames of Characters: A Content Analysis
Authors: Rakesh K. Maurya
ID: openalex.W3108706674
URL: https://doi.org/10.25215/0303.055

Caste affiliated surnames are very common in India. In fact, they convey a lot
of caste stereotypes about the bearer. This is a subtle way of practicing caste
based identities and related discriminations. The current study is a content
analysis of the surnames of leading fictional characters in Hindi TV serials and
movies to see if TV and cinema propagate caste stereotypes through surnames of
their fictional characters. The result shows excessive use of upper caste
affiliated surnames used both in TV serials and cinema while surnames affiliated
to lower castes have negligible presence in these programmes.
