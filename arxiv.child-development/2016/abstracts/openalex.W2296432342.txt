Title: Sleep duration and risk of obesity among a sample of Victorian school children
Authors: Bridget Morrissey, Mary Malakellis, Jillian Whelan, Lynne Millar, Boyd Swinburn
ID: openalex.W2296432342
URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/s12889-016-2913-4

The strong positive relationship between weight status and sleep deprivation
merits further research though PA and sedentary time do not seem to be involved
in the relationship. Strategies to improve sleep duration may help obesity
prevention initiatives in the future.
