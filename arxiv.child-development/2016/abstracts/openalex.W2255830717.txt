Title: Effect of Pattern and Duration of Breastfeeding on the Consumption of Fruits and Vegetables among Preschool Children
Authors: Betina Soldateli, Álvaro Vigo, Elsa Regina Justo Giugliani
ID: openalex.W2255830717
URL: https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0148357

Longer duration of breastfeeding was positively associated with consumption of
vegetables in children aged 4-7 years; however, there was no association with
consumption of fruits. Exclusive breastfeeding duration did not have influence
on consumption of fruits or vegetables.
