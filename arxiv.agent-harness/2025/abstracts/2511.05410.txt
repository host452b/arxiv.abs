Title: Story Arena: A Multi-Agent Environment for Envisioning the Future of Software Engineering
Authors: Justin D. Weisz, Michael Muller, Kush R. Varshney
ID: 2511.05410
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.05410

What better way to understand the impact of AI on software engineering than to
ask AI itself? We constructed Story Arena, a multi-agent "writer's room" in
which multiple AI agents, independently imbued with a position statement on the
future of software engineering, converse with each other to develop a shared
vision. They then use this shared vision to collaboratively construct a design
fiction that depicts this vision in narrative form. We present "The Code of
Trust," a short fiction that investigates themes of human comprehension, trust,
content ownership, augmentation vs. replacement, and uncertain futures in human-
AI co-creation.
