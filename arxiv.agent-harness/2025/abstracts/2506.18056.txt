Title: Weighted Assumption Based Argumentation to reason about ethical principles and actions
Authors: Paolo Baldi, Fabio Aurelio D'Asaro, Abeer Dyoub, Francesca Alessandra Lisi
ID: 2506.18056
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.18056

Abstract:
We augment Assumption Based Argumentation (ABA for short) with weighted
argumentation. In a nutshell, we assign weights to arguments and then derive the
weight of attacks between ABA arguments. We illustrate our proposal through
running examples in the field of ethical reasoning, and present an
implementation based on Answer Set Programming.
