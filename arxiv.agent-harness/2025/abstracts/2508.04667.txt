Title: How are CS students using resources and AI tools for coding tasks?
Authors: Natalia Echeverry, Arun Lekshmi Narayanan
ID: 2508.04667
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.04667

Abstract:
A survey of 26 CS students reveals that AI coding assistants are mainly used for
writing code (second to online searches) while AI chatbots are the top resource
for debugging. Participants with different coding experience prefer online help
over direct human help from peers and instructors.
