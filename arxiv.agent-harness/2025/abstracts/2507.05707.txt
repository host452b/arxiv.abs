Title: Agentic-R1: Distilled Dual-Strategy Reasoning
Authors: Weihua Du, Pranjal Aggarwal, Sean Welleck, Yiming Yang
ID: 2507.05707
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.05707

Abstract:
Current long chain-of-thought (long-CoT) models excel at mathematical reasoning
but rely on slow and error-prone natural language traces. Tool-augmented agents
address arithmetic via code execution, but often falter on complex logical
tasks. We introduce a fine-tuning framework, DualDistill, that distills
complementary reasoning strategies from multiple teachers into a unified student
model. Using this approach, we train Agentic-R1, which dynamically selects the
optimal strategy for each query, invoking tools for arithmetic and algorithmic
problems, and using text-based reasoning for abstract ones. Our method improves
accuracy across a range of tasks, including both computation-intensive and
standard benchmarks, demonstrating the effectiveness of multi-strategy
distillation in achieving robust and efficient reasoning. Our project is
available at https://github.com/StigLidu/DualDistill
