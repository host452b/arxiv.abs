Title: A Vision for Auto Research with LLM Agents
Authors: Chengwei Liu, Chong Wang, Jiayue Cao, Jingquan Ge, Kun Wang et al.
ID: 2504.18765
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.18765

Abstract:
This paper introduces Agent-Based Auto Research, a structured multi-agent
framework designed to automate, coordinate, and optimize the full lifecycle of
scientific research. Leveraging the capabilities of large language models (LLMs)
and modular agent collaboration, the system spans all major research phases,
including literature review, ideation, methodology planning, experimentation,
paper writing, peer review response, and dissemination. By addressing issues
such as fragmented workflows, uneven methodological expertise, and cognitive
overload, the framework offers a systematic and scalable approach to scientific
inquiry. Preliminary explorations demonstrate the feasibility and potential of
Auto Research as a promising paradigm for self-improving, AI-driven research
processes.
