Title: GeoFlow: Agentic Workflow Automation for Geospatial Tasks
Authors: Amulya Bhattaram, Justin Chung, Stanley Chung, Ranit Gupta, Janani Ramamoorthy et al.
ID: 2508.04719
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.04719

Abstract:
We present GeoFlow, a method that automatically generates agentic workflows for
geospatial tasks. Unlike prior work that focuses on reasoning decomposition and
leaves API selection implicit, our method provides each agent with detailed
tool-calling objectives to guide geospatial API invocation at runtime. GeoFlow
increases agentic success by 6.8% and reduces token usage by up to fourfold
across major LLM families compared to state-of-the-art approaches.
