Title: Simulating Influence Dynamics with LLM Agents
Authors: Mehwish Nasim, Syed Muslim Gilani, Amin Qasmi, Usman Naseem
ID: 2503.08709
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.08709

Abstract:
This paper introduces a simulator designed for opinion dynamics researchers to
model competing influences within social networks in the presence of LLM-based
agents. By integrating established opinion dynamics principles with
state-of-the-art LLMs, this tool enables the study of influence propagation and
counter-misinformation strategies. The simulator is particularly valuable for
researchers in social science, psychology, and operations research, allowing
them to analyse societal phenomena without requiring extensive coding expertise.
Additionally, the simulator will be openly available on GitHub, ensuring
accessibility and adaptability for those who wish to extend its capabilities for
their own research.
