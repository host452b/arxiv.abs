Title: Auctions with LLM Summaries
Authors: Kumar Avinava Dubey, Zhe Feng, Rahul Kidambi, Aranyak Mehta, Di Wang
ID: 2404.08126
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.08126

Abstract:
We study an auction setting in which bidders bid for placement of their content
within a summary generated by a large language model (LLM), e.g., an ad auction
in which the display is a summary paragraph of multiple ads. This generalizes
the classic ad settings such as position auctions to an LLM generated setting,
which allows us to handle general display formats. We propose a novel factorized
framework in which an auction module and an LLM module work together via a
prediction model to provide welfare maximizing summary outputs in an incentive
compatible manner. We provide a theoretical analysis of this framework and
synthetic experiments to demonstrate the feasibility and validity of the system
together with welfare comparisons.
