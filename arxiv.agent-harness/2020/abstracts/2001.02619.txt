Title: D3BA: A Tool for Optimizing Business Processes Using Non-Deterministic Planning
Authors: Tathagata Chakraborti, Yasaman Khazaeni
ID: 2001.02619
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2001.02619

Abstract:
This paper builds upon recent work in the declarative design of dialogue agents
and proposes an exciting new tool -- D3BA -- Declarative Design for Digital
Business Automation, built to optimize business processes using the power of AI
planning. The tool provides a powerful framework to build, optimize, and
maintain complex business processes and optimize them by composing with services
that automate one or more subtasks. We illustrate salient features of this
composition technique, compare with other philosophies of composition, and
highlight exciting opportunities for research in this emerging field of business
process automation.
