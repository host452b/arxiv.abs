Title: Towards Effective Human-AI Collaboration in GUI-Based Interactive Task Learning Agents
Authors: Toby Jia-Jun Li, Jingya Chen, Tom M. Mitchell, Brad A. Myers
ID: 2003.02622
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2003.02622

We argue that a key challenge in enabling usable and useful interactive task
learning for intelligent agents is to facilitate effective Human-AI
collaboration. We reflect on our past 5 years of efforts on designing,
developing and studying the SUGILITE system, discuss the issues on incorporating
recent advances in AI with HCI principles in mixed-initiative interactions and
multi-modal interactions, and summarize the lessons we learned. Lastly, we
identify several challenges and opportunities, and describe our ongoing work
