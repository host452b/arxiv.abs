Title: Research on a Fractional Integral of Fractional Rational Function
Authors: Chii-Huei Yu
ID: zenodo.8168839
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/zenodo.8168839

<p><strong>Abstract:</strong> In this paper, based on Jumarie&rsquo;s modified
Riemann-Liouville (R-L) fractional calculus and a new multiplication of
fractional analytic functions, a fractional integral of fractional rational is
studied. In addition, our result is a generalization of classical calculus
result.</p>  <p><strong>Keywords:</strong> Jumarie&rsquo;s modified R-L
fractional calculus, new multiplication, fractional analytic functions,
fractional integral, fractional rational function.</p>
