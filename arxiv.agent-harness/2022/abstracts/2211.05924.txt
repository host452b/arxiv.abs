Title: Slightly Altruistic Nash Equilibrium for Multi-agent Pursuit-Evasion Games With Input Constraints
Authors: Dongting Li
ID: 2211.05924
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2211.05924

This is an initial manuscript that presents the basic idea of "slightly
altruistic Nash equilibrium", "bi-layer game topology", "rolling horizon target
selection". This manuscript is just used for peer discussion and joint Ph.D.
application affairs rather than submission to any journal. Thus some references
are not all provided. The complete paper for submission will be provided in the
next version.
