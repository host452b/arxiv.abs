Title: An Evaluation of the Archive of Formal Proofs
Authors: Carlin MacKenzie, Jacques Fleuriot, James Vaughan
ID: 2104.01052
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2104.01052

The Archive of Formal Proofs (AFP) is an online repository of formal proofs for
the Isabelle proof assistant. It serves as a central location for publishing,
discovering, and viewing libraries of proofs. We conducted an online survey in
November 2020 to assess the suitability of the website. In this report, we
present and discuss the results, which showed that long-term users of the
website are generally satisfied with the AFP but that there are a number of
areas, such as navigation, search and script browsing, that need improvement.
