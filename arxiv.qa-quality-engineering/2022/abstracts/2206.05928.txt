Title: Compressive Clustering with an Optical Processing Unit
Authors: Luc Giffon, Rémi Gribonval
ID: 2206.05928
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2206.05928

Abstract:
We explore the use of Optical Processing Units (OPU) to compute random Fourier
features for sketching, and adapt the overall compressive clustering pipeline to
this setting. We also propose some tools to help tuning a critical
hyper-parameter of compressive clustering.
