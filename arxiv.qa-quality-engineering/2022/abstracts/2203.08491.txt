Title: Deepchecks: A Library for Testing and Validating Machine Learning Models and Data
Authors: Shir Chorev, Philip Tannor, Dan Ben Israel, Noam Bressler, Itay Gabbay et al.
ID: 2203.08491
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.08491

Abstract:
This paper presents Deepchecks, a Python library for comprehensively validating
machine learning models and data. Our goal is to provide an easy-to-use library
comprising of many checks related to various types of issues, such as model
predictive performance, data integrity, data distribution mismatches, and more.
The package is distributed under the GNU Affero General Public License (AGPL)
and relies on core libraries from the scientific Python ecosystem: scikit-learn,
PyTorch, NumPy, pandas, and SciPy. Source code, documentation, examples, and an
extensive user guide can be found at
\url{https://github.com/deepchecks/deepchecks} and
\url{https://docs.deepchecks.com/}.
