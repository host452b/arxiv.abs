Title: Is this bug severe? A text-cum-graph based model for bug severity prediction
Authors: Rima Hazra, Arpit Dwivedi, Animesh Mukherjee
ID: 2207.00623
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2207.00623

Abstract:
Repositories of large software systems have become commonplace. This massive
expansion has resulted in the emergence of various problems in these software
platforms including identification of (i) bug-prone packages, (ii) critical
bugs, and (iii) severity of bugs. One of the important goals would be to mine
these bugs and recommend them to the developers to resolve them. The first step
to this is that one has to accurately detect the extent of severity of the bugs.
In this paper, we take up this task of predicting the severity of bugs in the
near future. Contextualized neural models built on the text description of a bug
and the user comments about the bug help to achieve reasonably good performance.
Further information on how the bugs are related to each other in terms of the
ways they affect packages can be summarised in the form of a graph and used
along with the text to get additional benefits.
