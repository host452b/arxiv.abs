Title: Enabling Software Defined Optical Networks
Authors: Deven Panchal
ID: 2402.11695
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.11695

Abstract:
This paper gives an overview of Software Defined Optical Networks or SDONs and
how they can be implemented. It traces the evolution of Optical networks upto
GMPLS and traces the idea of SDN and builds upto OpenFlow. The paper explores
the need for SDONs and explains what a SDON solution could look like, including
the hardware. It also seeks to explain how OpenFlow could be used as a part of
this solution to overcome the limitations of GMPLS.
