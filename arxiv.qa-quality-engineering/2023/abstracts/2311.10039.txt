Title: Software Dependability Measurement at the Age Of 36
Authors: Robert V. Binder
ID: 2311.10039
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.10039

Abstract:
Thirty-six years after the first edition of IEEE standard 982.1, Measures of the
Software Aspects of Dependability, the third edition focuses on the measurement
of in-service software dependability. This article explains how this new point
of view evolved and shaped the third edition's guidance for software
dependability measurement.
