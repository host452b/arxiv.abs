Title: Seeding Contradiction: a fast method for generating full-coverage test suites
Authors: Li Huang, Bertrand Meyer, Manuel Oriol
ID: 2309.04232
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.04232

Abstract:
The regression test suite, a key resource for managing program evolution, needs
to achieve 100% coverage, or very close, to be useful. Devising a test suite
manually is unacceptably tedious, but existing automated methods are often
inefficient. The method described in this article, ``Seeding Contradiction'',
inserts incorrect instructions into every basic block of the program, enabling
an SMT-based Hoare-style prover to generate a counterexample for every branch of
the program and, from the collection of all such counterexamples, a test suite.
The method is static, works fast, and achieves excellent coverage.
