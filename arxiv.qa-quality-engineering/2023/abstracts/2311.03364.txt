Title: BDD-Based Framework with RL Integration: An approach for videogames automated testing
Authors: Vincent Mastain, Fabio Petrillo
ID: 2311.03364
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.03364

Abstract:
Testing plays a vital role in software development, but in the realm of video
games, the process differs from traditional software development practices. Game
developers typically rely on human testers who are provided with checklists to
evaluate various elements. While major game developers already employ automated
testing using script-based bots, the increasing complexity of video games is
pushing the limits of scripted solutions, necessitating the adoption of more
advanced testing strategies. To assist game studios in enhancing the quality of
their games through automated testing, we propose the integration of Behavior
Driven Development (BDD) with Reinforcement Learning (RL). This positional paper
summarizes our proposal and framework under development.
