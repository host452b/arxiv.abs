Title: QI2 -- an Interactive Tool for Data Quality Assurance
Authors: Simon Geerkens, Christian Sieberichs, Alexander Braun, Thomas Waschulzik
ID: 2307.03419
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.03419

Abstract:
The importance of high data quality is increasing with the growing impact and
distribution of ML systems and big data. Also the planned AI Act from the
European commission defines challenging legal requirements for data quality
especially for the market introduction of safety relevant ML systems. In this
paper we introduce a novel approach that supports the data quality assurance
process of multiple data quality aspects. This approach enables the verification
of quantitative data quality requirements. The concept and benefits are
introduced and explained on small example data sets. How the method is applied
is demonstrated on the well known MNIST data set based an handwritten digits.
