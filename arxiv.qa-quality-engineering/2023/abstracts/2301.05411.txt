Title: Do the Defect Prediction Models Really Work?
Authors: Umamaheswara Sharma B, Ravichandra Sadam
ID: 2301.05411
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2301.05411

Abstract:
You may develop a potential prediction model, but how can I trust your model
that it will benefit my software?. Using a software defect prediction (SDP)
model as a tool, we address this fundamental problem in machine learning
research. This is a preliminary work targeted at providing an analysis of the
developed binary SDP model in real-time working environments.
