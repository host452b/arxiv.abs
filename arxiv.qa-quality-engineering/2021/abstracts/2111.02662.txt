Title: TEE-based Selective Testing of Local Workers in Federated Learning Systems
Authors: Wensheng Zhang, Trent Muhr
ID: 2111.02662
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2111.02662

Abstract:
This paper considers a federated learning system composed of a central
coordinating server and multiple distributed local workers, all having access to
trusted execution environments (TEEs). In order to ensure that the untrusted
workers correctly perform local learning, we propose a new TEE-based approach
that also combines techniques from applied cryptography, smart contract and game
theory. Theoretical analysis and implementation-based evaluations show that, the
proposed approach is secure, efficient and practical.
