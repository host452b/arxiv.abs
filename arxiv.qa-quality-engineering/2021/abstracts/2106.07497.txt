Title: Security testing using JUnit and Perl scripts
Authors: Julian Harty
ID: 2106.07497
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2106.07497

Abstract:
In this paper, I describe a recent practical experience where JUnit was used for
testing security bugs in addition to functional bugs. Perl scripts were also
used during the exploration phase. The application being tested was mature, but
insecure.
