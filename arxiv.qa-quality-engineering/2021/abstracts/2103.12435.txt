Title: Evaluation of the Sensitivity of RRAM Cells to Optical Fault Injection Attacks
Authors: Dmytro Petryk, Zoya Dyka, Eduardo Perez, Mamathamba Kalishettyhalli Mahadevaiaha, Ievgen Kabin et al.
ID: 2103.12435
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2103.12435

Abstract:
Resistive Random Access Memory (RRAM) is a type of Non-Volatile Memory (NVM). In
this paper we investigate the sensitivity of the TiN/Ti/Al:HfO2/TiN-based 1T-1R
RRAM cells implemented in a 250 nm CMOS IHP technology to the laser irradiation
in detail. Experimental results show the feasibility to influence the state of
the cells under laser irradiation, i.e. successful optical Fault Injection. We
focus on the selection of the parameters of the laser station and their
influence on the success of optical Fault Injections.
