Title: Teaching Software Testing and Debugging with the Serious Game Sojourner under Sabotage
Authors: Philipp Straubinger, Tim Greller, Gordon Fraser
ID: 2504.19291
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.19291

Abstract:
Software testing and debugging are often seen as tedious, making them
challenging to teach effectively. We present Sojourner under Sabotage, a
browser-based serious game that enhances learning through interactive,
narrative-driven challenges. Players act as spaceship crew members, using unit
tests and debugging techniques to fix sabotaged components. Sojourner under
Sabotage provides hands-on experience with the real-world testing framework
JUnit, improving student engagement, test coverage, and debugging skills.
