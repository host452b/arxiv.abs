Title: Psychometric Tests for AI Agents and Their Moduli Space
Authors: Przemyslaw Chojecki
ID: 2511.19262
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.19262

Abstract:
We develop a moduli-theoretic view of psychometric test batteries for AI agents
and connect it explicitly to the AAI score developed previously. First, we make
precise the notion of an AAI functional on a battery and set out axioms that any
reasonable autonomy/general intelligence score should satisfy. Second, we show
that the composite index ('AAI-Index') defined previously is a special case of
our AAI functional. Third, we introduce the notion of a cognitive core of an
agent relative to a battery and define the associated AAI$_{\textrm{core}}$
score as the restriction of an AAI functional to that core. Finally, we use
these notions to describe invariants of batteries under evaluation-preserving
symmetries and outline how moduli of equivalent batteries are organized.
