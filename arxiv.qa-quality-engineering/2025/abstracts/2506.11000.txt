Title: Ever-Improving Test Suite by Leveraging Large Language Models
Authors: Ketai Qiu
ID: 2506.11000
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.11000

Abstract:
Augmenting test suites with test cases that reflect the actual usage of the
software system is extremely important to sustain the quality of long lasting
software systems. In this paper, we propose E-Test, an approach that
incrementally augments a test suite with test cases that exercise behaviors that
emerge in production and that are not been tested yet. E-Test leverages Large
Language Models to identify already-tested, not-yet-tested, and error-prone unit
execution scenarios, and augment the test suite accordingly. Our experimental
evaluation shows that E-Test outperforms the main state-of-the-art approaches to
identify inadequately tested behaviors and optimize test suites.
