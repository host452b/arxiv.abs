Title: Testing Quasiperiodicity
Authors: Christine Awofeso, Ben Bals, Oded Lachish, Solon P. Pissis
ID: 2508.02231
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.02231

Abstract:
A cover (or quasiperiod) of a string $S$ is a shorter string $C$ such that every
position of $S$ is contained in some occurrence of $C$ as a substring. The
notion of covers was introduced by Apostolico and Ehrenfeucht over 30 years ago
[Theor. Comput. Sci. 1993] and it has received significant attention from the
combinatorial pattern matching community. In this note, we show how to
efficiently test whether $S$ admits a cover. Our tester can also be translated
into a streaming algorithm.
