Title: Towards Easy and Realistic Network Infrastructure Testing for Large-scale Machine Learning
Authors: Jinsun Yoo, ChonLam Lao, Lianjie Cao, Bob Lantz, Minlan Yu et al.
ID: 2504.20854
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.20854

Abstract:
This paper lays the foundation for Genie, a testing framework that captures the
impact of real hardware network behavior on ML workload performance, without
requiring expensive GPUs. Genie uses CPU-initiated traffic over a hardware
testbed to emulate GPU to GPU communication, and adapts the ASTRA-sim simulator
to model interaction between the network and the ML workload.
