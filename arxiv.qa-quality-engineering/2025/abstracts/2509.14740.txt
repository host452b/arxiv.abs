Title: Wireless Communication Performance Testing: From Laboratory Environment to Research Vessel
Authors: Andrei-Raoul Morariu, Andreas Strandberg, Bogdan Iancu, Jerker Bjorkqvist
ID: 2509.14740
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.14740

Abstract:
This study investigates signal transmission within a shared spectrum, focusing
on measurements conducted both in laboratory and outdoor environments. The
objective was to demonstrate how laboratory objects obstructing the line of
sight can attenuate the signal between a transmitter (Tx) and a receiver (Rx).
Additionally, we examined the impact of distance and placement in various
locations aboard an electric research boat on signal transmission efficiency.
These findings contribute to understanding whether the environmental factors
influence wireless communication in dynamic and obstructed environments.
