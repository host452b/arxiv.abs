Title: Design of Mutation Operators for Testing Geographic Information Systems
Authors: Suilen H. Alvarado
ID: 2003.00791
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2003.00791

Abstract:
In this article, we propose the definition of specific mutation operators for
testing Geographic Information Systems. We describe the process for applying the
operators and generating mutants, and present a case study where these mutation
operators are applied to two real-world applications.
