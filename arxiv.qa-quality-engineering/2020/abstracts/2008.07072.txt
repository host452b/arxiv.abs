Title: Artificial Neural Networks and Fault Injection Attacks
Authors: Shahin Tajik, Fatemeh Ganji
ID: 2008.07072
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2008.07072

Abstract:
This chapter is on the security assessment of artificial intelligence (AI) and
neural network (NN) accelerators in the face of fault injection attacks. More
specifically, it discusses the assets on these platforms and compares them with
ones known and well-studied in the field of cryptographic systems. This is a
crucial step that must be taken in order to define the threat models precisely.
With respect to that, fault attacks mounted on NNs and AI accelerators are
explored.
