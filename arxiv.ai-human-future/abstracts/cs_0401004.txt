Title: Cyborg Systems as Platforms for Computer-Vision Algorithm-Development for Astrobiology
Authors: Patrick C. McGuire, J. A. Rodriguez-Manfredi, E. Sebastian-Martinez, J. Gomez-Elvira, E. Diaz-Martinez, J. Ormo, K. Neuffer, A. Giaquinta, F. Camps-Martinez, A. Lepinette-Malvitte, J. Perez-Mercader, H. Ritter, M. Oesker, J. Ontrup, J. Walter
ID: cs/0401004
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/cs/0401004

Abstract:
Employing the allegorical imagery from the film "The Matrix", we motivate and discuss our `Cyborg Astrobiologist' research program. In this research program, we are using a wearable computer and video camcorder in order to test and train a computer-vision system to be a field-geologist and field-astrobiologist.
