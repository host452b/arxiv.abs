# arxiv.ai-human-future — AI × Human Evolution × Future Society

**1,814 arXiv abstracts** from 12 targeted searches across `cs`, `q-bio`, and interdisciplinary categories, covering the quantitative and biological dimensions of AI development and its long-term impact on humans, populations, and civilization.

**Core question:** When AI reaches a certain level of capability — what does the relationship between humans and artificial intelligence look like? How can we predict and quantify the future?
