Title: Inductive Bias Extraction and Matching for LLM Prompts
Authors: Christian M. Angel, Francis Ferraro
ID: 2508.10295
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.10295

Abstract:
The active research topic of prompt engineering makes it evident that LLMs are
sensitive to small changes in prompt wording. A portion of this can be ascribed
to the inductive bias that is present in the LLM. By using an LLM's output as a
portion of its prompt, we can more easily create satisfactory wording for
prompts. This has the effect of creating a prompt that matches the inductive
bias in model. Empirically, we show that using this Inductive Bias Extraction
and Matching strategy improves LLM Likert ratings used for classification by up
to 19% and LLM Likert ratings used for ranking by up to 27%.
