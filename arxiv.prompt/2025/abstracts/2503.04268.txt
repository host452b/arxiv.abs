Title: ControlFill: Spatially Adjustable Image Inpainting from Prompt Learning
Authors: Boseong Jeon
ID: 2503.04268
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.04268

Abstract:
In this report, I present an inpainting framework named \textit{ControlFill},
which involves training two distinct prompts: one for generating plausible
objects within a designated mask (\textit{creation}) and another for filling the
region by extending the background (\textit{removal}). During the inference
stage, these learned embeddings guide a diffusion network that operates without
requiring heavy text encoders. By adjusting the relative significance of the two
prompts and employing classifier-free guidance, users can control the intensity
of removal or creation. Furthermore, I introduce a method to spatially vary the
intensity of guidance by assigning different scales to individual pixels.
