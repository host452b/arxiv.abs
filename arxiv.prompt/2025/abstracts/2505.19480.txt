Title: Room Impulse Response as a Prompt for Acoustic Echo Cancellation
Authors: Fei Zhao, Shulin He, Xueliang Zhang
ID: 2505.19480
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.19480

Abstract:
Data-driven acoustic echo cancellation (AEC) methods, predominantly trained on
synthetic or constrained real-world datasets, encounter performance declines in
unseen echo scenarios, especially in real environments where echo paths are not
directly observable. Our proposed method counters this limitation by integrating
room impulse response (RIR) as a pivotal training prompt, aiming to improve the
generalization of AEC models in such unforeseen conditions. We also explore four
RIR prompt fusion methods. Comprehensive evaluations, including both simulated
RIR under unknown conditions and recorded RIR in real, demonstrate that the
proposed approach significantly improves performance compared to baseline
models. These results substantiate the effectiveness of our RIR-guided approach
in strengthening the model's generalization capabilities.
