Title: Commonsense-Aware Prompting for Controllable Empathetic Dialogue
Generation
Authors: Yiren Liu, Halil Kilicoglu
ID: 2302.01441
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.01441

Abstract:
Improving the emotional awareness of pre-trained language models is an emerging
important problem for dialogue generation tasks. Although prior studies have
introduced methods to improve empathetic dialogue generation, few have discussed
how to incorporate commonsense knowledge into pre-trained language models for
controllable dialogue generation. In this study, we propose a novel framework
that improves empathetic dialogue generation using pre-trained language models
by 1) incorporating commonsense knowledge through prompt verbalization, and 2)
controlling dialogue generation using a strategy-driven future discriminator. We
conducted experiments to reveal that both the incorporation of social
commonsense knowledge and enforcement of control over generation help to improve
generation performance. Finally, we discuss the implications of our study for
future research.
