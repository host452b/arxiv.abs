Title: Dynamic VLM-Guided Negative Prompting for Diffusion Models
Authors: Hoyeon Chang, Seungjin Kim, Yoonseok Choi
ID: 2510.26052
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26052

Abstract:
We propose a novel approach for dynamic negative prompting in diffusion models
that leverages Vision-Language Models (VLMs) to adaptively generate negative
prompts during the denoising process. Unlike traditional Negative Prompting
methods that use fixed negative prompts, our method generates intermediate image
predictions at specific denoising steps and queries a VLM to produce
contextually appropriate negative prompts. We evaluate our approach on various
benchmark datasets and demonstrate the trade-offs between negative guidance
strength and text-image alignment.
