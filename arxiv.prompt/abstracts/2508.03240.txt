Title: CardiffNLP at CLEARS-2025: Prompting Large Language Models for Plain
Language and Easy-to-Read Text Rewriting
Authors: Mutaz Ayesh, Nicolás Gutiérrez-Rolón, Fernando Alva-Manchego
ID: 2508.03240
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.03240

Abstract:
This paper details the CardiffNLP team's contribution to the CLEARS shared task
on Spanish text adaptation, hosted by IberLEF 2025. The shared task contained
two subtasks and the team submitted to both. Our team took an LLM-prompting
approach with different prompt variations. While we initially experimented with
LLaMA-3.2, we adopted Gemma-3 for our final submission, and landed third place
in Subtask 1 and second place in Subtask 2. We detail our numerous prompt
variations, examples, and experimental results.
