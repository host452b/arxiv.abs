Title: Retcon -- a Prompt-Based Technique for Precise Control of LLMs in
Conversations
Authors: David Kogan, Sam Nguyen, Masanori Suzuki, Feiyang Chen
ID: 2603.03317
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03317

Abstract:
Recent advances in Large Language Models (LLMs) allow agents to execute complex
natural language tasks. Many LLM applications, such as support agents, teaching
assistants, and interactive bots, involve multi-turn conversations. However, it
remains challenging to control LLMs in the context of such interactions,
particularly when the LLM behavior needs to be adjustable over the course of the
conversation. In this paper, we present Retcon, a few-shot prompting technique
designed to provide turn-level control over LLMs in conversations. We then
demonstrate that it performs significantly better than zero-shot and traditional
few-shot prompting.
