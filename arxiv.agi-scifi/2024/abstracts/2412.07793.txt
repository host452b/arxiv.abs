Title: Publication Trends in Artificial Intelligence Conferences: The Rise of
Super Prolific Authors
Authors: Ariful Azad, Afeefa Banu
ID: 2412.07793
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.07793

Abstract:
Papers published in top conferences contribute influential discoveries that are
reshaping the landscape of modern Artificial Intelligence (AI). We analyzed
87,137 papers from 11 AI conferences to examine publication trends over the past
decade. Our findings reveal a consistent increase in both the number of papers
and authors, reflecting the growing interest in AI research. We also observed a
rise in prolific researchers who publish dozens of papers at the same conference
each year. In light of this analysis, the AI research community should consider
revisiting authorship policies, addressing equity concerns, and evaluating the
workload of junior researchers to foster a more sustainable and inclusive
research environment.
