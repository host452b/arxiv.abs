Title: Grade of the law of distribution of the vector of the digital twin of the
enterprise
Authors: Sergei Masaev, Ivan Oreshnikov, Nikita Ivanitskiy, Valentina Vingert
ID: 2412.15562
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.15562

Abstract:
Digital transformation acts as a main factor in the development and
competitiveness of Japanese companies. In this context, the digital copy
management algorithm based on the P2M (Project to Method) method plays a
significant role in increasing efficiency and reducing costs through the
implementation of digital technologies. This research aims to develop and
implement an algorithm for managing a digital copy of a project using the P2M
method using the Kolmogorov test to evaluate the effectiveness of various
processes and determine the best ways to improve productivity.
