Title: Syntropy in complex systems: A complement to Shannon's Entropy
Authors: Santiago Mendez-Moreno
ID: 2403.13022
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.13022

Abstract:
This study introduces the syntropy function ($S_N$) and expectancy function
($E_N$), derived from the novel function $φ$, to provide a refined perspective
on complexity, extending beyond conventional entropy analysis. $S_N$ is designed
to detect localized coherent events, whereas $E_N$ encapsulates expected system
behaviors, offering a comprehensive framework for understanding system dynamics.
The manuscript explores essential theorems and properties, underscoring their
theoretical and practical implications. Future research will further elucidate
their roles, particularly in biological signals and dynamic systems, suggesting
a deep interplay between order and chaos.
