Title: Machine Learning-based vs Deep Learning-based Anomaly Detection in
Multivariate Time Series for Spacecraft Attitude Sensors
Authors: R. Gallon, F. Schiemenz, A. Krstova, A. Menicucci, E. Gill
ID: 2409.17841
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.17841

Abstract:
In the framework of Failure Detection, Isolation and Recovery (FDIR) on
spacecraft, new AI-based approaches are emerging in the state of the art to
overcome the limitations commonly imposed by traditional threshold checking.
  The present research aims at characterizing two different approaches to the
problem of stuck values detection in multivariate time series coming from
spacecraft attitude sensors. The analysis reveals the performance differences in
the two approaches, while commenting on their interpretability and
generalization to different scenarios.
