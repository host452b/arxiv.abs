Title: Integrating Reasoning Systems for Trustworthy AI, Proceedings of the 4th
Workshop on Logic and Practice of Programming (LPOP)
Authors: Anil Nerode, Yanhong A. Liu
ID: 2410.19738
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.19738

Abstract:
This proceedings contains abstracts and position papers for the work to be
presented at the fourth Logic and Practice of Programming (LPOP) Workshop. The
workshop is to be held in Dallas, Texas, USA, and as a hybrid event, on October
13, 2024, in conjunction with the 40th International Conference on Logic
Programming (ICLP). The focus of this workshop is integrating reasoning systems
for trustworthy AI, especially including integrating diverse models of
programming with rules and constraints.
