Title: Younger: The First Dataset for Artificial Intelligence-Generated Neural
Network Architecture
Authors: Zhengxin Yang, Wanling Gao, Luzhou Peng, Yunyou Huang, Fei Tang,
Jianfeng Zhan
ID: 2406.15132
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.15132

Abstract:
Designing and optimizing neural network architectures typically requires
extensive expertise, starting with handcrafted designs and then manual or
automated refinement. This dependency presents a significant barrier to rapid
innovation. Recognizing the complexity of automatically generating neural
network architecture from scratch, we introduce Younger, a pioneering dataset to
advance this ambitious goal. Derived from over 174K real-world models across
more than 30 tasks from various public model hubs, Younger includes 7,629 unique
architectures, and each is represented as a directed acyclic graph with detailed
operator-level information. The dataset facilitates two primary design
paradigms: global, for creating complete architectures from scratch, and local,
for detailed architecture component refinement. By establishing these
capabilities, Younger contributes to a new frontier, Artificial Intelligence-
Generated Neural Network Architecture (AIGNNA). Our experiments explore the
potential and effectiveness of Younger for automated architecture generation
and, as a secondary benefit, demonstrate that Younger can serve as a benchmark
dataset, advancing the development of graph neural networks. We release the
dataset and code publicly to lower the entry barriers and encourage further
research in this challenging area.
