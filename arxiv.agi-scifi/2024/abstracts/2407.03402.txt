Title: Internal entropy from heat current
Authors: Noam Schiller, Hiromi Ebisu, Gil Refael, Yuval Oreg
ID: 2407.03402
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.03402

Abstract:
We demonstrate that the effective internal entropy of quasiparticles within the
non-Abelian fractional quantum Hall effect manifests in the heat current through
a tunneling barrier. We derive the electric current and heat current resulting
from voltage and heat biases of the junction, taking into account the
quasiparticles' internal entropy. We find that when the tunneling processes are
dominated by quasiparticle tunneling of one type of charge, the effective
internal entropy can be inferred from the measurement of the heat current and
the charge current. Our methods may be used to conclusively identify non-Abelian
quasiparticles, such as the anyons that emerge in the $ν= 5/2$ fractional
quantum Hall state.
