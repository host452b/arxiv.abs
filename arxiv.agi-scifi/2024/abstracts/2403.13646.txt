Title: A scaling law for a model of epitaxially strained elastic films with
dislocations
Authors: Lukas Abel, Janusz Ginster, Barbara Zwicknagl
ID: 2403.13646
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.13646

Abstract:
A static variational model for shape formation in heteroepitaxial crystal growth
is considered. The energy functional takes into account surface energy, elastic
misfit-energy and nucleation energy of dislocations. A scaling law for the
infimal energy is proven. The results quantify the expectation that in certain
parameter regimes, island formation or topological defects are favorable. This
generalizes results in the purely elastic setting from [Goldman and Zwicknagl
(2014)]. To handle dislocations in the lower bound, a new variant of a ball-
construction combined with thorough local estimates is presented.
