Title: What is Meant by AGI? On the Definition of Artificial General
Intelligence
Authors: Bowen Xu
ID: 2404.10731
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.10731

Abstract:
This paper aims to establish a consensus on AGI's definition. General
intelligence refers to the adaptation to open environments according to certain
principles using limited resources. It emphasizes that adaptation or learning is
an indispensable property of intelligence, and places the controversial part
within the principles of intelligence, which can be described from different
perspectives.
