Title: Artificial intelligence-based blockchain-driven financial default
prediction
Authors: Junjun Huang
ID: 2410.00044
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.00044

Abstract:
With the rapid development of technology, blockchain and artificial intelligence
technology are playing a huge role in all walks of life. In the financial
sector, blockchain solves many security problems in data storage and management
in traditional systems with its advantages of decentralization and security. And
artificial intelligence has huge advantages in financial forecasting and risk
management through its powerful algorithmic modeling capabilities. In financial
default prediction using blockchain and artificial intelligence technology is a
very powerful application. Blockchain technology guarantees the credibility of
data and consistency on all nodes, and machine learning builds a high-level
default prediction model through detailed analysis of big data. This study
offers financial institutions new thoughts on financial technology in terms of
credit risk mitigation and financial system stabilization.
