Title: Exploiting Memory-aware Q-distribution Prediction for Nuclear Fusion via
Modern Hopfield Network
Authors: Qingchuan Ma, Shiao Wang, Tong Zheng, Xiaodong Dai, Yifeng Wang,
Qingquan Yang, Xiao Wang
ID: 2410.08889
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.08889

Abstract:
This study addresses the critical challenge of predicting the Q-distribution in
long-term stable nuclear fusion task, a key component for advancing clean energy
solutions. We introduce an innovative deep learning framework that employs
Modern Hopfield Networks to incorporate associative memory from historical
shots. Utilizing a newly compiled dataset, we demonstrate the effectiveness of
our approach in enhancing Q-distribution prediction. The proposed method
represents a significant advancement by leveraging historical memory information
for the first time in this context, showcasing improved prediction accuracy and
contributing to the optimization of nuclear fusion research.
