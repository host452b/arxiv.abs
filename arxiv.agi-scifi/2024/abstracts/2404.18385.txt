Title: Equivalence: An analysis of artists' roles with Image Generative AI from
Conceptual Art perspective through an interactive installation design practice
Authors: Yixuan Li, Dan C. Baciu, Marcos Novak, George Legrady
ID: 2404.18385
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.18385

Abstract:
Over the past year, the emergence of advanced text-to-image Generative AI models
has significantly impacted the art world, challenging traditional notions of
creativity and the role of artists. This study explores how artists interact
with these technologies, using a 5P model (Purpose, People, Process, Product,
and Press) based on Rhodes' creativity framework to compare the artistic
processes behind Conceptual Art and Image Generative AI. To exemplify this
framework, a practical case study titled "Equivalence", a multi-screen
interactive installation that converts users' speech input into continuously
evolving paintings developed based on Stable Diffusion and NLP algorithms, was
developed. Through comprehensive analysis and the case study, this work aims to
broaden our understanding of artists' roles and foster a deeper appreciation for
the creative aspects inherent in artwork created with Image Generative AI.
