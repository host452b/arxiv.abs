Title: Quantum memristors for neuromorphic quantum machine learning
Authors: Lucas Lamata
ID: 2412.18979
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.18979

Abstract:
Quantum machine learning may permit to realize more efficient machine learning
calculations with near-term quantum devices. Among the diverse quantum machine
learning paradigms which are currently being considered, quantum memristors are
promising as a way of combining, in the same quantum hardware, a unitary
evolution with the nonlinearity provided by the measurement and feedforward.
Thus, an efficient way of deploying neuromorphic quantum computing for quantum
machine learning may be enabled.
