Title: Holography of Transmission Lines: Insights of Continuous MERA and AdS/CFT
Authors: So Katagiri
ID: 2407.04171
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.04171

Abstract:
This study examines the holographic representation of the quantum theory of
transmission lines, which play a crucial role in quantum computing and quantum
information. Utilizing Yurke and Denker's quantum circuit network theory within
the framework of continuous MERA (cMERA) in AdS space, we analyze the
quantization and interactions of transmission lines. The metric is revealed to
be described by the inductance of the quantum circuit, which is AdS-space in its
0-limit. These results provide new insights into handling and controlling
complex phenomena in quantum circuits, potentially advancing the understanding
of quantum computing and quantum communication.
