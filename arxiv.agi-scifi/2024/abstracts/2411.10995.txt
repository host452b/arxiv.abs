Title: From Crime to Hypercrime: Evolving Threats and Law Enforcement's New
Mandate in the AI Age
Authors: Francesco Schiliro
ID: 2411.10995
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.10995

Abstract:
The paper examines the trajectory of crime, tracing its evolution from
traditional forms to digital manifestations in cybercrime, and proposes
"Hypercrime" as the latest frontier. Leveraging insights from Michael McGuire's
"Hypercrime: The New Geometry of Harm," the study calls for a paradigm shift in
law enforcement strategies to meet the challenges posed by AI-driven hypercrime.
Emphasis is placed on understanding hypercrime's complexity, developing
proactive policies, and embracing technological tools to mitigate risks
associated with AI misuse.
