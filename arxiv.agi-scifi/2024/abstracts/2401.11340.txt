Title: Permutation group entropy: a new route to complexity for real-valued
processes
Authors: José M. Amigó, Roberto Dale, Piergiulio Tempesta
ID: 2401.11340
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.11340

Abstract:
This is a review of group entropy and its application to permutation complexity.
Specifically we revisit a new approach to the notion of complexity in time serie
analysis, based on both permutation entropy and group entropy. As a result, the
permutation entropy rate can be extended from deterministic dynamics to random
processes. More generally, our approach provides a unified framework to discuss
chaotic and random behaviours.
