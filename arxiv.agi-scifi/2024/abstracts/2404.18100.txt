Title: Accurate and Unbiased Reconstruction of CMB B Mode using Deep Learning
Authors: Srikanta Pal, Sarvesh Kumar Yadav, Rajib Saha, Tarun Souradeep
ID: 2404.18100
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.18100

Abstract:
An ingeniously designed autoencoder (PrimeNet) using simulated observations of
future generation ECHO satellite mission recovers CMB B mode map, angular
spectrum for multipoles $\ell \lesssim 9$ and tensor to scalar ratio $r$ {\it
limited only by cosmic variance down to $r= 0.0001$ and below}. We use diverse,
realistically complex and detailed foreground models. PrimeNet predicts accurate
results even when data with $r=0$ are tested which were not used in training,
implying robust and efficient predictive power. The work eliminates a major
bottleneck of weak CMB B mode reconstruction and takes a leap forward for
understanding fundamental physics of the primordial Universe.
