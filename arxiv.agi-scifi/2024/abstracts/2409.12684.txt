Title: Detecting Hyperons in neutron stars -- a machine learning approach
Authors: Valéria Carvalho, Márcio Ferreira, Constança Providência
ID: 2409.12684
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.12684

Abstract:
We present a neural network classification model for detecting the presence of
hyperonic degrees of freedom in neutron stars. The models take radii and/or
tidal deformabilities as input and give the probability for the presence of
hyperons in the neutron star composition. Different numbers of observations and
different levels of uncertainty in the neutron star properties are tested. The
models have been trained on a dataset of well-calibrated microscopic equations
of state of neutron star matter based on a relativistic mean-field formalism.
Real data and data generated from a different description of hyperonic matter
are used to test the performance of the models.
