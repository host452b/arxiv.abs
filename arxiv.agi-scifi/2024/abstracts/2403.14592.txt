Title: Envisioning the Next-Generation AI Coding Assistants: Insights &
Proposals
Authors: Khanh Nghiem, Anh Minh Nguyen, Nghi D. Q. Bui
ID: 2403.14592
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.14592

Abstract:
As a research-product hybrid group in AI for Software Engineering (AI4SE), we
present four key takeaways from our experience developing in-IDE AI coding
assistants. AI coding assistants should set clear expectations for usage,
integrate with advanced IDE capabilities and existing extensions, use extendable
backend designs, and collect app data responsibly for downstream analyses. We
propose open questions and challenges that academia and industry should address
to realize the vision of next-generation AI coding assistants.
