Title: Towards Risk Analysis of the Impact of AI on the Deliberate Biological
Threat Landscape
Authors: Matthew E. Walsh
ID: 2401.12755
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2401.12755

Abstract:
The perception that the convergence of biological engineering and artificial
intelligence (AI) could enable increased biorisk has recently drawn attention to
the governance of biotechnology and artificial intelligence. The 2023 Executive
Order, Executive Order on the Safe, Secure, and Trustworthy Development and Use
of Artificial Intelligence, requires an assessment of how artificial
intelligence can increase biorisk. Within this perspective, quantitative and
qualitative frameworks for evaluating biorisk are presented. Both frameworks are
exercised using notional scenarios and their benefits and limitations are then
discussed. Finally, the perspective concludes by noting that assessment and
evaluation methodologies must keep pace with advances of AI in the life
sciences.
