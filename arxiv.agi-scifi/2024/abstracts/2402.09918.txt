Title: Quantum gravity phenomenology and the blackbody radiation
Authors: R. Turcati, I. Soares, S. B. Duarte
ID: 2402.09918
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.09918

Abstract:
We analyze the blackbody radiation problem in the presence of quantum gravity
effects encoded in modified dispersion relations. The spectral radiance and the
generalized Stefan-Boltzmann law are studied in this context. Furthermore, the
regime of low temperatures is also contemplated, where features related to the
blackbody thermal laws and the thermodynamic quantities such as energy,
pressure, entropy, and specific heat are obtained. Possible implications in
compact objects such as neutron stars are also discussed.
