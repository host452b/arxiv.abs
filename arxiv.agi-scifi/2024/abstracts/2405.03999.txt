Title: Interaction Design for Human-AI Choreography Co-creation
Authors: Yimeng Liu
ID: 2405.03999
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.03999

Abstract:
Human-AI co-creation aims to combine human and AI strengths for artistic results
exceeding individual capabilities. Frameworks exist for painting, music, and
poetry, but choreography's embodied nature demands a dedicated approach. This
paper explores AI-assisted choreography techniques (e.g., generative ideation,
embodied improvisation) and analyzes interaction design -- how humans and AI
collaborate and communicate -- to inform the design considerations of future
human-AI choreography co-creation systems.
