Title: AI security and cyber risk in IoT systems
Authors: Petar Radanliev, David De Roure, Carsten Maple, Jason R. C. Nurse,
Razvan Nicolescu, Uchenna Ani
ID: 2410.09194
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.09194

Abstract:
We present a dependency model tailored to the context of current challenges in
data strategies and make recommendations for the cybersecurity community. The
model can be used for cyber risk estimation and assessment and generic risk
impact assessment.
