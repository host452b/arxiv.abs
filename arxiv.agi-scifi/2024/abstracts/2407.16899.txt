Title: A Framework for AI assisted Musical Devices
Authors: Miguel Civit, Luis Munoz Saavedra, Francisco Jose Cuadrado, Charles
Tijus, Maria J. Escalona
ID: 2407.16899
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.16899

Abstract:
In this paper we present a novel framework for the study and design of AI
assisted musical devices (AIMEs). Initially, we present a taxonomy of these
devices and illustrate it with a set of scenarios and personas. Later, we
propose a generic architecture for the implementation of AIMEs and present some
examples from the scenarios. We show that the proposed framework and
architecture are a valid tool for the study of intelligent musical devices.
