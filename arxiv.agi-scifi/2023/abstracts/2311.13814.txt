Title: Safe Physical Human-Robot Interaction through Variable Impedance Control
based on ISO/TS 15066
Authors: Armin Ghanbarzadeh, Esmaeil Najafi
ID: 2311.13814
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.13814

Abstract:
The successful implementation of Physical Human-Robot Interaction in industrial
environments depends on ensuring safe collaboration between human operators and
robotic devices. This necessitates the adoption of measures that guarantee the
safety of human operators in close proximity to robots, without constraining the
speed and motion of the robotic systems. This paper proposes a novel variable
impedance-based controller for cobots that ensures safe collaboration by
adhering to the ISO/TS 15066 safety standard, namely power and force limiting
mode, while achieving higher operational speeds. The effectiveness of the
proposed controller has been compared with conventional methods and implemented
on two different robotic platforms. The results demonstrate the designed
controller achieves higher speeds, while maintaining compliance with safety
standards. The proposed variable impedance holds significant potential for
enabling efficient and safe collaboration between humans and robots in
industrial settings.
