Title: Black holes in quantum gravity
Authors: Daniel Harlow
ID: 2304.10367
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2304.10367

Abstract:
This chapter gives an overview of the quantum aspects of black holes, focusing
on the black hole information problem, the counting of black hole entropy in
string theory, and the emergence of spacetime in holography. It is aimed at a
broad physics audience, and does not presuppose knowledge of string theory or
holography.
