Title: SCRITA 2023: Trust, Acceptance and Social Cues in Human-Robot Interaction
Authors: Alessandra Rossi, Patrick Holthaus, Gabriella Lakatos, Sílvia Moros,
Lewis Riches
ID: 2311.05401
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.05401

Abstract:
This workshop focused on identifying the challenges and dynamics between people
and robots to foster short interactions and long-lasting relationships in
different fields, from educational, service, collaborative, companion, care-home
and medical robotics. For that, this workshop facilitated a discussion about
people's trust towards robots "in the field", inviting workshop participants to
contribute their past experiences and lessons learnt.
