Title: Quantum Computing with dartboards
Authors: Ishaan Ganti, Srinivasan S. Iyengar
ID: 2305.06153
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.06153

Abstract:
We present a physically appealing and elegant picture for quantum computing
using rules constructed for a game of darts. A dartboard is used to represent
the state space in quantum mechanics and the act of throwing the dart is shown
to have close similarities to the concept of measurement, or collapse of the
wavefunction in quantum mechanics. The analogy is constructed in arbitrary
dimensional spaces, that is using arbitrary dimensional dartboards, and for for
such arbitrary spaces this also provides us a ``visual'' description of
uncertainty. Finally, connections of qubits and quantum computing algorithms is
also made opening the possibility to construct analogies between quantum
algorithms and coupled dart-throw competitions.
