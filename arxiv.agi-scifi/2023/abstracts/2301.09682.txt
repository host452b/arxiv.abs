Title: Using I4.0 digital twins in agriculture
Authors: Rodrigo Falcão, Raghad Matar, Bernd Rauch
ID: 2301.09682
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2301.09682

Abstract:
Agriculture is a huge domain where an enormous landscape of systems interact to
support agricultural processes, which are becoming increasingly digital. From
the perspective of agricultural service providers, a prominent challenge is
interoperability. In the Fraunhofer lighthouse project Cognitive Agriculture
(COGNAC), we investigated how the usage of Industry 4.0 digital twins (I4.0 DTs)
can help overcome this challenge. This paper contributes architecture drivers
and a solution concept using I4.0 DTs in the agricultural domain. Furthermore,
we discuss the opportunities and limitations offered by I4.0 DTs for the
agricultural domain.
