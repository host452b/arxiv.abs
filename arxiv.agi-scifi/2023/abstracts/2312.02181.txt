Title: How Generative-AI can be Effectively used in Government Chatbots
Authors: Zeteng Lin
ID: 2312.02181
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.02181

Abstract:
With the rapid development of artificial intelligence and breakthroughs in
machine learning and natural language processing, intelligent question-answering
robots have become widely used in government affairs. This paper conducts a
horizontal comparison between Guangdong Province's government chatbots, ChatGPT,
and Wenxin Ernie, two large language models, to analyze the strengths and
weaknesses of existing government chatbots and AIGC technology. The study finds
significant differences between government chatbots and large language models.
China's government chatbots are still in an exploratory stage and have a gap to
close to achieve "intelligence." To explore the future direction of government
chatbots more deeply, this research proposes targeted optimization paths to help
generative AI be effectively applied in government chatbot conversations.
