Title: Notes on time entanglement and pseudo-entropy
Authors: K. Narayan, Hitesh K. Saini
ID: 2303.01307
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2303.01307

Abstract:
Following arXiv:2210.12963 [hep-th], we investigate aspects of the time
evolution operator regarded as a density operator and associated entanglement-
like structures in various quantum systems. These involve timelike separations
and generically lead to complex-valued entropy, although there are interesting
real subfamilies. There are many parallels and close relations with reduced
transition matrices and pseudo-entropy, which we discuss and clarify. For
instance, a related quantity involves the time evolution operator along with a
projection onto some initial state, which amounts to analysing pseudo-entropy
for the initial state and its time-evolved final state.
