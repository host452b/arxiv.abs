Title: On the Potential of Artificial Intelligence Chatbots for Data Exploration
of Federated Bioinformatics Knowledge Graphs
Authors: Ana-Claudia Sima, Tarcisio Mendes de Farias
ID: 2304.10427
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2304.10427

Abstract:
In this paper, we present work in progress on the role of artificial
intelligence (AI) chatbots, such as ChatGPT, in facilitating data access to
federated knowledge graphs. In particular, we provide examples from the field of
bioinformatics, to illustrate the potential use of Conversational AI to describe
datasets, as well as generate and explain (federated) queries across datasets
for the benefit of domain experts.
