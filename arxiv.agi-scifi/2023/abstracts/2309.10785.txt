Title: Quantum Gravity from dynamical metric fluctuations
Authors: Jan M. Pawlowski, Manuel Reichert
ID: 2309.10785
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.10785

Abstract:
In this contribution, we discuss the asymptotic safety scenario for quantum
gravity by evaluating the correlation functions of dynamical metric
fluctuations. This is done with a functional renormalisation group approach that
disentangles dynamical metric fluctuations from the background metric. We detail
the derivation of the respective flow equations on space-time manifolds with
Euclidean and Lorentzian signatures and discuss the diffeomorphism symmetry
constraints on the flow as well as the convergence of systematic vertex
expansion schemes. We then proceed with a comprehensive review of results of
momentum-dependent correlation functions at vanishing cutoff scale, the phase
structure of the asymptotically safe Standard Model, and spectral properties of
asymptotically safe gravity from direct computations in space-times with
Lorentzian signatures such as the graviton spectral function.
