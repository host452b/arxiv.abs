Title: SLIQ: Quantum Image Similarity Networks on Noisy Quantum Computers
Authors: Daniel Silver, Tirthak Patel, Aditya Ranjan, Harshitta Gandhi, William
Cutler, Devesh Tiwari
ID: 2309.15259
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.15259

Abstract:
Exploration into quantum machine learning has grown tremendously in recent years
due to the ability of quantum computers to speed up classical programs. However,
these efforts have yet to solve unsupervised similarity detection tasks due to
the challenge of porting them to run on quantum computers. To overcome this
challenge, we propose SLIQ, the first open-sourced work for resource-efficient
quantum similarity detection networks, built with practical and effective
quantum learning and variance-reducing algorithms.
