Title: Generative Network Layer for Communication Systems with Artificial
Intelligence
Authors: Mathias Thorsager, Israel Leyva-Mayorga, Beatriz Soret, Petar Popovski
ID: 2312.05398
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.05398

Abstract:
The traditional role of the network layer is the transfer of packet replicas
from source to destination through intermediate network nodes. We present a
generative network layer that uses Generative AI (GenAI) at intermediate or edge
network nodes and analyze its impact on the required data rates in the network.
We conduct a case study where the GenAI-aided nodes generate images from prompts
that consist of substantially compressed latent representations. The results
from network flow analyses under image quality constraints show that the
generative network layer can achieve an improvement of more than 100% in terms
of the required data rate.
