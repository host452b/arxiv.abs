Title: On the Quantum Bousso Bound in JT gravity
Authors: Victor Franken, François Rondeau
ID: 2311.17152
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.17152

Abstract:
We prove the Strominger-Thompson quantum Bousso bound in the infinite class of
conformal vacua in semiclassical JT gravity, with postive or negative
cosmological constant. The Bousso-Fisher-Leichenauer-Wall quantum Bousso bound
follows from an analogous derivation, requiring only initial quantum non-
expansion. In this process, we show that the quantity
${2πk^μk^ν<:T_{μν}:>-S''-\frac{6}{c}(S')^2}$ vanishes in any vacuum state,
entailing a stronger version of Wall's quantum null energy condition. We derive
an entropy formula in the presence of a generic class of two reflecting
boundaries, in order to apply our argument to the half reduction model of de
Sitter JT gravity.
