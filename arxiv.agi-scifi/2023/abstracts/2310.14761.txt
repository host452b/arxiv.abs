Title: A generalized pseudo-rotation with positive topological entropy
Authors: Erman Cineli
ID: 2310.14761
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.14761

Abstract:
In this note we give examples of Hamiltonian diffeomorphisms which are on one
hand dynamically complicated, for instance with positive topological entropy,
and on the other hand minimal from the perspective of Floer theory. The
minimality is in the sense that the barcode of the Floer complex of all iterates
of these maps consists of only infinite bars. In particular, the maps have zero
barcode entropy.
