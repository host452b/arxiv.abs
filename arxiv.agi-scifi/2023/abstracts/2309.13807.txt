Title: Forecasting large collections of time series: feature-based methods
Authors: Li Li, Feng Li, Yanfei Kang
ID: 2309.13807
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.13807

Abstract:
In economics and many other forecasting domains, the real world problems are too
complex for a single model that assumes a specific data generation process. The
forecasting performance of different methods changes depending on the nature of
the time series. When forecasting large collections of time series, two lines of
approaches have been developed using time series features, namely feature-based
model selection and feature-based model combination. This chapter discusses the
state-of-the-art feature-based methods, with reference to open-source software
implementations.
