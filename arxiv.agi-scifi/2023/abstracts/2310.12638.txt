Title: PSYCHIC: A Neuro-Symbolic Framework for Knowledge Graph Question-
Answering Grounding
Authors: Hanna Abi Akl
ID: 2310.12638
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.12638

Abstract:
The Scholarly Question Answering over Linked Data (Scholarly QALD) at The
International Semantic Web Conference (ISWC) 2023 challenge presents two sub-
tasks to tackle question answering (QA) over knowledge graphs (KGs). We answer
the KGQA over DBLP (DBLP-QUAD) task by proposing a neuro-symbolic (NS) framework
based on PSYCHIC, an extractive QA model capable of identifying the query and
entities related to a KG question. Our system achieved a F1 score of 00.18% on
question answering and came in third place for entity linking (EL) with a score
of 71.00%.
