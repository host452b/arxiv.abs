Title: On the initial state of the universe in quantum gravity and the time
arrow
Authors: Carlos Silva, Renan Aragão, Francisco A. Brito
ID: 2311.05670
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.05670

Abstract:
We address the thermodynamic evolution of the universe under quantum gravity by
considering the conditions for the existence of an arrow of time that drives it.
We find that for such an arrow of time to exist, the initial state of the cosmos
must correspond to a state of negative entropy. This open the possibility that
our universe emerged from a Dirac sea of negative entropy.
