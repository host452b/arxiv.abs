Title: Finiteness of hyperbolic entropy for holomorphic foliations with non-
degenerate singularities
Authors: François Bacher
ID: 2311.17236
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.17236

Abstract:
Consider $\mathscr{F}=(M,\mathscr{L},E)$ a Brody-hyperbolic foliation on a
compact complex surface $M$. Suppose that the singularities of $\mathscr{F}$ are
all non-degenerate. We show that the hyperbolic entropy of $\mathscr{F}$ is
finite.
