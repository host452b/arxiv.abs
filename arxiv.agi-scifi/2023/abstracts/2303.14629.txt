Title: Average entropy and asymptotics
Authors: Tatyana Barron, Manimugdha Saikia
ID: 2303.14629
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2303.14629

Abstract:
We determine the $N\to \infty$ asymptotics of the expected value of entanglement
entropy in $H_{1,N}\otimes H_{2,N}$, where $H_{1,N}$ and $H_{2,N}$ are the
spaces of holomorphic sections of the $N$-th tensor powers of hermitian ample
line bundles on compact complex manifolds.
