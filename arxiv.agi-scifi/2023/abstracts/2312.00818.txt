Title: The perpetual motion machine of AI-generated data and the distraction of
ChatGPT-as-scientist
Authors: Jennifer Listgarten
ID: 2312.00818
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.00818

Abstract:
Since ChatGPT works so well, are we on the cusp of solving science with AI? Is
not AlphaFold2 suggestive that the potential of LLMs in biology and the sciences
more broadly is limitless? Can we use AI itself to bridge the lack of data in
the sciences in order to then train an AI? Herein we present a discussion of
these topics.
