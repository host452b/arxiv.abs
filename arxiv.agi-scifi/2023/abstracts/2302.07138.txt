Title: The Heritage Digital Twin: a bicycle made for two. The integration of
digital methodologies into cultural heritage research
Authors: Franco Niccolucci, Béatrice Markhoff, Maria Theodoridou, Achille
Felicetti, Sorin Hermon
ID: 2302.07138
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.07138

Abstract:
The paper concerns the definition of a novel ontology for cultural heritage
based on the concept of digital twin. The ontology, called Heritage Digital Twin
ontology, is a compatible extension of the well-known CIDOC CRM ISO standard for
cultural heritage documentation and incorporates all the different documentation
systems presently in use for cultural heritage documentation. In the authors'
view, it supports documentation interoperability at a higher level than the ones
currently in use and enables effective cooperation among different users.
