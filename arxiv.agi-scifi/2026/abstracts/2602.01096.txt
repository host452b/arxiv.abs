Title: Replica Phase Transition with Quantum Gravity Corrections
Authors: Jun Nian, Yuan Zhong
ID: 2602.01096
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.01096

Abstract:
Motivated by bulk replica wormholes, we study the boundary effective theory that
describes the near-horizon fluctuations of a near-extremal Reissner-Nordström
black hole. This theory consists of a Schwarzian mode and a $U(1)$ phase mode.
We compute the partition function of this boundary theory on replica geometries,
from which the entropy is derived. Our analysis reveals a rich phase structure,
in which the dominance of connected or disconnected replica configurations leads
to a phase transition controlled by the temperature and the coupling constants
$C$, $K$, and $\mathcal{E}$ of the 1d effective theory.
