Title: Mathematical Foundations of Deep Learning
Authors: Xiaojing Ye
ID: 2603.18387
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.18387

Abstract:
This draft book offers a comprehensive and rigorous treatment of the
mathematical principles underlying modern deep learning. The book spans core
theoretical topics, from the approximation capabilities of deep neural networks,
the theory and algorithms of optimal control and reinforcement learning
integrated with deep learning techniques, to contemporary generative models that
drive today's advances in artificial intelligence.
