Title: PIDT: Physics-Informed Digital Twin for Optical Fiber Parameter
Estimation
Authors: Zicong Jiang, Magnus Karlsson, Erik Agrell, Christian Häger
ID: 2601.07436
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.07436

Abstract:
We propose physics-informed digital twin (PIDT): a fiber parameter estimation
approach that combines a parameterized split-step method with a physics-informed
loss. PIDT improves accuracy and convergence speed with lower complexity
compared to previous neural operators.
