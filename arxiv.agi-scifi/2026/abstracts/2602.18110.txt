Title: Cavity Solitons as a Nonlinear Substrate for Photonic Neuromorphic
Computing
Authors: Amir Arsalan Arabieh, Alessandro Lupo, Simon-Pierre Gorza, Serge Massar
ID: 2602.18110
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.18110

Abstract:
Reservoir computing leverages nonlinear dynamics of physical systems to process
temporal information with minimal training cost. Here, we demonstrate that
cavity solitons sustained in a fiber optical cavity provide an optical platform
for photonic reservoir computing. Our methodology exploits the use of a phase-
modulated drive laser to encode the input, while the reservoir states are
accessed through frequency-resolved readout. Numerical simulations indicate that
the emission of Kelly waves enriches the dynamics and enhances performance for
machine learning tasks. We evaluate the performance of the cavity-soliton
reservoir computer on several standard benchmark tasks.
