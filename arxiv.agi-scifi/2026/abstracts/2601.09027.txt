Title: Agentic AI and Machine Learning for Accelerated Materials Discovery and
Applications
Authors: Jihua Chen, Panagiotis Christakopoulos, Karuna D. Chen, Ilia N. Ivanov,
Rigoberto Advincula
ID: 2601.09027
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.09027

Abstract:
Artificial Intelligence (AI), especially AI agents, is increasingly being
applied to chemistry, healthcare, and manufacturing to enhance productivity. In
this review, we discuss the progress of AI and agentic AI in areas related to,
and beyond polymer materials and discovery chemistry. More specifically, the
focus is on the need for efficient discovery, core concepts, and large language
models. Consequently, applications are showcased in scenarios such as (1) flow
chemistry, (2) biosensors, and (3) batteries.
