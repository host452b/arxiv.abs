Title: Two imaginarity monotones induced by unified $(α,β)$-relative entropy
Authors: Chuanfa Wu, Zhaoqi Wu
ID: 2506.09799
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.09799

Abstract:
Complex numbers play a pivotal role in both mathematics and physics,
particularly in quantum mechanics, and are extensively utilized to depict the
behavior of microscopic particles. Recognizing the significance of complex
numbers, a framework of imaginarity resource theory has recently been
established. In this work, we propose two types of imaginarity monotones induced
by the unified $(α,β)$-relative entropy and investigate their properties.
Moreover, we give explicit examples to illustrate our results.
