Title: Phase space tableau simulation for quantum computation
Authors: Selman Ipek, Atak Talay Yucel, Farzad Shahi, Cagdas Ozdemir, Cihan Okay
ID: 2506.04033
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.04033

Abstract:
We introduce a novel tableau-based classical simulation method for quantum
computation, formulated within the phase space framework of the extended
stabilizer theory of closed non-contextual operators. This method enables the
efficient classical simulation of a broader class of quantum circuits beyond the
stabilizer formalism. We implement the simulator and benchmark its performance
on basic quantum algorithms, including the hidden shift and Deutsch--Jozsa
algorithms.
