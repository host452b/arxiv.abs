Title: MusicScaffold: Bridging Machine Efficiency and Human Growth in Adolescent
Creative Education through Generative AI
Authors: Zhejing Hu, Yan Liu, Zhi Zhang, Gong Chen, Bruce X. B. Yu, Junxian Li,
Jiannong Cao
ID: 2509.10327
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.10327

Abstract:
Adolescence is marked by strong creative impulses but limited strategies for
structured expression, often leading to frustration or disengagement. While
generative AI lowers technical barriers and delivers efficient outputs, its role
in fostering adolescents' expressive growth has been overlooked. We propose
MusicScaffold, the first adolescent-centered framework that repositions AI as a
guide, coach, and partner, making expressive strategies transparent and
learnable, and supporting autonomy. In a four-week study with middle school
students (ages 12--14), MusicScaffold enhanced cognitive specificity, behavioral
self-regulation, and affective confidence in music creation. By reframing
generative AI as a scaffold rather than a generator, this work bridges the
machine efficiency of generative systems with human growth in adolescent
creative education.
