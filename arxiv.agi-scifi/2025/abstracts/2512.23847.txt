Title: A Test of Lookahead Bias in LLM Forecasts
Authors: Zhenyu Gao, Wenxi Jiang, Yutong Yan
ID: 2512.23847
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.23847

Abstract:
We develop a statistical test to detect lookahead bias in economic forecasts
generated by large language models (LLMs). Using state-of-the-art pre-training
data detection techniques, we estimate the likelihood that a given prompt
appeared in an LLM's training corpus, a statistic we term Lookahead Propensity
(LAP). We formally show that a positive correlation between LAP and forecast
accuracy indicates the presence and magnitude of lookahead bias, and apply the
test to two forecasting tasks: news headlines predicting stock returns and
earnings call transcripts predicting capital expenditures. Our test provides a
cost-efficient, diagnostic tool for assessing the validity and reliability of
LLM-generated forecasts.
