Title: A Composable Agentic System for Automated Visual Data Reporting
Authors: Péter Ferenc Gyarmati, Dominik Moritz, Torsten Möller, Laura Koesten
ID: 2509.05721
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.05721

Abstract:
To address the brittleness of monolithic AI agents, our prototype for automated
visual data reporting explores a Human-AI Partnership model. Its hybrid, multi-
agent architecture strategically externalizes logic from LLMs to deterministic
modules, leveraging the rule-based system Draco for principled visualization
design. The system delivers a dual-output: an interactive Observable report with
Mosaic for reader exploration, and executable Marimo notebooks for deep,
analyst-facing traceability. This granular architecture yields a fully automatic
yet auditable and steerable system, charting a path toward a more synergistic
partnership between human experts and AI. For reproducibility, our
implementation and examples are available at https://peter-
gy.github.io/VISxGenAI-2025/.
