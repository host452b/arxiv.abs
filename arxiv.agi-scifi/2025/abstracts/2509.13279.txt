Title: HARMONIC: A Content-Centric Cognitive Robotic Architecture
Authors: Sanjay Oruganti, Sergei Nirenburg, Marjorie McShane, Jesse English,
Michael K. Roberts, Christian Arndt, Carlos Gonzalez, Mingyo Seo, Luis Sentis
ID: 2509.13279
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.13279

Abstract:
This paper introduces HARMONIC, a cognitive-robotic architecture designed for
robots in human-robotic teams. HARMONIC supports semantic perception
interpretation, human-like decision-making, and intentional language
communication. It addresses the issues of safety and quality of results; aims to
solve problems of data scarcity, explainability, and safety; and promotes
transparency and trust. Two proof-of-concept HARMONIC-based robotic systems are
demonstrated, each implemented in both a high-fidelity simulation environment
and on physical robotic platforms.
