Title: A Review on Generative AI For Text-To-Image and Image-To-Image Generation
and Implications To Scientific Images
Authors: Zineb Sordo, Eric Chagnon, Daniela Ushizima
ID: 2502.21151
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.21151

Abstract:
This review surveys the state-of-the-art in text-to-image and image-to-image
generation within the scope of generative AI. We provide a comparative analysis
of three prominent architectures: Variational Autoencoders, Generative
Adversarial Networks and Diffusion Models. For each, we elucidate core concepts,
architectural innovations, and practical strengths and limitations, particularly
for scientific image understanding. Finally, we discuss critical open challenges
and potential future research directions in this rapidly evolving field.
