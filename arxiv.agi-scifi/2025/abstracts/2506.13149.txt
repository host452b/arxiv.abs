Title: Cognitive Synergy Architecture: SEGO for Human-Centric Collaborative
Robots
Authors: Jaehong Oh
ID: 2506.13149
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.13149

Abstract:
This paper presents SEGO (Semantic Graph Ontology), a cognitive mapping
architecture designed to integrate geometric perception, semantic reasoning, and
explanation generation into a unified framework for human-centric collaborative
robotics. SEGO constructs dynamic cognitive scene graphs that represent not only
the spatial configuration of the environment but also the semantic relations and
ontological consistency among detected objects. The architecture seamlessly
combines SLAM-based localization, deep-learning-based object detection and
tracking, and ontology-driven reasoning to enable real-time, semantically
coherent mapping.
