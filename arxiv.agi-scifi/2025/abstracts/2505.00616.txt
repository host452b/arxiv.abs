Title: Catastrophic Liability: Managing Systemic Risks in Frontier AI
Development
Authors: Aidan Kierans, Kaley Rittichier, Utku Sonsayar, Avijit Ghosh
ID: 2505.00616
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.00616

Abstract:
As artificial intelligence systems grow more capable and autonomous, frontier AI
development poses potential systemic risks that could affect society at a
massive scale. Current practices at many AI labs developing these systems lack
sufficient transparency around safety measures, testing procedures, and
governance structures. This opacity makes it challenging to verify safety claims
or establish appropriate liability when harm occurs. Drawing on liability
frameworks from nuclear energy, aviation software, cybersecurity, and
healthcare, we propose a comprehensive approach to safety documentation and
accountability in frontier AI development.
