Title: Voltage Mode Winner-Take-All Circuit for Neuromorphic Systems
Authors: Abdullah M. Zyarah, Dhireesha Kudithipudi
ID: 2507.04338
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.04338

Abstract:
Recent advances in neuromorphic computing demonstrate on-device learning
capabilities with low power consumption. One of the key learning units in these
systems is the winner-take-all circuit. In this research, we propose a winner-
take-all circuit that can be configured to achieve k-winner and hysteresis
properties, simulated in IBM 65 nm node. The circuit dissipated 34.9 $μ$W of
power with a latency of 10.4 ns, while processing 1000 inputs. The utility of
the circuit is demonstrated for spatial filtering and classification.
