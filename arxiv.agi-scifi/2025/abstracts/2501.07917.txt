Abstract:
This roadmap consolidates recent advances while exploring emerging applications,
reflecting the remarkable diversity of hardware platforms, neuromorphic
concepts, and implementation philosophies reported in the field. It emphasizes
the critical role of cross-disciplinary collaboration in this rapidly evolving
field.
