Title: LLM Architecture, Scaling Laws, and Economics: A Quick Summary
Authors: William H. Press
ID: 2511.11572
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.11572

Abstract:
The current standard architecture of Large Language Models (LLMs) with QKV self-
attention is briefly summarized, including the architecture of a typical
Transformer. Scaling laws for compute (flops) and memory (parameters plus data)
are given, along with their present (2025) rough cost estimates for the
parameters of present LLMs of various scales, including discussion of whether
DeepSeek should be viewed as a special case. Nothing here is new, but this
material seems not otherwise readily available in summary form.
