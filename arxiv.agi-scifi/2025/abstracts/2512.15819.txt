Title: Introductory Courses on Digital Twins: an Experience Report
Authors: John S Fitzgerald, Philip James, Cláudio Gomes, Peter Gorm Larsen
ID: 2512.15819
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15819

Abstract:
We describe and compare two new courses on model-based approaches to the
engineering of Digital Twins. One course was delivered to doctoral students from
a range of largely non-computational backgrounds, and the other to Masters
students with computing experience. We describe the goals, content and delivery
of the courses, and review experience gained to date. Key lessons focus on the
importance of providing common baselines for participants coming from diverse
technical backgrounds.
