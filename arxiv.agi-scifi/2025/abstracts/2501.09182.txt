Title: A Blockchain-Enabled Approach to Cross-Border Compliance and Trust
Authors: Vikram Kulothungan
ID: 2501.09182
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.09182

Abstract:
As artificial intelligence (AI) systems become increasingly integral to critical
infrastructure and global operations, the need for a unified, trustworthy
governance framework is more urgent that ever. This paper proposes a novel
approach to AI governance, utilizing blockchain and distributed ledger
technologies (DLT) to establish a decentralized, globally recognized framework
that ensures security, privacy, and trustworthiness of AI systems across
borders. The paper presents specific implementation scenarios within the
financial sector, outlines a phased deployment timeline over the next decade,
and addresses potential challenges with solutions grounded in current research.
By synthesizing advancements in blockchain, AI ethics, and cybersecurity, this
paper offers a comprehensive roadmap for a decentralized AI governance framework
capable of adapting to the complex and evolving landscape of global AI
regulation.
