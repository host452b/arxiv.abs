Title: Towards Effective Human-in-the-Loop Assistive AI Agents
Authors: Filippos Bellos, Yayuan Li, Cary Shu, Ruey Day, Jeffrey M. Siskind,
Jason J. Corso
ID: 2507.18374
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.18374

Abstract:
Effective human-AI collaboration for physical task completion has significant
potential in both everyday activities and professional domains. AI agents
equipped with informative guidance can enhance human performance, but evaluating
such collaboration remains challenging due to the complexity of human-in-the-
loop interactions. In this work, we introduce an evaluation framework and a
multimodal dataset of human-AI interactions designed to assess how AI guidance
affects procedural task performance, error reduction and learning outcomes.
Besides, we develop an augmented reality (AR)-equipped AI agent that provides
interactive guidance in real-world tasks, from cooking to battlefield medicine.
Through human studies, we share empirical insights into AI-assisted human
performance and demonstrate that AI-assisted collaboration improves task
completion.
