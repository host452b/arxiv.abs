Title: Trainable dynamical masking for readout-free optical computing
Authors: S. Bogdanov, E. Manuylovich, S. K. Turitsyn
ID: 2505.23464
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.23464

Abstract:
Nonlinear systems, transforming an input signal into a high-dimensional output
feature space, can be used for non-conventional computing. This approach,
however, requires a change of system parameters during training rather than
coefficients in a software program. We propose here to use available off-the-
shelf high-speed optical communication devices and technologies to implement a
trainable dynamical mask in addition to or even instead of the traditional
readout layer for extreme learning machine-based computing. The computational
potential of the proposed approach is demonstrated with both regression and time
series prediction tasks.
