Title: How do Humans take an Object from a Robot: Behavior changes observed in a
User Study
Authors: Parag Khanna, Elmira Yadollahi, Iolanda Leite, Mårten Björkman,
Christian Smith
ID: 2501.02127
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.02127

Abstract:
To facilitate human-robot interaction and gain human trust, a robot should
recognize and adapt to changes in human behavior. This work documents different
human behaviors observed while taking objects from an interactive robot in an
experimental study, categorized across two dimensions: pull force applied and
handedness. We also present the changes observed in human behavior upon repeated
interaction with the robot to take various objects.
