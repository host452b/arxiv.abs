Title: A Comprehensive Survey on Generative AI for Video-to-Music Generation
Authors: Shulei Ji, Songruoyao Wu, Zihao Wang, Shuyu Li, Kejun Zhang
ID: 2502.12489
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.12489

Abstract:
The burgeoning growth of video-to-music generation can be attributed to the
ascendancy of multimodal generative models. However, there is a lack of
literature that comprehensively combs through the work in this field. To fill
this gap, this paper presents a comprehensive review of video-to-music
generation using deep generative AI techniques, focusing on three key
components: conditioning input construction, conditioning mechanism, and music
generation frameworks. We categorize existing approaches based on their designs
for each component, clarifying the roles of different strategies. Preceding
this, we provide a fine-grained categorization of video and music modalities,
illustrating how different categories influence the design of components within
the generation pipelines. Furthermore, we summarize available multimodal
datasets and evaluation metrics while highlighting ongoing challenges in the
field.
