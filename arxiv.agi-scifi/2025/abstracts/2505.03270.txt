Title: Power-Aware Digital Twin of Coherent Optical Receiver
Authors: Ambashri Purkayastha, Camille Delezoide, Fabien Boitier, Vinod Bajaj,
Mounia Lourdiane, Cédric Ware, Patricia Layec
ID: 2505.03270
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.03270

Abstract:
We propose a digital twin of coherent receivers based on an extended physical
model for predicting quality of transmission under receiver power variations. We
experimentally validate it, demonstrating an accuracy improved by up to 1.5dB.
