Title: Digital Twin for Estimating QoT Statistics in Presence of PDL and
Transceiver Imperfections
Authors: Ambashri Purkayastha, Camille Delezoide, Vinod Bajaj, Mounia Lourdiane,
Cédric Ware, Patricia Layec
ID: 2511.07005
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.07005

Abstract:
We propose a physics-based digital twin to predict the statistical QoT
distribution of a realistic optical lightpath. We demonstrate up to 0.73 dB
accuracy improvement in worst-case SNR prediction for short distance
transmissions in linear regime.
