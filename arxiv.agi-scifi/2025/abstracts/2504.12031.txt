Title: Proof-Carrying Neuro-Symbolic Code
Authors: Ekaterina Komendantskaya
ID: 2504.12031
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.12031

Abstract:
This invited paper introduces the concept of "proof-carrying neuro-symbolic
code" and explains its meaning and value, from both the "neural" and the
"symbolic" perspectives. The talk outlines the first successes and challenges
that this new area of research faces.
