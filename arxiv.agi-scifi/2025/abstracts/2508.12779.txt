Title: Application of Quantum Annealing to Computation of Molecular Properties
Authors: Pradyot Pritam Sahoo, V. S. Prasannaa, B. P. Das
ID: 2508.12779
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.12779

Abstract:
We present the results of our quantum annealing computations of the permanent
electric dipole
  moments of several molecules. By applying an electric field as a perturbation
and measuring the
  corresponding energy responses, the molecular electric dipole moments are
obtained numerically
  through the finite field method. The ground-state electronic wavefunctions and
energies are obtained
  using the quantum annealer eignsolver algorithm. This work provides a pathway
for the computation
  of molecular properties in the quantum annealing paradigm.
