Title: On Weighted Entropy Generating Function
Authors: Smitha S., Mary Andrewsa, Sudheesh K. Kattumannil
ID: 2507.15057
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.15057

Abstract:
In this paper, we study the properties of the weighted entropy generating
function (WEGF). We also introduce the weighted residual entropy generating
function (WREGF) and establish some characterization results based on its
connections with the hazard rate and the mean residual life function.
Furthermore, we propose two new classes of life distributions derived from
WREGF. We also study the non-parametric estimation of WREGF. A non-parametric
test for the Pareto type I distribution is developed based on entropy
characterization. To evaluate the performance of the test statistics, we conduct
an extensive Monte Carlo simulation study. Finally, we apply the proposed method
to two real-life datasets.
