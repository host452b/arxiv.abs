Title: Aristotle: IMO-level Automated Theorem Proving
Authors: Tudor Achim, Alex Best, Alberto Bietti, Kevin Der, Mathïs Fédérico,
Sergei Gukov, Daniel Halpern-Leistner, Kirsten Henningsgard, Yury Kudryashov,
Alexander Meiburg, Martin Michelsen, Riley Patterson, Eric Rodriguez, Laura
Scharff, Vikram Shanker, Vladmir Sicca, Hari Sowrirajan, Aidan Swope, Matyas
Tamas, Vlad Tenev, Jonathan Thomm, Harold Williams, Lawrence Wu
ID: 2510.01346
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.01346

Abstract:
We introduce Aristotle, an AI system that combines formal verification with
informal reasoning, achieving gold-medal-equivalent performance on the 2025
International Mathematical Olympiad problems. Aristotle integrates three main
components: a Lean proof search system, an informal reasoning system that
generates and formalizes lemmas, and a dedicated geometry solver. Our system
demonstrates state-of-the-art performance with favorable scaling properties for
automated theorem proving.
