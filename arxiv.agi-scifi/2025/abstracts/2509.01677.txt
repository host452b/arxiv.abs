Title: Machine Learning in the 2HDM2S model for Dark Matter
Authors: Rafael Boto, Tiago P. Rebelo, Jorge C. Romão, João P. Silva
ID: 2509.01677
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.01677

Abstract:
We introduce a two real scalar singlet extension of the two Higgs doublet model.
We study the vacuum structure, the bounded from below conditions, the
restrictions from the oblique parameters S,T and U, as well as the unitarity
constraints. We submit the model to collider and Dark Matter experimental
constraints and explore its allowed parameter space. We compare randomly
populated simulations, simulations starting near the alignment limit, and a
Machine Learning based exploration. Using Evolutionary Strategies, we
efficiently search for regions with two Dark Matter candidates.
