Title: E2E Parking Dataset: An Open Benchmark for End-to-End Autonomous Parking
Authors: Kejia Gao, Liguo Zhou, Mingjun Liu, Alois Knoll
ID: 2504.10812
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.10812

Abstract:
End-to-end learning has shown great potential in autonomous parking, yet the
lack of publicly available datasets limits reproducibility and benchmarking.
While prior work introduced a visual-based parking model and a pipeline for data
generation, training, and close-loop test, the dataset itself was not released.
To bridge this gap, we create and open-source a high-quality dataset for end-to-
end autonomous parking. Using the original model, we achieve an overall success
rate of 85.16% with lower average position and orientation errors (0.24 meters
and 0.34 degrees).
