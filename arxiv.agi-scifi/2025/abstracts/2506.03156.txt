Title: Gauging Growth: AGI Mathematical Metrics for Economic Progress
Authors: Davit Gondauri
ID: 2506.03156
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.03156

Abstract:
Today, the economy is greatly influenced by Artificial General Intelligence
(AGI). The purpose of this paper is to determine the impact of the quantitative
relations of AGI on the country's economic parameters. The authors use the
analysis of historical data in the research, develop a new mathematical
algorithm that refers to the level of AGI development, and conduct a regression
analysis. The economic effect of AGI is deduced if it affects the growth of real
GDP. As a result of the analysis, it is revealed that there is a positive
Pearson correlation between the growth of AGI and real GDP; that is, to increase
GDP by 1%, an average increase of 12.5% of AGI is required.
