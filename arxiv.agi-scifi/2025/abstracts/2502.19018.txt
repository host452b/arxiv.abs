Title: An explicit Enriques surface with an automorphism of minimum entropy
Authors: Simon Brandhorst, Matthias Zach
ID: 2502.19018
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.19018

Abstract:
We derive explicit equations for the Oguiso-Yu automorphism of minimum
topological entropy on a complex Enriques surface. The approach is computer
aided and makes use of elliptic fibrations.
