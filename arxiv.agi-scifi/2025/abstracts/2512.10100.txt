Title: Robust AI Security and Alignment: A Sisyphean Endeavor?
Authors: Apostol Vassilev
ID: 2512.10100
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10100

Abstract:
This manuscript establishes information-theoretic limitations for robustness of
AI security and alignment by extending Gödel's incompleteness theorem to AI.
Knowing these limitations and preparing for the challenges they bring is
critically important for the responsible adoption of the AI technology.
Practical approaches to dealing with these challenges are provided as well.
Broader implications for cognitive reasoning limitations of AI systems are also
proven.
