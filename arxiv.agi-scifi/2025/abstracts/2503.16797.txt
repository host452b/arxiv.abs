Title: A Learnability Analysis on Neuro-Symbolic Learning
Authors: Hao-Yuan He, Ming Li
ID: 2503.16797
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.16797

Abstract:
This paper analyzes the learnability of neuro-symbolic (NeSy) tasks within
hybrid systems. We show that the learnability of NeSy tasks can be characterized
by their derived constraint satisfaction problems (DCSPs). Specifically, a task
is learnable if the corresponding DCSP has a unique solution; otherwise, it is
unlearnable. For learnable tasks, we establish error bounds by exploiting the
clustering property of the hypothesis space. Additionally, we analyze the
asymptotic error for general NeSy tasks, showing that the expected error scales
with the disagreement among solutions. Our results offer a principled approach
to determining learnability and provide insights into the design of new
algorithms.
