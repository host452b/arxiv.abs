Title: Prediction of Permissioned Blockchain Performance for Resource Scaling
Configurations
Authors: Seungwoo Jung, Yeonho Yoo, Gyeongsik Yang, Chuck Yoo
ID: 2503.15769
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.15769

Abstract:
Blockchain is increasingly offered as blockchain-as-a-service (BaaS) by cloud
service providers. However, configuring BaaS appropriately for optimal
performance and reliability resorts to try-and-error. A key challenge is that
BaaS is often perceived as a ``black-box,'' leading to uncertainties in
performance and resource provisioning. Previous studies attempted to address
this challenge; however, the impacts of both vertical and horizontal scaling
remain elusive. To this end, we present machine learning-based models to predict
network reliability and throughput based on scaling configurations. In our
evaluation, the models exhibit prediction errors of ~1.9%, which is highly
accurate and can be applied in the real-world.
