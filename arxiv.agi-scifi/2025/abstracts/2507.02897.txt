Title: Regulation Compliant AI for Fusion: Real-Time Image Analysis-Based
Control of Divertor Detachment in Tokamaks
Authors: Nathaniel Chen, Cheolsik Byun, Azarakash Jalalvand, Sangkyeun Kim,
Andrew Rothstein, Filippo Scotti, Steve Allen, David Eldon, Keith Erickson,
Egemen Kolemen
ID: 2507.02897
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.02897

Abstract:
While artificial intelligence (AI) has been promising for fusion control, its
inherent black-box nature will make compliant implementation in regulatory
environments a challenge. This study implements and validates a real-time AI
enabled linear and interpretable control system for successful divertor
detachment control with the DIII-D lower divertor camera. Using D2 gas, we
demonstrate feedback divertor detachment control with a mean absolute difference
of 2% from the target for both detachment and reattachment. This automatic
training and linear processing framework can be extended to any image based
diagnostic for regulatory compliant controller necessary for future fusion
reactors.
