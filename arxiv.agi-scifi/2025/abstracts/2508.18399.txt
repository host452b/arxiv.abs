Title: Maintenance automation: methods for robotics manipulation planning and
execution
Authors: Christian Friedrich, Ralf Gulde, Armin Lechler, Alexander Verl
ID: 2508.18399
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.18399

Abstract:
Automating complex tasks using robotic systems requires skills for planning,
control and execution. This paper proposes a complete robotic system for
maintenance automation, which can automate disassembly and assembly operations
under environmental uncertainties (e.g. deviations between prior plan
information). The cognition of the robotic system is based on a planning
approach (using CAD and RGBD data) and includes a method to interpret a symbolic
plan and transform it to a set of executable robot instructions. The complete
system is experimentally evaluated using real-world applications. This work
shows the first step to transfer these theoretical results into a practical
robotic solution.
