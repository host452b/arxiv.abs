Title: Machine learning in lattice quantum gravity
Authors: Jan Ambjorn, Zbigniew Drogosz, Jakub Gizbert-Studnicki, Andrzej
Görlich, Dániel Németh, Marcus Reitz
ID: 2510.02159
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.02159

Abstract:
Using numerical data coming from Monte Carlo simulations of four-dimensional
Causal Dynamical Triangulations, we study how automated machine learning
algorithms can be used to recognize transitions between different phases of
quantum geometries observed in lattice quantum gravity. We tested seven
supervised and seven unsupervised machine learning models and found that most of
them were very successful in that task, even outperforming standard methods
based on order parameters.
