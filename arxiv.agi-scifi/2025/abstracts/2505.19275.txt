Title: Agentic Information Theory: Ergodicity and Intrinsic Semantics of
Information Processes
Authors: James P. Crutchfield, Alexandra Jurgens
ID: 2505.19275
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.19275

Abstract:
We develop information theory for the temporal behavior of memoryful agents
moving through complex -- structured, stochastic -- environments. We introduce
and explore information processes -- stochastic processes produced by cognitive
agents in real-time as they interact with and interpret incoming stimuli. We
provide basic results on the ergodicity and semantics of the resulting time
series of Shannon information measures that monitor an agent's adapting view of
uncertainty and structural correlation in its environment.
