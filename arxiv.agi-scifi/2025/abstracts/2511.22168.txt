Title: Composite AdS geodesics for CFT correlators and timelike entanglement
entropy
Authors: Hardik Bohra, Allic Sivaramakrishnan
ID: 2511.22168
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.22168

Abstract:
We study how to recover timelike worldlines in AdS from CFT data as a toy model
for holographically reconstructing realistic observers. We give a bulk
extremization procedure that determines composite timelike-spacelike geodesics
that connect timelike-separated boundary points. The total geodesic length
matches the length extracted from CFT correlators at the timelike-separated
points. We show agreement in Poincaré AdS, for generic boundary points in global
AdS, and also for the BTZ solution, in which the timelike segment probes behind
the horizon. We refine related methods to compute timelike entanglement entropy
in AdS$_3$/CFT$_2$ and recover known results.
