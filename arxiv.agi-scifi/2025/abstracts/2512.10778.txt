Title: Building Audio-Visual Digital Twins with Smartphones
Authors: Zitong Lan, Yiwei Tang, Yuhan Wang, Haowen Lai, Yiduo Hao, Mingmin Zhao
ID: 2512.10778
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10778

Abstract:
Digital twins today are almost entirely visual, overlooking acoustics-a core
component of spatial realism and interaction. We introduce AV-Twin, the first
practical system that constructs editable audio-visual digital twins using only
commodity smartphones. AV-Twin combines mobile RIR capture and a visual-assisted
acoustic field model to efficiently reconstruct room acoustics. It further
recovers per-surface material properties through differentiable acoustic
rendering, enabling users to modify materials, geometry, and layout while
automatically updating both audio and visuals. Together, these capabilities
establish a practical path toward fully modifiable audio-visual digital twins
for real-world environments.
