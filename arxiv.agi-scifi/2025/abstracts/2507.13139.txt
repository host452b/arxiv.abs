Title: Entropy-minimizing diffeomorphisms of pseudo-Anosov type on K3 surfaces
Authors: Benson Farb, Eduard Looijenga
ID: 2507.13139
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.13139

Abstract:
We construct diffeomorphisms of ``pseudo-Anosov type'' on K3 surfaces M. In
particular we obtain infinitely many examples of such diffeomorphisms that
minimize entropy in their homotopy class, and for which neither the
diffeomorphism nor any diffeomorphism homotopic to it preserves any complex
structure on M.
