Title: Optical SETI at ESO in the 2040s
Authors: Valenitn D. Ivanov
ID: 2512.18903
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.18903

Abstract:
The searches for other life and for intelligence are fundamental problems that
science faces today. Most searches so far have been focused on radio, but
optical laser communication is an alternative, well suited for a ground-based
observatory. A project to search for artificial laser communications with the
current and future extreme multiplexity spectroscopic facilities that ESO may
develop by the 2040s is outlined. The monochromatic light is a clearly
identifiable technosignature. The enormous corollary outreach potential of this
initiative is underlined.
