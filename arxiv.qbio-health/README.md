# arxiv.qbio-health — Key Health Factors Across the Human Lifespan (0–80)

**1,125 arXiv abstracts** from 7 targeted searches in the `q-bio` category, covering the most evidence-backed factors for human health across all life stages.

**Core principle (Pareto 80/20):** Identify the ~20% of key factors that drive ~80% of health outcomes — for practical reference by ordinary people.

## Disease Risk by Age & Sex — Evidence from Abstracts

> Sampled from paper abstracts/conclusions. Each stage lists the highest-probability disease per sex and the evidence-backed prevention strategy. Paper citations in `[brackets]` refer to the reference list at the end of this section.

### Ages 0–5 | Neural & Immune Foundation
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|
| Male | Sleep-disordered breathing (OSA; 1–5% of school-age children; boys have stronger persistent short-sleep trajectories) | [Winn 2020] | Reduce allergens (dust mites, mold); side-sleeping posture; avoid obesity |
| Female | Vitamin D deficiency → impaired sleep & immunity (cord-blood 25OHD < 12 ng/ml significantly increases short-sleep risk in toddlers) | [Plancoulaine 2019] | Vitamin D 400–1000 IU/day; outdoor sunlight exposure |

### Ages 5–10 | Bone & Cognitive Acceleration
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|
| Male | Asthma (slightly higher prevalence in boys; low indoor microbial diversity is an independent risk factor) | [Fu 2019] | Improve indoor microbial diversity; reduce dust mites and mold |
| Female | Asthma; suboptimal peak bone density accumulation | [Fu 2019] | Daily impact exercise (jump rope) ≥ 60 min; adequate calcium intake |

### Ages 10–15 | Puberty Bone & Hormone Peak Window
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|
| Male | Chronic sleep deprivation syndrome (circadian clock shift vs. early school start; persistent short-sleep patterns stronger in boys) | [Plancoulaine 2019] | Protect 8–10 h sleep; avoid forced early wake; social support for mental health |
| Female | Dieting-induced peak bone density impairment; iron-deficiency anemia (90% of peak bone mass accumulates before 18 — irreversible) | [Plancoulaine 2019] | Calcium 1200 mg/day + protein 1.2 g/kg/day; iron-rich foods; refuse calorie restriction |

### Ages 15–20 | Physical Peak Establishment
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|
| Male | Smoking/alcohol-induced irreversible vascular endothelial damage (teenage male smoking rates far exceed females globally) | [Sun S. 2025] | Zero smoking (any amount causes damage); limit alcohol; build aerobic capacity |
| Female | Cervical cancer (HPV risk rises after sexual debut; vaccine efficacy highest before 20); depression/anxiety (2× male rate) | [Jarynowski 2015] | **HPV vaccine before age 20** (cost-effective: 5–10k EUR/QALY); aerobic exercise reduces anxiety/depression |

### Ages 20–25 | Metabolic & Habit Setting
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|
| Male | Early metabolic syndrome; cardiovascular risk trajectory (sedentary + high-sugar diet initiates insulin resistance; VO₂max in 20s predicts long-term cardiac health) | [Brinks 2017] | Establish 3–5× weekly exercise; quit smoking; first metabolic blood panel baseline |
| Female | Cervical cancer (HPV screening window); depression/anxiety (peak first-onset decade) | [Jarynowski 2015] | Annual HPV/Pap screening; regular aerobic exercise (strongest evidence-based depression prevention) |

### Ages 25–30 | Fertility & Stress Management
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|
| Male | Early hypertension; pre-diabetes (chronic stress → elevated cortisol → insulin resistance onset) | [Corson 2024] | **First annual metabolic blood panel** (BP / fasting glucose / lipids / Vit D / CRP); strength training |
| Female | Pregnancy-related complications; postpartum depression (sedentary pregnancy → gestational diabetes + depression) | [Corson 2024] | Moderate-intensity aerobic exercise during pregnancy (significantly reduces depression and gestational diabetes risk) |

### Ages 30–35 | Early Metabolic Slowdown
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|
| Male | Coronary artery disease / hypertension (risk accumulating rapidly); pre-diabetes | [Su 2023] | Control BP < 130/85; fasting glucose monitoring; 2–3× weekly resistance training |
| Female | Breast cancer risk rising; rheumatoid arthritis (female incidence 3× male; gut dysbiosis drives bone erosion) | [Su 2023] | Monthly breast self-exam; gut microbiota diversification (30+ plant species/week); watch for symmetric joint pain |

### Ages 35–40 | Hormone Shift Warning Period
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|
| Male | **Myocardial infarction** (men develop CVD 10–15 years earlier than women); type 2 diabetes | [Sun S. 2025] | Omega-3 supplementation (OR 0.17 for MI in trial); **4-metric metabolic screen** (waist / BP / glucose / lipids) |
| Female | **Breast cancer** (mammography from age 40); thyroid disease (female incidence 5–10× male) | [Reza 2025] | Annual mammogram from age 40; thyroid function (TSH/FT4) annual check; blood glucose control |

### Ages 40–45 | Metabolic Inflection Point
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|
| Male | **Acute MI** (peak-onset decade for men); type 2 diabetes (insulin sensitivity drops sharply; brain ~8+ years older in diabetics/hypertensives by sleep EEG) | [Sun H. 2018] | HbA1c + fasting insulin annual test; 16:8 intermittent fasting; Framingham CV risk score |
| Female | **Breast cancer** (40–70 = highest incidence window); metabolic syndrome | [Reza 2025] | Annual mammogram; blood glucose control (diabetes increases breast cancer risk 20–27%); control visceral fat |

### Ages 45–50 | Menopause Transition / Male Androgen Decline
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|

| Female | **Osteoporosis** (estrogen drop → bone density loss 1–2%/year); cardiovascular risk rapidly converging with males | [Kamml 2023] | **First DEXA bone density scan**; BP target < 120/80; weight-bearing + impact exercise ≥ 3×/week |

### Ages 50–55 | Sarcopenia & Cognitive Protection
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|

| Female | **Breast cancer** (50–70 = peak incidence); osteoporotic fracture | [Sun H. 2018] | Annual mammogram; DEXA-guided treatment decision; 30–40 g protein/meal + strength training |

### Ages 55–60 | Fall Risk & Balance Intervention Window
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|

| Female | **Alzheimer's disease / MCI** (female risk rapidly exceeds male post-menopause); hip fracture | [Mai 2023] | **Neurofeedback / cognitive training for MCI** (evidence-backed); 10–15 min daily balance practice; fall prevention |

### Ages 60–65 | Social Connection & Functional Independence
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|

| Female | **Alzheimer's disease** (65+ women: 50–70% higher prevalence than men); hip fracture | [Harach 2015] | **Gut microbiota intervention** (microbiota causally linked to amyloid pathology); cognitive engagement ≥ 3×/week; Tai Chi |

### Ages 65–70 | Pre-Frailty Prevention
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|
| Male | **Cardiovascular death** (dominant); prostate cancer complications; cognitive decline | [Mayor 2023] | Functional strength training (grip strength = whole-body function proxy); polypharmacy annual review |
| Female | **Alzheimer's disease** (incidence 2× male); osteoporotic fractures | [Mayor 2023] [Keresztes 2021] | **Intermittent fasting / caloric restriction** (activates AMPK/mTOR neuroprotective pathways); social engagement |

### Ages 70–75 | Fragile Bone & Last Cognitive Protection Window
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|
| Male | **Cardiovascular disease** (leading cause); pneumonia (declining immunity); Parkinson's (RBD screening window) | [Kim 2019] | **Pneumococcal vaccine**; RBD sleep screening (PD/AD early signal 10–20 years before motor symptoms); Vit D 800–2000 IU/day |
| Female | **Alzheimer's / dementia** (70+ women: 2× male prevalence); hip fracture (1-year mortality 20–30%) | [Alegre 2025] | **Sleep quality monitoring** (reduced REM/dreaming is discriminative AD early signal); probiotics / FMT; home fall-proofing |

### Ages 75–80 | Quality of Life & Functional Independence
| Sex | Top Disease Risk | Paper | Prevention |
|-----|----------------|-------|-----------|
| Male | **Cardiovascular disease** (dominant ~40% of deaths); frailty syndrome; pneumonia | [Mayor 2023] | Any form of daily activity (walking / gardening / household chores); pneumococcal vaccine; 25–30 g protein/meal |
| Female | **Alzheimer's / dementia** (75+ women: 2–3× male prevalence); post-fracture bed rest complications; malnutrition (3–4× higher in solitary elderly) | [Alegre 2025] | **Social dining** (raises protein/calorie intake + reduces depression); cognitive + social engagement; post-fracture rehab within 48 h |

## Abstracts

## Search Parameters

- Category: `q-bio` (all sub-categories)
- Sort: `relevance`
- Search field: `-t` title search with OR syntax (e.g. `"aging or longevity or lifespan"`)
- Tool: [arxs](https://github.com/host452b/arxs) v1.0.3

## Relevance Filtering

Papers scored by presence of human-health keywords (human subjects, clinical, diet, exercise, sleep, microbiome, mortality, biomarkers, etc.) and penalized for non-human model organisms (C. elegans, yeast, E. coli), physics content, or non-health biology. Papers with negative scores moved to `abstracts_removed/`.
