Title: On a species survival model
Authors: Iddo Ben-Ari, Anastasios Matzavinos, Alexander Roitershtein
ID: 1006.2585
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1006.2585

Abstract:
In this paper we provide some sharp asymptotic results for a stochastic model of species survival recently proposed by Guiol, Marchado, and Schinazi.
