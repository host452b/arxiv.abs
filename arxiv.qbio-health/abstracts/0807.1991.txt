Title: The Dynamic Template Mechanism - A Co-Ordination Role for Shear, Thermal and Electrical Stressors in Muscle Cell Contraction
Authors: J. T. Lofthouse
ID: 0807.1991
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/0807.1991

Abstract:
The Dynamic Template is applied to muscle cells, and is shown to provide a feedback mechanism that can co-ordinate the rearrangements of cytoskeletal proteins that accompany contraction
