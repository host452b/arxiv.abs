Title: Global Illumination of non-Euclidean spaces
Authors: Tiago Novello, Vinicius da Silva, Luiz Velho
ID: 2003.11133
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2003.11133

Abstract:
This paper presents a path tracer algorithm to compute the global illumination of non-Euclidean manifolds. We use the 3D torus as an example.
