Title: Designing Interactions with Furniture: Towards Multi-Sensorial Interaction Design Processes for Interactive Furniture
Authors: Pedro Campos, Nils Ehrenberg, Miguel Campos
ID: 1803.01145
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1803.01145

Abstract:
In this paper, we argue for novel user experience design methods, in the context of reimagining ergonomics of interactive furniture.
