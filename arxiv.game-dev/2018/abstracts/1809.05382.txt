Title: The application features MMC mobile SAGE course of Mathematics
Authors: Kateryna Slovak, Maiia Popel
ID: 1809.05382
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1809.05382

Abstract:
Using SCM higher mathematics course allows you to: more clearly understandable apply theoretical material; deprive students of routine calculations; provide multilevel learning process, and therefore enhances cognitive interest and most importantly - to make learning faster and meaningful.
