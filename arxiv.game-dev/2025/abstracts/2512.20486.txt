Title: The Design of an Interactive Proof Mode for Dafny
Authors: Ştefan Ciobâcă, K. Rustan M. Leino, Ştefan-Alexandru Mercaş, Roxana-Mihaela Timon
ID: 2512.20486
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.20486

Abstract:
We propose to extend the Dafny system with an interactive proof mode. We present a motivating example, how the IPM works, including the main design choices we make, and a prototype implementation.
