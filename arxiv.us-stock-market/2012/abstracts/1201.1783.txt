Title: A Goal Programming Model with Satisfaction Function for Risk Management and Optimal Portfolio Diversification
Authors: Davide La Torre, Marco Maggis
ID: 1201.1783
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1201.1783

We extend the classical risk minimization model with scalar risk measures to the
general case of set-valued risk measures. The problem we obtain is a set-valued
optimization model and we propose a goal programming-based approach with
satisfaction function to obtain a solution which represents the best compromise
between goals and the achievement levels. Numerical examples are provided to
illustrate how the method works in practical situations.
