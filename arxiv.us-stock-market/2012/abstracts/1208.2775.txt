Title: Physical approach to price momentum and its application to momentum strategy
Authors: Jaehyung Choi
ID: 1208.2775
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1208.2775

We introduce various quantitative and mathematical definitions for price
momentum of financial instruments. The price momentum is quantified with
velocity and mass concepts originated from the momentum in physics. By using the
physical momentum of price as a selection criterion, the weekly contrarian
strategies are implemented in South Korea KOSPI 200 and US S&P 500 universes.
The alternative strategies constructed by the physical momentum achieve the
better expected returns and reward-risk measures than those of the traditional
contrarian strategy in weekly scale. The portfolio performance is not understood
by the Fama-French three-factor model.
