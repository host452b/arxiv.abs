Title: Perturbative Expansion of FBSDE in an Incomplete Market with Stochastic Volatility
Authors: Masaaki Fujii, Akihiko Takahashi
ID: 1202.0608
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1202.0608

In this work, we apply our newly proposed perturbative expansion technique to a
quadratic growth FBSDE appearing in an incomplete market with stochastic
volatility that is not perfectly hedgeable. By combining standard asymptotic
expansion technique for the underlying volatility process, we derive explicit
expression for the solution of the FBSDE up to the third order of volatility-of-
volatility, which can be directly translated into the optimal investment
strategy. We compare our approximation with the exact solution, which is known
to be derived by the Cole-Hopf transformation in this popular setup. The result
is very encouraging and shows good accuracy of the approximation up to quite
long maturities. Since our new methodology can be extended straightforwardly to
multi-dimensional setups, we expect it will open real possibilities to obtain
explicit optimal portfolios or hedging strategies under realistic assumptions.
