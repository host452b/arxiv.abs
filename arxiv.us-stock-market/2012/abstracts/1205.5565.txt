Title: Modeling and Pricing of Covariance and Correlation Swaps for Financial Markets with Semi-Markov Volatilities
Authors: Giovanni Salvi, Anatoliy V. Swishchuk
ID: 1205.5565
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1205.5565

In this paper, we model financial markets with semi-Markov volatilities and
price covarinace and correlation swaps for this markets. Numerical evaluations
of vari- nace, volatility, covarinace and correlations swaps with semi-Markov
volatility are presented as well. The novelty of the paper lies in pricing of
volatility swaps in closed form, and pricing of covariance and correlation swaps
in a market with two risky assets.
