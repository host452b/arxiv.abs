Title: Nonlinear Stochastic Model of Return matching to the data of New York and Vilnius Stock Exchanges
Authors: V. Gontis, A. Kononovicius
ID: 1003.5356
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1003.5356

We scale and analyze the empirical data of return from New York and Vilnius
stock exchanges matching it to the same nonlinear double stochastic model of
return in financial market.
