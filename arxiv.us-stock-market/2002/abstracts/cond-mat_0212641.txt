Title: Generalized Technical Analysis. Effects of transaction volume and risk
Authors: M. Ausloos, K. Ivanova
ID: cond-mat/0212641
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/cond-mat/0212641

We generalize the momentum indicator idea taking into account the volume of
transactions as a multiplicative factor. We compare returns obtained following
strategies based on the classical or the generalized technical analysis, taking
into account a sort of risk investor criterion.
