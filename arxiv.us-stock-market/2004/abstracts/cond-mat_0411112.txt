Title: Components of multifractality in high-frequency stock returns
Authors: J. Kwapien, P. Oswiecimka, S. Drozdz
ID: cond-mat/0411112
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/cond-mat/0411112

We analyzed multifractal properties of 5-minute stock returns from a period of
over two years for 100 highly capitalized American companies. The two sources:
fat-tailed probability distributions and nonlinear temporal correlations,
vitally contribute to the observed multifractal dynamics of the returns. For
majority of the companies the temporal correlations constitute a much more
significant related factor, however.
