Title: On pricing of interest rate derivatives
Authors: T. Di Matteo, M. Airoldi, E. Scalas
ID: cond-mat/0401445
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/cond-mat/0401445

At present, there is an explosion of practical interest in the pricing of
interest rate (IR) derivatives. Textbook pricing methods do not take into
account the leptokurticity of the underlying IR process. In this paper, such a
leptokurtic behaviour is illustrated using LIBOR data, and a possible martingale
pricing scheme is discussed.
