Title: Extreme times in financial markets
Authors: Jaume Masoliver, Miquel Montero, Josep Perello
ID: cond-mat/0406556
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/cond-mat/0406556

We apply the theory of continuous time random walks to study some aspects of the
extreme value problem applied to financial time series. We focus our attention
on extreme times, specifically the mean exit time and the mean first-passage
time. We set the general equations for these extremes and evaluate the mean exit
time for actual data.
