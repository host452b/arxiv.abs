Title: A Horserace of Volatility Models for Cryptocurrency: Evidence from Bitcoin Spot and Option Markets
Authors: Yeguang Chi, Wenyan Hao
ID: 2010.07402
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2010.07402

We test various volatility models using the Bitcoin spot price series. Our
models include HIST, EMA ARCH, GARCH, and EGARCH, models. Both of our in-sample-
fit and out-of-sample-forecast results suggest that GARCH and EGARCH models
perform much better than other models. Moreover, the EGARCH model's asymmetric
term is positive and insignificant, which suggests that Bitcoin prices lack the
asymmetric volatility response to past returns. Finally, we formulate an option
trading strategy by exploiting the volatility spread between the GARCH
volatility forecast and the option's implied volatility. We show that a simple
volatility-spread trading strategy with delta-hedging can yield robust profits.
