Title: A general framework for a joint calibration of VIX and VXX options
Authors: Martino Grasselli, Andrea Mazzoran, Andrea Pallavicini
ID: 2012.08353
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2012.08353

We analyze the VIX futures market with a focus on the exchange-traded notes
written on such contracts, in particular we investigate the VXX notes tracking
the short-end part of the futures term structure. Inspired by recent
developments in commodity smile modelling, we present a multi-factor stochastic-
local volatility model that is able to jointly calibrate plain vanilla options
both on VIX futures and VXX notes, thus going beyond the failure of purely
stochastic or simply local volatility models. We discuss numerical results on
real market data by highlighting the impact of model parameters on implied
volatilities.
