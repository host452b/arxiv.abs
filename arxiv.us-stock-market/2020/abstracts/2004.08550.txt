Title: Long memory in select stock returns using an alternative wavelet log-scale alignment approach
Authors: Avishek Bhandari, Bandi Kamaiah
ID: 2004.08550
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2004.08550

This study investigates the efficiency of some select stock markets. Using an
improved wavelet estimator of long range dependence, we show evidence of long
memory in the stock returns of some emerging Asian economies. However, developed
markets of Europe and the United States did not exhibit long memory thereby
confirming the efficiency of developed stock markets. On the other hand,
emerging Asian markets are found to be less efficient as long memory is more
pronounced in these markets.
