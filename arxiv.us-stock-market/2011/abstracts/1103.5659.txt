Title: U.S. Core Inflation: A Wavelet Analysis
Authors: kevin dowd, john cotter
ID: 1103.5659
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1103.5659

This paper proposes the use of wavelet methods to estimate U.S. core inflation.
It explains wavelet methods and suggests they are ideally suited to this task.
Comparisons are made with traditional CPI-based and regression-based measures
for their performance in following trend inflation and predicting future
inflation. Results suggest that wavelet-based measures perform better, and
sometimes much better, than the traditional approaches. These results suggest
that wavelet methods are a promising avenue for future research on core
inflation.
