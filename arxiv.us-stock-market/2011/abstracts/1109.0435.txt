Title: The string prediction models as an invariants of time series in forex market
Authors: Richard Pincak, Marian Repasan
ID: 1109.0435
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1109.0435

In this paper we apply a new approach of the string theory to the real financial
market. It is direct extension and application of the work [1] into prediction
of prices. The models are constructed with an idea of prediction models based on
the string invariants (PMBSI). The performance of PMBSI is compared to support
vector machines (SVM) and artificial neural networks (ANN) on an artificial and
a financial time series. Brief overview of the results and analysis is given.
The first model is based on the correlation function as invariant and the second
one is an application based on the deviations from the closed string/pattern
form (PMBCS). We found the difference between these two approaches. The first
model cannot predict the behavior of the forex market with good efficiency in
comparison with the second one which is, in addition, able to make relevant
profit per year.
