Title: Time series momentum and contrarian effects in the Chinese stock market
Authors: Huai-Long Shi, Wei-Xing Zhou
ID: 1702.07374
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1702.07374

This paper concentrates on the time series momentum or contrarian effects in the
Chinese stock market. We evaluate the performance of the time series momentum
strategy applied to major stock indices in mainland China and explore the
relation between the performance of time series momentum strategies and some
firm-specific characteristics. Our findings indicate that there is a time series
momentum effect in the short run and a contrarian effect in the long run in the
Chinese stock market. The performances of the time series momentum and
contrarian strategies are highly dependent on the look-back and holding periods
and firm-specific characteristics.
