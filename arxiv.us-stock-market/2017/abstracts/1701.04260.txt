Title: On VIX Futures in the rough Bergomi model
Authors: Antoine Jacquier, Claude Martini, Aitor Muguruza
ID: 1701.04260
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1701.04260

The rough Bergomi model introduced by Bayer, Friz and Gatheral has been
outperforming conventional Markovian stochastic volatility models by reproducing
implied volatility smiles in a very realistic manner, in particular for short
maturities. We investigate here the dynamics of the VIX and the forward variance
curve generated by this model, and develop efficient pricing algorithms for VIX
futures and options. We further analyse the validity of the rough Bergomi model
to jointly describe the VIX and the SPX, and present a joint calibration
algorithm based on the hybrid scheme by Bennedsen, Lunde and Pakkanen.
