Title: Measuring Communication Quality of Interest Rate Announcements
Authors: Jonathan Benchimol, Itamar Caspi, Sophia Kazinnik
ID: 2506.09868
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.09868

We use text-mining techniques to measure the accessibility and quality of
information within the texts of interest rate announcements published by the
Bank of Israel over the past decade. We find that comprehension of interest rate
announcements published by the Bank of Israel requires fewer years of education
than interest rate announcements published by the Federal Reserve and the
European Central Bank. In addition, we show that the sentiment within these
announcements is aligned with economic fluctuations. We also find that textual
uncertainty is correlated with the volatility of the domestic financial market.
