Title: Optimization Method of Multi-factor Investment Model Driven by Deep Learning for Risk Control
Authors: Ruisi Li, Xinhui Gu
ID: 2507.00332
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.00332

Propose a deep learning driven multi factor investment model optimization method
for risk control. By constructing a deep learning model based on Long Short Term
Memory (LSTM) and combining it with a multi factor investment model, we optimize
factor selection and weight determination to enhance the model's adaptability
and robustness to market changes. Empirical analysis shows that the LSTM model
is significantly superior to the benchmark model in risk control indicators such
as maximum retracement, Sharp ratio and value at risk (VaR), and shows strong
adaptability and robustness in different market environments. Furthermore, the
model is applied to the actual portfolio to optimize the asset allocation, which
significantly improves the performance of the portfolio, provides investors with
more scientific and accurate investment decision-making basis, and effectively
balances the benefits and risks.
