Title: Quasiconvex risk measures with markets volatility
Authors: Fei Sun, Yijun Hu
ID: 1806.08701
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1806.08701

Since the quasiconvex risk measures is a bigger class than the well known convex
risk measures, the study of quasiconvex risk measures makes sense especially in
the financial markets with volatility. In this paper, we will study the
quasiconvex risk measures defined on a special space $L^{p(\cdot)}$ where the
variable exponent $p(\cdot)$ is no longer a given real number like the space
$L^{p}$, but a random variable, which reflects the possible volatility of the
financial markets. The dual representation for this quasiconvex risk measures
will also provided.
