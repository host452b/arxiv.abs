Title: CAP and Monetary Policy
Authors: Carl Duisberg
ID: 1807.09475
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1807.09475

Despite the importance of CAP-related agricultural market regulation mechanisms
within Europe, the agricultural sectors in European countries retain a degree of
sensitivity to macroeconomic activity and policies. This reality now raises the
question of the effects to be expected from the implementation of the single
monetary policy on these agricultural sectors within the Monetary Union.
