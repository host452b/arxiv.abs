Title: Call option on the maximum of the interest rate in the one factor affine model
Authors: Mohamad Houda
ID: 1309.5565
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1309.5565

We determine an explicit formula for the Laplace transform of the price of an
option on a maximal interest rate when the instantaneous rate satisfies Cox-
Ingersoll-Ross's model. This generalizes considerably one result of Leblanc-
Scaillet.
