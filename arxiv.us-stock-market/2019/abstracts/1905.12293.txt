Title: Centered and non-centered variance inflation factor
Authors: Román Salmerón Gómez, Catalina García García y José García Pérez
ID: 1905.12293
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1905.12293

This paper analyzes the diagnostic of near multicollinearity in a multiple
linear regression from auxiliary centered regressions (with intercept) and non-
centered (without intercept). From these auxiliary regression, the centered and
non-centered Variance Inflation Factors are calculated, respectively. It is also
presented an expression that relate both of them.
