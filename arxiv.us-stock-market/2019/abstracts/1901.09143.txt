Title: A Study on Neural Network Architecture Applied to the Prediction of Brazilian Stock Returns
Authors: Leonardo Felizardo, Afonso Pinto
ID: 1901.09143
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1901.09143

In this paper we present a statistical analysis about the characteristics that
we intend to influence in the performance of the neural networks in terms of
assertiveness in the prediction of Brazilian stock returns. We created a
population of architectures for analysis and extracted the sample that had the
best assertive performance. It was verified how the characteristics of this
sample stand out and affect the neural networks. In addition, we make inferences
about what kind of influence the different architectures have on the performance
of neural networks. In the study, the prediction of the return of a Brazilian
stock traded on the stock exchange of São Paulo to measure the error committed
by the different architectures of constructed neural networks. The results are
promising and indicate that some aspects of the neural network architecture have
a significant impact on the assertiveness of the model.
