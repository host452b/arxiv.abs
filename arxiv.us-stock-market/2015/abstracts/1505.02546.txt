Title: Approximate hedging problem with transaction costs in stochastic volatility markets
Authors: Thai Huu Nguyen, Serguei Pergamenshchikov
ID: 1505.02546
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1505.02546

This paper studies the problem of option replication in general stochastic
volatility markets with transaction costs, using a new specification for the
volatility adjustment in Leland's algorithm \cite{Leland}. We prove several
limit theorems for the normalized replication error of Leland's strategy, as
well as that of the strategy suggested by Lépinette. The asymptotic results
obtained not only generalize the existing results, but also enable us to fix the
under-hedging property pointed out by Kabanov and Safarian. We also discuss
possible methods to improve the convergence rate and to reduce the option price
inclusive of transaction costs.
