Title: Time Series Analysis in American Stock Market Recovering in Post COVID-19 Pandemic Period
Authors: Weilin Fu, Zhuoran Li, Yupeng Zhang, Xingyou Zhou
ID: 2212.05369
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.05369

Every financial crisis has caused a dual shock to the global economy. The
shortage of market liquidity, such as default in debt and bonds, has led to the
spread of bankruptcies, such as Lehman Brothers in 2008. Using the data for the
ETFs of the S&P 500, Nasdaq 100, and Dow Jones Industrial Average collected from
Yahoo Finance, this study implemented Deep Learning, Neuro Network, and Time-
series to analyze the trend of the American Stock Market in the post-COVID-19
period. LSTM model in Neuro Network to predict the future trend, which suggests
the US stock market keeps falling for the post-COVID-19 period. This study
reveals a reasonable allocation method of Long Short-Term Memory for which there
is strong evidence.
