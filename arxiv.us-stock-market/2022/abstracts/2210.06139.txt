Title: Zero-Knowledge Optimal Monetary Policy under Stochastic Dominance
Authors: David Cerezo Sánchez
ID: 2210.06139
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.06139

Optimal simple rules for the monetary policy of the first stochastically
dominant crypto-currency are derived in a Dynamic Stochastic General Equilibrium
(DSGE) model, in order to provide optimal responses to changes in inflation,
output, and other sources of uncertainty. The optimal monetary policy
stochastically dominates all the previous crypto-currencies, thus the efficient
portfolio is to go long on the stochastically dominant crypto-currency: a
strategy-proof arbitrage featuring a higher Omega ratio with higher expected
returns, inducing an investment-efficient Nash equilibrium over the crypto-
market. Zero-knowledge proofs of the monetary policy are committed on the
blockchain: an implementation is provided.
