Title: Ensemble learning for portfolio valuation and risk management
Authors: Lotfi Boudabsa, Damir Filipović
ID: 2204.05926
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2204.05926

We introduce an ensemble learning method for dynamic portfolio valuation and
risk management building on regression trees. We learn the dynamic value process
of a derivative portfolio from a finite sample of its cumulative cash flow. The
estimator is given in closed form. The method is fast and accurate, and scales
well with sample size and path space dimension. The method can also be applied
to Bermudan style options. Numerical experiments show good results in moderate
dimension problems.
