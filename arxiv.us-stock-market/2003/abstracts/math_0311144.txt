Title: A model of the term structure of interest rates based on Lévy fields
Authors: Sergio Albeverio, Eugene Lytvynov, Andrea Mahnig
ID: math/0311144
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/math/0311144

An extension of the Heath--Jarrow--Morton model for the development of
instantaneous forward interest rates with deterministic coefficients and
Gaussian as well as Lévy field noise terms is given. In the special case where
the Lévy field is absent, one recovers a model discussed by D.P.~Kennedy.
