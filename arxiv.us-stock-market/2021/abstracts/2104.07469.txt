Title: A comparative study of Different Machine Learning Regressors For Stock Market Prediction
Authors: Nazish Ashfaq, Zubair Nawaz, Muhammad Ilyas
ID: 2104.07469
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2104.07469

For the development of successful share trading strategies, forecasting the
course of action of the stock market index is important. Effective prediction of
closing stock prices could guarantee investors attractive benefits. Machine
learning algorithms have the ability to process and forecast almost reliable
closing prices for historical stock patterns. In this article, we intensively
studied NASDAQ stock market and targeted to choose the portfolio of ten
different companies belongs to different sectors. The objective is to compute
opening price of next day stock using historical data. To fulfill this task nine
different Machine Learning regressor applied on this data and evaluated using
MSE and R2 as performance metric.
