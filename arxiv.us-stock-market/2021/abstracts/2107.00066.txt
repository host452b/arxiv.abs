Title: Market regime classification with signatures
Authors: Paul Bilokon, Antoine Jacquier, Conor McIndoe
ID: 2107.00066
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2107.00066

We provide a data-driven algorithm to classify market regimes for time series.
We utilise the path signature, encoding time series into easy-to-describe
objects, and provide a metric structure which establishes a connection between
separation of regimes and clustering of points.
