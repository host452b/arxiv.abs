Title: An Algorithmic Approach to Non-self-financing Hedging in a Discrete-Time Incomplete Market
Authors: N. Josephy, L. Kimball, A. Nagaev, M. Pasniewski, V. Steblovskaya
ID: math/0606471
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/math/0606471

We present an algorithm producing a dynamic non-self-financing hedging strategy
in an incomplete market corresponding to investor-relevant risk criterion. The
optimization is a two stage process that first determines admissible model
parameters that correspond to the market price of the option being hedged. The
second stage applies various merit functions to bootstrapped samples of model
residuals to choose an optimal set of model parameters from the admissible set.
Results are presented for options traded on the New York Stock Exchange.
