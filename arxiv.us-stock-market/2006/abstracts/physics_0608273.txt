Title: Waiting times between orders and trades in double-auction markets
Authors: Enrico Scalas, Taisei Kaizoji, Michael Kirchler, Juergen Huber, Alessandra Tedeschi
ID: physics/0608273
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/physics/0608273

In this paper, the survival function of waiting times between orders and the
corresponding trades in a double-auction market is studied both by means of
experiments and of empirical data. It turns out that, already at the level of
order durations, the survival function cannot be represented by a single
exponential, thus ruling out the hypothesis of constant activity during trading.
This fact has direct consequences for market microstructural models. They must
include such a non-exponential behaviour to be realistic.
