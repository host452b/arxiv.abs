Title: Characteristic time scales of tick quotes on foreign currency markets: an empirical study and agent-based model
Authors: Aki-Hiro Sato
ID: physics/0512163
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/physics/0512163

Power spectrum densities for the number of tick quotes per minute (market
activity) on three currency markets (USD/JPY, EUR/USD, and JPY/EUR) for periods
from January 1999 to December 2000 are analyzed. We find some peaks on the power
spectrum densities at a few minutes. We develop the double-threshold agent model
and confirm that stochastic resonance occurs for the market activity of this
model. We propose a hypothesis that the periodicities found on the power
spectrum densities can be observed due to stochastic resonance.
