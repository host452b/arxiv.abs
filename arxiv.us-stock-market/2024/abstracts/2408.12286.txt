Title: Momentum Informed Inflation-at-Risk
Authors: Tibor Szendrei, Arnab Bhattacharjee
ID: 2408.12286
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2408.12286

Growth-at-Risk has recently become a key measure of macroeconomic tail-risk,
which has seen it be researched extensively. Surprisingly, the same cannot be
said for Inflation-at-Risk where both tails, deflation and high inflation, are
of key concern to policymakers, which has seen comparatively much less research.
This paper will tackle this gap and provide estimates for Inflation-at-Risk. The
key insight of the paper is that inflation is best characterised by a
combination of two types of nonlinearities: quantile variation, and conditioning
on the momentum of inflation.
