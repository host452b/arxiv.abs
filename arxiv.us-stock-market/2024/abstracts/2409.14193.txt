Title: Interest rate derivatives in a CTMC setting: pricing, replication and Ross recovery
Authors: Tim Leung, Matthew Lorig
ID: 2409.14193
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.14193

We consider a financial market in which the short rate is modeled by a
continuous time Markov chain (CTMC) with a finite state space. In this setting,
we show how to price any financial derivative whose payoff is a function of the
state of the underlying CTMC at the maturity date. We also show how to replicate
such claims by trading only a money market account and zero-coupon bonds.
Finally, using an extension of Ross' Recovery Theorem due to Qin and Linetsky,
we deduce the real-world dynamics of the CTMC.
