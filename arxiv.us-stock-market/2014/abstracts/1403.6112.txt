Title: The role of the "Maximizing Output Growth Inflation Rate" in monetary policy
Authors: Dominique Pepin
ID: 1403.6112
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1403.6112

The paper discusses the role of monetary policy when potential output depends on
the inflation rate. If the intention of the central bank is to maximize actual
output growth, then it has to be credibly committed to a strict inflation
targeting rule, and to take the MOGIR (the Maximizing Output Growth Inflation
Rate) as the target.
