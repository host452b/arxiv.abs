Title: Inflation and speculation in a dynamic macroeconomic model
Authors: Matheus Grasselli, Adrien Nguyen Huu
ID: 1412.7500
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1412.7500

We study a monetary version of the Keen model by merging two alternative
extensions, namely the addition of a dynamic price level and the introduction of
speculation. We recall and study old and new equilibria, together with their
local stability analysis. This includes a state of recession associated with a
deflationary regime and characterized by falling employment but constant wage
shares, with  or without an accompanying debt crisis. We also emphasize some new
qualitative behavior of the extended model, in particular its ability to produce
and describe repeated financial crises as a natural pace of the economy, and its
suitability to describe the relationship between economic growth and financial
activities.
