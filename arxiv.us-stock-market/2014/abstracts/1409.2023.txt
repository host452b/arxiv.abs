Title: Optimal investment with bounded above utilities in discrete time markets
Authors: Miklos Rasonyi
ID: 1409.2023
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1409.2023

We consider an arbitrage-free, discrete time and frictionless market. We prove
that an investor maximising the expected utility of her terminal wealth can
always find an optimal investment strategy provided that her dissatisfaction of
infinite losses is infinite and her utility function is non-decreasing,
continuous and bounded above. The same result is shown for cumulative prospect
theory preferences, under additional assumptions.
