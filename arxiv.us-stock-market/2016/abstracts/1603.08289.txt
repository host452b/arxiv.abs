Title: Pricing variance swaps in a hybrid model of stochastic volatility and interest rate with regime-switching
Authors: Jiling Cao, Teh Raihana Nazirah Roslan, Wenjun Zhang
ID: 1603.08289
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1603.08289

In this paper, we consider the problem of pricing discretely-sampled variance
swaps based on a hybrid model of stochastic volatility and stochastic interest
rate with regime-switching. Our modelling framework extends the Heston
stochastic volatility model by including the CIR stochastic interest rate and
model parameters that switch according to a continuous-time observable Markov
chain process. A semi-closed form pricing formula for variance swaps is derived.
The pricing formula is assessed through numerical implementations, and the
impact of including regime-switching on pricing variance swaps is also
discussed.
