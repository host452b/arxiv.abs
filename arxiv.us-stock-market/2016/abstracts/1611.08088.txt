Title: Multiple Time Series Ising Model for Financial Market Simulations
Authors: Tetsuya Takaishi
ID: 1611.08088
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1611.08088

In this paper we propose an Ising model which simulates multiple financial time
series. Our model introduces the interaction which couples to spins of other
systems. Simulations from our model show that time series exhibit the volatility
clustering that is often observed in the real financial markets. Furthermore we
also find non-zero cross correlations between the volatilities from our model.
Thus our model can simulate stock markets where volatilities of stocks are
mutually correlated.
