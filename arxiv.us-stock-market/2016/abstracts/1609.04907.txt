Title: Asset Pricing in a Semi-Markov Modulated Market with Time-dependent Volatility
Authors: Tanmay S. Patankar
ID: 1609.04907
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1609.04907

This project attempts to address the problem of asset pricing in a financial
market, where the interest rates and volatilities exhibit regime switching. This
is an extension of the Black-Scholes model. Studies of Markov-modulated regime
switching models have been well-documented. This project extends that notion to
a class of semi-Markov processes known as age-dependent processes. We also allow
for time-dependence in volatility within regimes. We show that the problem of
option pricing in such a market is equivalent to solving a certain integral
equation.
