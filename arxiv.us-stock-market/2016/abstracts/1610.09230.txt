Title: Robust Utility Maximization in Discrete-Time Markets with Friction
Authors: Ariel Neufeld, Mario Sikic
ID: 1610.09230
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/1610.09230

We study a robust stochastic optimization problem in the quasi-sure setting in
discrete-time. We show that under a lineality-type condition the problem admits
a maximizer. This condition is implied by the no-arbitrage condition in models
of financial markets. As a corollary, we obtain existence of an utility
maximizer in the frictionless market model, markets with proportional
transaction costs and also more general convex costs, like in the case of market
impact.
