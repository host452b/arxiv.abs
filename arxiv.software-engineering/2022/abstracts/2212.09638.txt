Title: Co-designing for a Hybrid Workplace Experience in Software Development
Authors: Zhendong Wang, Yi-Hung Chou, Kayla Fathi, Tobias Schimmer, Peter Colligan et al.
ID: 2212.09638
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.09638

Abstract:
With increasing demands for flexible work models, many IT organizations have
adapted to hybrid work that promises enhanced team productivity as well as work
satisfaction. To achieve productive engineering practice, collaborative product
innovation, and effective mentorship in the ensuing hybrid work, we introduce a
workshop approach on co-designing for a hybrid workplace experience and provide
implications for continuously improving collaborative software development at
scale.
