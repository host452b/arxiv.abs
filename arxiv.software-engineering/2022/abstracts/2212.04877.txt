Title: Industry Best Practices in Robotics Software Engineering
Authors: Robert Bocchino, Arne Nordmann, Allison Thackston, Andreas Angerer, Federico Ciccozzi et al.
ID: 2212.04877
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.04877

Abstract:
Robotics software is pushing the limits of software engineering practice. The
3rd International Workshop on Robotics Software Engineering held a panel on "the
best practices for robotic software engineering". This article shares the key
takeaways that emerged from the discussion among the panelists and the workshop,
ranging from architecting practices at the NASA/Caltech Jet Propulsion
Laboratory, model-driven development at Bosch, development and testing of
autonomous driving systems at Waymo, and testing of robotics software at XITASO.
Researchers and practitioners can build on the contents of this paper to gain a
fresh perspective on their activities and focus on the most pressing practices
and challenges in developing robotics software today.
