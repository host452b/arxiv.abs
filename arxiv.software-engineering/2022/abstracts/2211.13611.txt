Title: Software Architecture and System Design of Rubin Observatory
Authors: William O'Mullane, Frossie Economou, Kian-Tat Lim, Fritz Mueller, Tim Jenness et al.
ID: 2211.13611
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2211.13611

Abstract:
Starting from a description of the Rubin Observatory Data Management System
Architecture, and drawing on our experience with and involvement in a range of
other projects including Gaia, SDSS, UKIRT, and JCMT, we derive a series of
generic design patterns and lessons learned.
