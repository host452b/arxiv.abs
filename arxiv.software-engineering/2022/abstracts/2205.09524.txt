Title: Security Analysis of DeFi: Vulnerabilities, Attacks and Advances
Authors: Wenkai Li, Jiuyang Bu, Xiaoqi Li, Xianyi Chen
ID: 2205.09524
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2205.09524

Abstract:
Decentralized finance (DeFi) in Ethereum is a financial ecosystem built on the
blockchain that has locked over 200 billion USD until April 2022. All
transaction information is transparent and open when transacting through the
DeFi protocol, which has led to a series of attacks. Several studies have
attempted to optimize it from both economic and technical perspectives. However,
few works analyze the vulnerabilities and optimizations of the entire DeFi
system. In this paper, we first systematically analyze vulnerabilities related
to DeFi in Ethereum at several levels, then we investigate real-world attacks.
Finally, we summarize the achievements of DeFi optimization and provide some
future directions.
