Title: Rapid Development of a Mobile Robot Simulation Environment
Authors: Gordon Stein, Chan-Jin Chung
ID: 2208.04820
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2208.04820

Abstract:
Robotics simulation provides many advantages during the development of an
intelligent ground vehicle (IGV) such as testing the software components in
varying scenarios without requiring a complete physical robot. This paper
discusses a 3D simulation environment created using rapid application
development and the Unity game engine to enable testing during a mobile robotics
competition. Our experience shows that the simulation environment contributed
greatly to the development of software for the competition. The simulator also
contributed to the hardware development of the robot.
