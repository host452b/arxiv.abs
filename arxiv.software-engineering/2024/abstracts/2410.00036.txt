Title: InsightPulse: An IoT-based System for User Experience Interview Analysis
Authors: Dian Lyu, Yuetong Lu, Jassie He, Murad Mehrab Abrar, Ruijun Xie et al.
ID: 2410.00036
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.00036

Abstract:
Conducting efficient and effective user experience (UX) interviews often poses
challenges, such as maintaining focus on key topics and managing the duration of
interviews and post-interview analyses. To address these issues, this paper
introduces InsightPulse, an Internet of Things (IoT)-based hardware and software
system designed to streamline and enhance the UX interview process through
speech analysis and Artificial Intelligence. InsightPulse provides real-time
support during user interviews by automatically identifying and highlighting key
discussion points, proactively suggesting follow-up questions, and generating
thematic summaries. These features enable more insightful discoveries and help
to manage interview duration effectively. Additionally, the system features a
robust backend analytics dashboard that simplifies the post-interview review
process, thus facilitating the quick extraction of actionable insights and
enhancing overall UX research efficiency.
