Title: Security of and by Generative AI platforms
Authors: Hari Hayagreevan, Souvik Khamaru
ID: 2410.13899
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.13899

Abstract:
This whitepaper highlights the dual importance of securing generative AI (genAI)
platforms and leveraging genAI for cybersecurity. As genAI technologies
proliferate, their misuse poses significant risks, including data breaches,
model tampering, and malicious content generation. Securing these platforms is
critical to protect sensitive data, ensure model integrity, and prevent
adversarial attacks. Simultaneously, genAI presents opportunities for enhancing
security by automating threat detection, vulnerability analysis, and incident
response. The whitepaper explores strategies for robust security frameworks
around genAI systems, while also showcasing how genAI can empower organizations
to anticipate, detect, and mitigate sophisticated cyber threats.
