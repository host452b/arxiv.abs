Title: Mobile user experience from the lens of project-based learning
Authors: Maria Spichkova
ID: 2404.02470
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.02470

Abstract:
This paper presents an overview of mobile application projects conducted at the
RMIT University as a part of the Learning and Teaching activities within
Bachelor and Master programs, in collaboration with industrial partners. We
discuss the lessons learned over eight years of teaching the corresponding
courses and compare the results of our student project to the trends summarised
in the recently published approached from other universities and countries.
