Title: Verificarlo CI: continuous integration for numerical optimization and debugging
Authors: Aurélien Delval, François Coppens, Eric Petit, Roman Iakymchuk, Pablo de Oliveira Castro
ID: 2407.08262
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.08262

Abstract:
Floating-point accuracy is an important concern when developing numerical
simulations or other compute-intensive codes. Tracking the introduction of
numerical regression is often delayed until it provokes unexpected bug for the
end-user. In this paper, we introduce Verificarlo CI, a continuous integration
workflow for the numerical optimization and debugging of a code over the course
of its development. We demonstrate applicability of Verificarlo CI on two
test-case applications.
