Title: A summary of the routing algorithm and their optimization,performance
Authors: Xunchi Ma
ID: 2402.15749
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2402.15749

Abstract:
This essay provides a comprehensive analysis of the optimization and performance
evaluation of various routing algorithms within the context of computer
networks. Routing algorithms are critical for determining the most efficient
path for data transmission between nodes in a network. The efficiency,
reliability, and scalability of a network heavily rely on the choice and
optimization of its routing algorithm. This paper begins with an overview of
fundamental routing strategies, including shortest path, flooding, distance
vector, and link state algorithms, and extends to more sophisticated techniques.
