Title: Balancing Innovation and Ethics in AI-Driven Software Development
Authors: Mohammad Baqar
ID: 2408.10252
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2408.10252

Abstract:
This paper critically examines the ethical implications of integrating AI tools
like GitHub Copilot and ChatGPT into the software development process. It
explores issues such as code ownership, bias, accountability, privacy, and the
potential impact on the job market. While these AI tools offer significant
benefits in terms of productivity and efficiency, they also introduce complex
ethical challenges. The paper argues that addressing these challenges is
essential to ensuring that AI's integration into software development is both
responsible and beneficial to society
