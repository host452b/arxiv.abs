Title: Evaluation of Network Based IDS and Deployment of multi-sensor IDS
Authors: Navya Iyengar
ID: 2007.11654
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2007.11654

Abstract:
Cloud-based and network-based technology has witnessed an exponential rise in
development. Adaptation of these latest technologies has opened flood gates for
data breaches, an increase in sophistication of cyber threats, and, a multitude
of new attack vectors. Numerous tools and solutions are currently available for
detection of these threats. Network-based Intrusion Detection Systems is one of
the most effective tools implemented to maintain confidentiality, integrity,
and, availability of networks. While there are several open source tools in the
offing, this paper evaluates two open-source NIDS Snort and Suricata, along with
strategic placement of multi sensor IDS in a WAN environment, in combination
with NIDS, for in time threat detection and protection of systems.
