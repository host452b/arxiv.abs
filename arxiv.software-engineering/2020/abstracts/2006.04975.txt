Title: Architectural Blueprints: The 4+1 View Model of Software Architecture
Authors: Philippe Kruchten
ID: 2006.04975
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.04975

Abstract:
This article presents a model for describing the architecture of
software-intensive systems, based on the use of multiple, concurrent views. This
use of multiple views allows to address separately the concerns of the various
stakeholders of the architecture: end-user, developers, systems engineers,
project managers, etc., and to handle separately the functional and non
functional requirements. Each of the five views is described, together with a
notation to capture it. The views are designed using an architecture-centered,
scenario-driven, iterative development process.
