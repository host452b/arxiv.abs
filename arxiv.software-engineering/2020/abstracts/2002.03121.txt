Title: PP-ind: Description of a Repository of Industrial Pair Programming Research Data
Authors: Franz Zieris, Lutz Prechelt
ID: 2002.03121
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2002.03121

Abstract:
PP-ind is a repository of audio-video-recordings of industrial pair programming
sessions. Since 2007, our research group has collected data in 13 companies. A
total of 57 developers worked together (mostly in groups of two, but also three
or four) in 67 sessions with a mean length of 1:35 hours. In this report, we
describe how we collected the data and provide summaries and characterizations
of the sessions.
