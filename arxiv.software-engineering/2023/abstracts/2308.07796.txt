Title: Research Software Engineering in 2030
Authors: Daniel S. Katz, Simon Hettrick
ID: 2308.07796
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2308.07796

Abstract:
This position paper for an invited talk on the "Future of eScience" discusses
the Research Software Engineering Movement and where it might be in 2030.
Because of the authors' experiences, it is aimed globally but with examples that
focus on the United States and United Kingdom.
