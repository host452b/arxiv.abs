Title: Securing Bystander Privacy in Mixed Reality While Protecting the User Experience
Authors: Matthew Corbett, Brendan David-John, Jiacheng Shang, Y. Charlie Hu, Bo Ji
ID: 2307.12847
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.12847

Abstract:
The modern Mixed Reality devices that make the Metaverse viable require vast
information about the physical world and can also violate the privacy of
unsuspecting or unwilling bystanders in their vicinity. In this article, we
provide an introduction to the problem, existing solutions, and avenues for
future research.
