Title: AI Product Security: A Primer for Developers
Authors: Ebenezer R. H. P. Isaac, Jim Reno
ID: 2304.11087
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2304.11087

Abstract:
Not too long ago, AI security used to mean the research and practice of how AI
can empower cybersecurity, that is, AI for security. Ever since Ian Goodfellow
and his team popularized adversarial attacks on machine learning, security for
AI became an important concern and also part of AI security. It is imperative to
understand the threats to machine learning products and avoid common pitfalls in
AI product development. This article is addressed to developers, designers,
managers and researchers of AI software products.
