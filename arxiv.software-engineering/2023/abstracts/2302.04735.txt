Title: A Multi-Layer Software Architecture for Aerial Cognitive Multi-Robot Systems in Power Line Inspection Tasks
Authors: Giuseppe Silano, Jan Bednar, Tiago Nascimento, Jesus Capitan, Martin Saska et al.
ID: 2302.04735
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.04735

Abstract:
This paper presents a multi-layer software architecture to perform cooperative
missions with a fleet of quadrotors providing support in electrical power line
inspection operations. The proposed software framework guarantees the compliance
with safety requirements between drones and human workers while ensuring that
the mission is carried out successfully. Besides, cognitive capabilities are
integrated in the multi-vehicle system in order to reply to unforeseen events
and external disturbances. The feasibility and effectiveness of the proposed
architecture are demonstrated by means of realistic simulations.
