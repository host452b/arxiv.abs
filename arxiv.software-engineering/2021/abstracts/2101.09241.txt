Title: A Survey of Requirements for COVID-19 Mitigation Strategies. Part II: Elicitation of Requirements
Authors: Wojciech Jamroga
ID: 2101.09241
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2101.09241

Abstract:
The COVID-19 pandemic has influenced virtually all aspects of our lives. Across
the world, countries have applied various mitigation strategies, based on
social, political, and technological instruments. We postulate that multi-agent
systems can provide a common platform to study (and balance) their essential
properties. We also show how to obtain a comprehensive list of the properties by
"distilling" them from media snippets. Finally, we present a preliminary take on
their formal specification, using ideas from multi-agent logics.
