Title: Developing and experimenting with LEO satellite constellations in OMNeT++
Authors: Aiden Valentine, George Parisis
ID: 2109.12046
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2109.12046

Abstract:
In this paper, we present our work in designing and implementing a LEO satellite
constellation simulation model1 within OMNeT++ and INET, which is validated by
comparing the results with existing work. Our model builds upon the fundamentals
of the Open Source Satellite Simulator (OS$^3$), which was ported to INET 4.3.
We describe how the model was integrated on top of the INET and ported OS$^3$
Framework. We then experiment with the simulation model to demonstrate its
viability in simulating LEO satellite constellations. This involved simulating
both outdated and more recent satellite constellations, using FCC filing
information, to validate latency results.
