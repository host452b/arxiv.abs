Title: An ELEGANT dataset with Denial of Service and Man in The Middle attacks
Authors: Bruno Sousa, Tiago Cruz, Miguel Arieiro, Vasco Pereira
ID: 2103.09380
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2103.09380

Abstract:
This document describes a dataset with diverse types of Denial of Service (DoS)
attacks and Man-in-the-Middle (MiTM) attacks. The data is available at the
following DOI 10.21227/mewp-g646. This document describes the data collection
process and provides useful information on how data can be employed to devise
models for cybersecurity in critical infrastructures using Programmable Logic
Controllers (PLCs)
