Title: Towards developing a realistic robotics simulation environment of an indoor vegetable greenhouse
Authors: Brent Van De Walker, Brendan Byrne, Joshua Near, Blake Purdie, Matthew Whatman et al.
ID: 2107.14348
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2107.14348

Abstract:
This article presents a method for developing a realistic robotics simulation
environment for application in vegetable greenhouses. The method pipeline starts
with the construction of a 3D cloud images of the greenhouse rows. This data is
then used to develop a robotics simulation environment using the CoppeliaSim
simulation software. The method has been tested using images from a commercial
greenhouse.
