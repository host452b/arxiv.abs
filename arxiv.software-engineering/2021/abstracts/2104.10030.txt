Title: Reproducibility Report for the Paper: QN-based Modeling and Analysis of Software Performance Antipatterns for Cyber-Physical Systems
Authors: Alessandro Pellegrini
ID: 2104.10030
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2104.10030

Abstract:
The authors have uploaded their artifact to Zenodo, which ensures a long-term
retention of the artifact. The artifact allows to re-run the experiments very
smoothly, and the dependencies are well documented. The process to regenerate
data for the figures and tables in the paper completes, and all results are
reproducible. This paper can thus receive the Artifacts Available badge. The
software in the artifact runs correctly with no trouble, and is relevant to the
paper, thus deserving the Artifacts Evaluated -- Functional badge. Given the
successful reproduction of all figures and tables, the Results Reproduced badge
can be assigned.
