Title: $\{log\}$: Applications to Software Specification, Prototyping and Verification
Authors: Maximiliano Cristiá, Gianfranco Rossi
ID: 2103.14933
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2103.14933

Abstract:
This document shows how Z specifications can be translated into $\{log\}$ and,
later, on how $\{log\}$ can be used to run simulations and automated proofs.
This can help users of other specification languages such as B and VDM to use
$\{log\}$ along the same lines. The presentation is rather informal and
user-oriented. More technical and formal presentations can be found in the
papers published by the authors. We also assume the reader has at least a basic
knowledge of the Z notation.
