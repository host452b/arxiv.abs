Title: Jolie & LEMMA: Model-Driven Engineering and Programming Languages Meet on Microservices
Authors: Saverio Giallorenzo, Fabrizio Montesi, Marco Peressotti, Florian Rademacher, Sabine Sachweh
ID: 2104.02458
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2104.02458

Abstract:
In the field of microservices, Model-Driven Engineering has emerged as a
powerful methodology for architectural design, and new programming languages
have introduced language abstractions to deal with microservice development more
effectively. In this article, we present the first preliminary investigation of
how the two approaches can be married, taking the LEMMA framework and the Jolie
programming language as respective representatives. By developing a conceptual
metamodel for Jolie, we elicit a strong link between the two approaches, which
shows that there is much to gain. We discuss a few low-hanging fruits that come
from our finding and present some interesting future directions that arise from
our new viewpoint.
