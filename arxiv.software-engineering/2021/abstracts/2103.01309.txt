Title: Investigating the potential impact of values on requirements and software engineering
Authors: Alistair Sutcliffe, Pete Sawyer, Wei Liu, Nelly Bencomo
ID: 2103.01309
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2103.01309

Abstract:
This paper describes an investigation into value-based software engineering and
proposes a comprehensive value taxonomy with an interpretation of design feature
implications. The value taxonomy is used to assess the design of Covid19 symptom
tracker applications.
