Title: CyberSecurity Challenges for Software Developer Awareness Training in Industrial Environments
Authors: Tiago Espinha Gasiba, Ulrike Lechner, Maria Pinto-Albuquerque
ID: 2102.05345
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2102.05345

Abstract:
Awareness of cybersecurity topics facilitates software developers to produce
secure code. This awareness is especially important in industrial environments
for the products and services in critical infrastructures. In this work, we
address how to raise awareness of software developers on the topic of secure
coding. We propose the "CyberSecurity Challenges", a serious game designed to be
used in an industrial environment and address software developers' needs. Our
work distils the experience gained in conducting these CyberSecurity Challenges
in an industrial setting. The main contributions are the design of the
CyberSecurity Challenges events, the analysis of the perceived benefits, and
practical advice for practitioners who wish to design or refine these games.
