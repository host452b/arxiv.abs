Title: Proceedings of CHIdeology 2026: CHI Workshop on Disentangling the fragmented politics, values and imaginaries of Human-Computer Interaction through ideologies
Authors: Felix Anand Epp, Matti Nelimarkka, Jesse Haapoja, Pedro Ferreira, Os Keyes et al.
ID: 2603.10681
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10681

Abstract:
This is the Proceedings of the First CHI Workshop on CHIdeology: Disentangling
the fragmented politics, values, and imaginaries of Human-Computer Interaction
through ideologies, held on Wednesday, 15 April, in Barcelona, Spain, at the ACM
CHI Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems.
