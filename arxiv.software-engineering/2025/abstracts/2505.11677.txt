Title: Enhancing Code Quality with Generative AI: Boosting Developer Warning Compliance
Authors: Hansen Chang, Christian DeLozier
ID: 2505.11677
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.11677

Abstract:
Programmers have long ignored warnings, especially those generated by static
analysis tools, due to the potential for false-positives. In some cases,
warnings may be indicative of larger issues, but programmers may not understand
how a seemingly unimportant warning can grow into a vulnerability. Because these
messages tend to be long and confusing, programmers tend to ignore them if they
do not cause readily identifiable issues. Large language models can simplify
these warnings, explain the gravity of important warnings, and suggest potential
fixes to increase developer compliance with fixing warnings.
