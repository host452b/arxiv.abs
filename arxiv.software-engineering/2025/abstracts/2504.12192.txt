Title: From Requirements to Architecture: Semi-Automatically Generating Software Architectures
Authors: Tobias Eisenreich
ID: 2504.12192
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.12192

Abstract:
To support junior and senior architects, I propose developing a new architecture
creation method that leverages LLMs' evolving capabilities to support the
architect. This method involves the architect's close collaboration with
LLM-fueled tooling over the whole process. The architect is guided through
Domain Model creation, Use Case specification, architectural decisions, and
architecture evaluation. While the architect can take complete control of the
process and the results, and use the tooling as a building set, they can follow
the intended process for maximum tooling support. The preliminary results
suggest the feasibility of this process and indicate major time savings for the
architect.
