Title: Communication in Agile Software Development -- A Mapping Study
Authors: Suddhasvatta Das, Kevin Gary
ID: 2504.20186
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.20186

Abstract:
Software industry is a fast-moving industry and to keep up with this pace the
development process also needs to be fast and efficient and Agile software
development (ASD) is the answer to this problem. Even though ASD has been in
there for over two decades there are still multiple unknown questions tied to
ASD that need to be addressed. In this study we are going to address one of the
most critical factors of ASD i.e. Communication. We conducted a review of 14
studies and found the areas under ASD communication that the community is
interested in as well as research gaps.
