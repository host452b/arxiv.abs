Title: Towards Human-AI Mutual Learning: A New Research Paradigm
Authors: Xiaomei Wang, Xiaoyu Chen
ID: 2405.04687
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.04687

Abstract:
This paper describes a new research paradigm for studying human-AI
collaboration, named "human-AI mutual learning", defined as the process where
humans and AI agents preserve, exchange, and improve knowledge during human-AI
collaboration. We describe relevant methodologies, motivations, domain examples,
benefits, challenges, and future research agenda under this paradigm.
