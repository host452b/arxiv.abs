Title: Frame Quantization of Neural Networks
Authors: Wojciech Czaja, Sanghoon Na
ID: 2404.08131
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.08131

We present a post-training quantization algorithm with error estimates relying
on ideas originating from frame theory. Specifically, we use first-order Sigma-
Delta ($ΣΔ$) quantization for finite unit-norm tight frames to quantize weight
matrices and biases in a neural network. In our scenario, we derive an error
bound between the original neural network and the quantized neural network in
terms of step size and the number of frame elements. We also demonstrate how to
leverage the redundancy of frames to achieve a quantized neural network with
higher accuracy.
