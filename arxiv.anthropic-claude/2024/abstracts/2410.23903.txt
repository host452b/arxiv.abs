Title: Neural Network Verification with PyRAT
Authors: Augustin Lemesle, Julien Lehmann, Tristan Le Gall
ID: 2410.23903
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.23903

As AI systems are becoming more and more popular and used in various critical
domains (health, transport, energy, ...), the need to provide guarantees and
trust of their safety is undeniable. To this end, we present PyRAT, a tool based
on abstract interpretation to verify the safety and the robustness of neural
networks. In this paper, we describe the different abstractions used by PyRAT to
find the reachable states of a neural network starting from its input as well as
the main features of the tool to provide fast and accurate analysis of neural
networks. PyRAT has already been used in several collaborations to ensure safety
guarantees, with its second place at the VNN-Comp 2024 showcasing its
performance.
