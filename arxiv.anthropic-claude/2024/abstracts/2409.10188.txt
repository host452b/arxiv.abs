Title: Enhancing RL Safety with Counterfactual LLM Reasoning
Authors: Dennis Gross, Helge Spieker
ID: 2409.10188
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.10188

Reinforcement learning (RL) policies may exhibit unsafe behavior and are hard to
explain. We use counterfactual large language model reasoning to enhance RL
policy safety post-training. We show that our approach improves and helps to
explain the RL policy safety.
