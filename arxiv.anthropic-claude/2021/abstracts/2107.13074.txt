Title: Toward AI Assistants That Let Designers Design
Authors: Sebastiaan De Peuter, Antti Oulasvirta, Samuel Kaski
ID: 2107.13074
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2107.13074

Abstract:
AI for supporting designers needs to be rethought. It should aim to cooperate,
not automate, by supporting and leveraging the creativity and problem-solving of
designers. The challenge for such AI is how to infer designers' goals and then
help them without being needlessly disruptive. We present AI-assisted design: a
framework for creating such AI, built around generative user models which enable
reasoning about designers' goals, reasoning, and capabilities.
