Title: Spoiler in a Textstack: How Much Can Transformers Help?
Authors: Anna Wróblewska, Paweł Rzepiński, Sylwia Sysko-Romańczuk
ID: 2112.12913
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2112.12913

Abstract:
This paper presents our research regarding spoiler detection in reviews. In this
use case, we describe the method of fine-tuning and organizing the available
text-based model tasks with the latest deep learning achievements and techniques
to interpret the models' results. Until now, spoiler research has been rarely
described in the literature. We tested the transfer learning approach and
different latest transformer architectures on two open datasets with annotated
spoilers (ROC AUC above 81\% on TV Tropes Movies dataset, and Goodreads dataset
above 88\%). We also collected data and assembled a new dataset with
fine-grained annotations. To that end, we employed interpretability techniques
and measures to assess the models' reliability and explain their results.
