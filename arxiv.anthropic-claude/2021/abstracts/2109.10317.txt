Title: Introduction to Neural Network Verification
Authors: Aws Albarghouthi
ID: 2109.10317
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2109.10317

Deep learning has transformed the way we think of software and what it can do.
But deep neural networks are fragile and their behaviors are often surprising.
In many settings, we need to provide formal guarantees on the safety, security,
correctness, or robustness of neural networks. This book covers foundational
ideas from formal verification and their adaptation to reasoning about neural
networks and deep learning.
