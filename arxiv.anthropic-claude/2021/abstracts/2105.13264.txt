Title: How saccadic vision might help with theinterpretability of deep networks
Authors: Iana Sereda, Grigory Osipov
ID: 2105.13264
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2105.13264

Abstract:
We describe how some problems (interpretability,lack of object-orientedness) of
modern deep networks potentiallycould be solved by adapting a biologically
plausible saccadicmechanism of perception. A sketch of such a saccadic
visionmodel is proposed. Proof of concept experimental results areprovided to
support the proposed approach.
