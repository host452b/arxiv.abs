Title: Notes on a Path to AI Assistance in Mathematical Reasoning
Authors: Alex Kontorovich
ID: 2310.02896
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.02896

Abstract:
These informal notes are based on the author's lecture at the National Academies
of Science, Engineering, and Mathematics workshop on "AI to Assist Mathematical
Reasoning" in June 2023. The goal is to think through a path by which we might
arrive at AI that is useful for the research mathematician.
