Title: ARDIAS: AI-Enhanced Research Management, Discovery, and Advisory System
Authors: Debayan Banerjee, Seid Muhie Yimam, Sushil Awale, Chris Biemann
ID: 2301.10577
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2301.10577

Abstract:
In this work, we present ARDIAS, a web-based application that aims to provide
researchers with a full suite of discovery and collaboration tools. ARDIAS
currently allows searching for authors and articles by name and gaining insights
into the research topics of a particular researcher. With the aid of AI-based
tools, ARDIAS aims to recommend potential collaborators and topics to
researchers. In the near future, we aim to add tools that allow researchers to
communicate with each other and start new projects.
