Title: Toward best research practices in AI Psychology
Authors: Anna A. Ivanova
ID: 2312.01276
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.01276

Abstract:
Language models have become an essential part of the burgeoning field of AI
Psychology. I discuss 14 methodological considerations that can help design more
robust, generalizable studies evaluating the cognitive abilities of
language-based AI systems, as well as to accurately interpret the results of
these studies.
