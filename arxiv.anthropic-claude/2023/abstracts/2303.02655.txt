Title: On Modifying a Neural Network's Perception
Authors: Manuel de Sousa Ribeiro, João Leite
ID: 2303.02655
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2303.02655

Artificial neural networks have proven to be extremely useful models that have
allowed for multiple recent breakthroughs in the field of Artificial
Intelligence and many others. However, they are typically regarded as black
boxes, given how difficult it is for humans to interpret how these models reach
their results. In this work, we propose a method which allows one to modify what
an artificial neural network is perceiving regarding specific human-defined
concepts, enabling the generation of hypothetical scenarios that could help
understand and even debug the neural network model. Through empirical
evaluation, in a synthetic dataset and in the ImageNet dataset, we test the
proposed method on different models, assessing whether the performed
manipulations are well interpreted by the models, and analyzing how they react
to them.
