Title: The inverse problem for neural networks
Authors: Marcelo Forets, Christian Schilling
ID: 2308.14093
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2308.14093

We study the problem of computing the preimage of a set under a neural network
with piecewise-affine activation functions. We recall an old result that the
preimage of a polyhedral set is again a union of polyhedral sets and can be
effectively computed. We show several applications of computing the preimage for
analysis and interpretability of neural networks.
