Title: AI Safety Subproblems for Software Engineering Researchers
Authors: David Gros, Prem Devanbu, Zhou Yu
ID: 2304.14597
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2304.14597

Abstract:
In this 4-page manuscript we discuss the problem of long-term AI Safety from a
Software Engineering (SE) research viewpoint. We briefly summarize long-term AI
Safety, and the challenge of avoiding harms from AI as systems meet or exceed
human capabilities, including software engineering capabilities (and approach
AGI / "HLMI"). We perform a quantified literature review suggesting that AI
Safety discussions are not common at SE venues. We make conjectures about how
software might change with rising capabilities, and categorize "subproblems"
which fit into traditional SE areas, proposing how work on similar problems
might improve the future of AI and SE.
