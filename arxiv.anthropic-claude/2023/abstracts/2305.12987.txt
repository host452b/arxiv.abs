Title: GPT-SW3: An Autoregressive Language Model for the Nordic Languages
Authors: Ariel Ekgren, Amaru Cuba Gyllensten, Felix Stollenwerk, Joey Öhman, Tim Isbister et al. (+5)
ID: 2305.12987
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.12987

This paper details the process of developing the first native large generative
language model for the Nordic languages, GPT-SW3. We cover all parts of the
development process, from data collection and processing, training configuration
and instruction finetuning, to evaluation and considerations for release
strategies. We hope that this paper can serve as a guide and reference for other
researchers that undertake the development of large generative models for
smaller languages.
