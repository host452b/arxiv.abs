Title: On the Kolmogorov neural networks
Authors: Aysu Ismayilova, Vugar Ismailov
ID: 2311.00049
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.00049

In this paper, we show that the Kolmogorov two hidden layer neural network model
with a continuous, discontinuous bounded or unbounded activation function in the
second hidden layer can precisely represent continuous, discontinuous bounded
and all unbounded multivariate functions, respectively.
