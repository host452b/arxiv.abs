Title: Time-Continuous Modeling for Temporal Affective Pattern Recognition in LLMs
Authors: Rezky Kam, Coddy N. Siswanto
ID: 2601.12341
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.12341

This paper introduces a dataset and conceptual framework for LLMs to mimic real
world emotional dynamics through time and in-context learning leveraging
physics-informed neural network, opening a possibility for interpretable
dialogue modeling.
