Title: Graph neural networks and MSO
Authors: Veeti Ahvonen, Damian Heiman, Antti Kuusisto
ID: 2505.07816
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.07816

We give an alternative proof for the existing result that recurrent graph neural
networks working with reals have the same expressive power in restriction to
monadic second-order logic MSO as the graded modal substitution calculus. The
proof is based on constructing distributed automata that capture all MSO-
definable node properties over trees. We also consider some variants of the
acceptance conditions.
