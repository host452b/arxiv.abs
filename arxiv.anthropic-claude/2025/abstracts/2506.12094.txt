Title: Military AI Cyber Agents (MAICAs) Constitute a Global Threat to Critical Infrastructure
Authors: Timothy Dubber, Seth Lazar
ID: 2506.12094
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.12094

Abstract:
This paper argues that autonomous AI cyber-weapons - Military-AI Cyber Agents
(MAICAs) - create a credible pathway to catastrophic risk. It sets out the
technical feasibility of MAICAs, explains why geopolitics and the nature of
cyberspace make MAICAs a catastrophic risk, and proposes political, defensive-AI
and analogue-resilience measures to blunt the threat.
