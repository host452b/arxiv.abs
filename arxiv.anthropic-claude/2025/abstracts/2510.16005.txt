Title: Breaking Guardrails, Facing Walls: Insights on Adversarial AI for Defenders & Researchers
Authors: Giacomo Bertollo, Naz Bodemir, Jonah Burgess
ID: 2510.16005
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.16005

Abstract:
Analyzing 500 CTF participants, this paper shows that while participants readily
bypassed simple AI guardrails using common techniques, layered multi-step
defenses still posed significant challenges, offering concrete insights for
building safer AI systems.
