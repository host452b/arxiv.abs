Title: The Alberta Plan for AI Research
Authors: Richard S. Sutton, Michael Bowling, Patrick M. Pilarski
ID: 2208.11173
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2208.11173

Abstract:
Herein we describe our approach to artificial intelligence research, which we
call the Alberta Plan. The Alberta Plan is pursued within our research groups in
Alberta and by others who are like minded throughout the world. We welcome all
who would join us in this pursuit.
