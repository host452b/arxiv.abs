Title: Multiresolution Neural Networks for Imaging
Authors: Hallison Paz, Tiago Novello, Vinicius Silva, Luiz Schirmer, Guilherme Schardong et al. (+3)
ID: 2208.11813
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2208.11813

We present MR-Net, a general architecture for multiresolution neural networks,
and a framework for imaging applications based on this architecture. Our
coordinate-based networks are continuous both in space and in scale as they are
composed of multiple stages that progressively add finer details. Besides that,
they are a compact and efficient representation. We show examples of
multiresolution image representation and applications to texturemagnification,
minification, and antialiasing. This document is the extended version of the
paper [PNS+22]. It includes additional material that would not fit the page
limitations of the conference track for publication.
