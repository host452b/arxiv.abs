Title: Evaluating the Text-to-SQL Capabilities of Large Language Models
Authors: Nitarshan Rajkumar, Raymond Li, Dzmitry Bahdanau
ID: 2204.00498
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2204.00498

Abstract:
We perform an empirical evaluation of Text-to-SQL capabilities of the Codex
language model. We find that, without any finetuning, Codex is a strong baseline
on the Spider benchmark; we also analyze the failure modes of Codex in this
setting. Furthermore, we demonstrate on the GeoQuery and Scholar benchmarks that
a small number of in-domain examples provided in the prompt enables Codex to
perform better than state-of-the-art models finetuned on such few-shot examples.
