Title: A Neural ODE Interpretation of Transformer Layers
Authors: Yaofeng Desmond Zhong, Tongtao Zhang, Amit Chakraborty, Biswadip Dey
ID: 2212.06011
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.06011

Transformer layers, which use an alternating pattern of multi-head attention and
multi-layer perceptron (MLP) layers, provide an effective tool for a variety of
machine learning problems. As the transformer layers use residual connections to
avoid the problem of vanishing gradients, they can be viewed as the numerical
integration of a differential equation. In this extended abstract, we build upon
this connection and propose a modification of the internal architecture of a
transformer layer. The proposed model places the multi-head attention sublayer
and the MLP sublayer parallel to each other. Our experiments show that this
simple modification improves the performance of transformer networks in multiple
tasks. Moreover, for the image classification task, we show that using neural
ODE solvers with a sophisticated integration scheme further improves
performance.
